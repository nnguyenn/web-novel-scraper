“I’m Jenna Shirai! From Townia… huh?”

Jenna furrowed her brows.

Her dark eyes blinked endlessly.

“You look… bizarre.”

Jenna smiled awkwardly.

The first-born 5-star hero summoned in Townia, Wiryung, looked at me sitting on the bench.

Black hair and black eyes.

She was wearing a wide and fluttering martial artist’s robe,

And a scabbard with tassels hung at her waist.

“Excuse me, where are you from…”

“Jenna.”

I interrupted.

“She’s not from Townia.”

You can tell at a glance.

From hair color to facial features, body type to attire, everything was different.

“…This place.”

The woman opened her mouth.

It was a voice with no emotion.

“This place is called Townia.”

Wiryung looked at the floor with hollow eyes.

[‘Wiryung (★★★★★)’ has fallen into despondency!]

[Tips / Heroes in ‘despondency’ cannot be deployed on missions. Console and cheer them up to relieve despondency!]

I held my forehead.

Of course. The head office wouldn’t easily release a 5-star.

It seems we’ve been thoroughly played.

“Jenna, go upstairs first. I’ll talk to her.”

“Shouldn’t I stay?”

“It’s okay.”

“Fine. See you later, sis.”

Jenna glanced at Wiryung, who was standing still, then climbed the stairs to the upper floor.

“How long are you going to stay dazed?”

“I… couldn’t protect…”

She mumbled to herself as if she had gone mad.

“What a nuisance.”

I grabbed Wiryung’s wrist roughly.

If I remember correctly, there was an empty room in the left corner of the first floor.

When I grabbed her wrist and led her, she staggered and followed me.

Clunk.

After entering the room, I locked the door and sat Wiryung on the steel chair in the middle of the room.

Then I sat on the chair opposite and faced Wiryung.

“Hey.”

“…”

“Aren’t you going to answer?”

“It wasn’t a dream…”

I grabbed Wiryung’s chin and lifted it towards me.

“Answer my question. If you don’t want to die.”

“…You.”

“You, what.”

“Are you the lord of this place?”

“Lord?”

What kind of word is that?

After thinking for a moment, I recalled that ‘lord’ was similar to ‘sub-master.’

“Maybe.”

“I see.”

Wiryung gave a bitter smile.

And then she fell silent again.

‘This is infuriating.’

I looked ahead with my legs crossed.

Wiryung was still lowering her head.

‘Unique attire. Strange words. Peculiar accent.’

I could understand her words, but her tone was subtly different.

It felt like a foreigner speaking Korean.

‘An East Asian out of nowhere, huh?’

I scratched my head.

The worlds belonging to Mobius were almost Western in style.

Each world had knights, warriors, archers, rogues, mages, and elementalists,

With medieval castles, monsters, dragons, and magic.

‘Status window.’

[Nan Wiryung (★★★★★) Lv.1 (Exp 0/10)]

[Class: Weaponmaster]

[Strength: 36/36]

[Intelligence: 10/10]

[Health: 38/38]

[Agility: 41/41]

[Possessed Skills: Advanced Swordsmanship (Lv.1), Clear Mind (Lv.1), Heart Eyes (Lv.1), Sword Unity (Lv.1)]

[Engraving Slots: 2/2]

[1: Advanced Qi Manipulation (B+, Lv.1)]

[A special technique using a unique organ given only to those of pure blood from a noble family. Can use higher-grade Qi Manipulation.]

[Effect: Strength, Health, Agility +20%, Magic Resistance +15%]

[Unique Skill: Qi]

[Note: Due to the ‘Oath of the Sworn,’ the abilities of the engravings are halved in other places.]

[2: Flying Swallow Sword (A-, Lv.1)]

[Once swung, it flies like a swallow. Specialized in spatial suppression swordsmanship.]

[Unique Skill: Flying Swllow]

[Note: Due to the ‘Oath of the Sworn,’ the abilities of the imprints are halved in other places.]

I had no choice but to click my tongue.

This girl was just level 1.

But her strength was 36, health 38, agility 41.

At level 1, she was comparable to a level 20 hero. She came out with highly efficient skills like advanced swordsmanship, and had two rare B-grade or higher imprints.

‘No wonder people risk their wealth for such a draw.’

The starting line was different.

I tried all sorts of things to get a born 5-star, but never succeeded.

Of course, even born 5-stars had their drawbacks.

‘It’s hard to trade between users.’

It was possible to trade.

When I was transporting resources from the ruins, I bought a 5-star hero with gems.

But I couldn’t use the guy I bought properly.

He pined away and died.

Born 5-stars could hardly exert their power if their affiliation changed.

The current training center head of Niflheimr was a very rare case.

So to use a 5-star properly, you had to summon and use it yourself.

‘Oath of the Sworn, huh.’

She came out with an obviously annoying penalty.

Abilities of the engravings are halved in ‘other places’?

Bet it would apply in Townia too.

“…”

I looked at Wiryung again.

Going over what I knew about Pick Me Up.

I seemed to know where that woman came from.

‘There’s only one place.’

I smiled bitterly.

That woman wasn’t from Server 2.

In other words, she came from the overseas server of Pick Me Up.

No wonder I was confused.

The Korean server didn’t have that kind of world setting.

It must be Server 3.

Server 3 covered the whole of East Asia, including China and Japan.

In other words, she came from abroad.

In red, (Lotus).

The character was embroidered on the left chest of her clothing.

“Muryeon, huh.”

Wiryung, who had her head bowed, raised it.

“How do you know…!”

“So, you’re a second-hand good.”

Even if the server was different, it was unmistakable.

It was that famous and powerful.

Account name: Muryeon.

The user at the top of the millions of Pick Me Up accounts.

Currently ranked 2nd.

The problem was, that a hero from Muryeon was now in front of me as an event prize.

This could only mean one thing.

I closed my eyes.

‘The second ranker is dead.’

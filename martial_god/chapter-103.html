“…You call yourself an expert, Miss?”

“Why not just bet for Seong Jihan? You’ve earned a lot doing that.”

The initial amount Lee Hayeon had lost was considerably recovered thanks to following Seong Jihan’s bets. However, as she recovered, she became more and more anxious.

“People can’t keep winning like this.”

How could there be an endless streak of victories in this cruel world of gambling? Even if you’ve boasted a 100% win rate so far, this world can turn against you at any time.

And Lee Hayeon believed that this was the time when Seong Jihan wouldn’t come in first.

“Boss. It’s too late…”

‘Such a delayed message actually gives me more assurance.’

Having already made up her mind, Lee Hayeon sipped the remaining wine and approached the computer.

“It’s a secret from the Boss, right?”

“Of course~ But why did you choose Wang Lin instead of Barren?”

“Well, Barren is strong, but he seems a bit unreliable.”

Perhaps it was because of his disgraceful performance during the previous Count Naseed raid.

Although Mages like Barren had the advantage in defense maps, Lee Hayeon believed that Wang Lin from China was the top candidate to win.

“Sister Gayeong.”

“Yes. Honey.”

“…What honey? What are you two signaling about?”

For some reason, it felt annoying.

When Lee Hayeon inquired, Yoon Seah casually responded.

“Oh. She said there’s a tip that helps when doing weights.”

“Oh, there’s something like that?”

“Yes, Miss.”

Although she felt uneasy, if the two of them said so, it must be true.

“Okay. Make sure you learn well by watching what Gayeong does.”

“Sure. Sister. Hehe.”

Feeling guilty that Lee Hayeon was worried about her, Yoon Seah couldn’t help but feel a bit sorry.

However, she thought,

‘Please take care of it.’

Although she felt apologetic, the stakes were too high to miss out.

“Miss, thank you always.”

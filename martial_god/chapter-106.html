The northern wall was shattered in an instant by a gigantic hand made of black iron.

Seong Jihan had seen it before.

‘It’s the iron hand I saw in the 10 towers. Was it called the Black Hand?’

Apostle of Destruction, ‘Black Hand’.

He hadn’t encountered this monster in his previous life.

The beings called apostles varied greatly in power.

Some were as moderate as the Count Naseed of the Valley of the End map, and others as mighty as the Apostle of the Apocalypse capable of destroying nations.

‘It probably isn’t as strong as the Apostle of the Apocalypse.’

Seong Jihan, wielding his Phoenix’s Arrow, charged straight towards the broken wall.

For Ariel, who could transform her body into a shadow, the Black Hand’s physical attacks were ineffective.

However,

The same was true for Ariel’s attacks on the Black Hand.

The shadow of the Black Hand rose and transformed into a sword, piercing through it, but…

“So hard…”

Clang!

The Black Hand was incredibly hard, Ariel’s shadow sword couldn’t even scratch it, as they both shared the same dark nature.

Neither could harm the other.

To break this deadlock, outside intervention was necessary.

And then,

“He’s here.”

The Black Hand let out an exclamation, sensing the energy from the Thunderbolt Strike of the Phoenix’s Arrow.

The Black Hand turned transparent again.

From Seong Jihan’s keen senses, the iron hand had completely vanished.

The Black Hand that disappeared in an instant, leaping freely through space.

The Black Hand seemed blocked by something in the air.

The Absolute Domain of the Force.

This expanded realm, growing with the power level, didn’t easily get pushed by the Black Hand.

How did it completely erase its presence?

Seong Jihan decided he must restrain it with all his might now that it has shown itself.

The swirling spirits inside had twisted faces.

All of them wore crowns.

Whoosh!

When gathered, the spirits didn’t seem like much.

But when spread out, each of them showed their extraordinary presence.

After all, if they were ‘Kings’ acknowledged by Kill the King,

A giant man riding a horse with a beard.

He didn’t look as majestic as when Seong Jihan saw him in the Conqueror’s Mausoleum.

[Black Magician. Can you revive me?]

This newly appeared soul of the Conqueror, unlike the one who brought him, was full of confidence.

He referred to the robed figure as the Black Magician and asked confidently.

“Ah, yes. Do you want to live again? You’ve already conquered the continent. You left the world too early.”

[My empire is crumbling. I need to set it right.]

“You justify with even more trivial reasons. Look at the ghost you brought here.”

The robed figure casually pointed to the side.

The newly arrived soul of the Conqueror turned and looked surprised.

[What, why… why am I there?]

“You from the original world have already been consumed by me. This is a fictional world recreating that moment. You are… yeah, a fake.”

[That’s impossible…!]

“And your real soul here told me his true intentions. Hey, want to say it?”

When the robed figure pointed at the real soul, he slowly opened his mouth with a dark expression.

[…You’re right. I simply wanted to live longer. My will to live never faded, which is why I existed as a spirit in the tomb.]

[Being deceived by the promise of revival and coming here. Even if it’s me, it’s really foolish. Eventually, you’ll be absorbed into me.]

The power that seemed like it could burn this alternate world at any moment.

“Hey, he seems pretty strong, doesn’t he? Does the fake seem stronger?”

[There are no system constraints in this world.]

Seong Jihan deduced that the ‘other one’ the robed figure was referring to was himself.

After all, this place was inside the BattleNet game.

Even if he was captured and died here, it naturally had nothing to do with his real self.

‘But for some reason, it doesn’t feel like it would end just like that if I were caught right now.’

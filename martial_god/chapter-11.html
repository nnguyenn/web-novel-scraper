-Battlenet Match Prediction Specialist, Mister Seong’s Channel

Even though she came from a wealthy family, why was she subscribed to such a gambling-oriented channel?

“Hold on a second, Miss.”

Gayeong urgently stopped Ha-yeon from scrolling the mouse wheel down.

“Huh? Why?”

“Look at the title of the live broadcast right at the top!”

“Uh, why would this person suddenly do a broadcast? It’s been a while… Huh?”

Hayeon initially raised her head absentmindedly, then widened her eyes.

The title of the live video.

“Daughter and Brother-in-law of the Sword King addressing the Current Situation and Sharing the Conversation with the Sword King.”

“I couldn’t reveal that I came from the future, but…”

[Currently 10,000 viewers have gathered.]

[Standard Achievement, ‘Gather Viewers (1)’ has been cleared.]

[Obtained 1,000 Achievement Points as a reward.]

And as Seong Jihan, who was putting on a convincing act, continued, an achievement point notification popped up before his eyes.

“Just as I thought.”

BattleTube was also part of Battle Net. Here, too, one could earn achievement points. Viewership numbers, subscriber counts, likes, dislikes, total views of a video, and more.The possibilities for earning achievement points on BattleTube were boundless.

“It’s going to be hard to break the ‘dislike’ achievement. I’ll have to receive a lot this time.”

While a video with too many dislikes would result in losses when converted to GP on Battle Tube, Seong Jihan didn’t care much. Achievement points were more important than GP.

Especially now, when he could only achieve a Bronze rank at best, he needed to break as many achievements as possible through major issues.

“After all, my face is going to be on sale anyway.”

Even without stating his position, it would spread widely enough within a week for the whole nation to know. It’s better to earn some achievements while getting favorable media coverage.

And two hours had passed since Seong Jihan began to complain.

[Currently 30,000 viewers have gathered.]

[Standard Achievement, ‘Recruit Viewers (2)’ has been cleared.]

People had come to see it because he had revealed his stance, but after watching him complain for two hours about the screen not showing, they couldn’t help but hit the dislike button.

If the content hadn’t been related to Sword King, 99% of the viewers would have left by now.

“30,000 people? They should hit the dislike button a bit more.”

On the other hand, Seong Ji-han was regretful that he hadn’t achieved the ‘dislike’ achievement (2).

However, it was time to stop the broadcast now.

[Tailing?]

‘As far as I know, the only other owner of the ‘Late Bloomer’ is Xin Youhwa from China.’

Could they have actually sent someone after her?

If it’s the person tailing them, then capturing them would only be catching a small fry.

‘It must be Xin Youhwa.’

Xin Youhwa, the world-ranked second from his past life and the owner of China’s ‘Late Bloomer’ Gift, was targeting Yoon Seah.

‘If they’re making moves like this, I feel like proactively taking them out.’

From Seong Jihan’s perspective, he felt no reluctance in killing people since he had passed through many hardships in his previous life.

But there was a problem…

‘I need to find out where Xin Youhwa is.’

China is vast.

Moreover, unlike in his previous life, Xin Youhwa was not a famous player this time, so it was difficult to find out her whereabouts.

‘I guess I have to keep watching Seah until she becomes a Gold Rank.’

Seong Jihan’s brows furrowed.

He would have loved to take action immediately, but finding a single person in China was almost impossible.

‘It would be great if Xin Youhwa suddenly appeared before me.’

But the opponent wasn’t a fool, so that was unlikely.

Seong Jihan naturally thought so.

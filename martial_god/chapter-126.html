Chapter 126

Outside the BattleNet Testing Arena.

“Ah, that kid isn’t coming. He must be scared.”

One of the national representative warriors who was smoking spat on the ground.

“Ah, I wanted to teach him a lesson. Such a pity.”

“Jaeho, just moments ago you were saying, ‘What if I lose?’ right?”

“Ah, brother! That was just a comment. How can I lose to someone too scared to show up?”

“Ugh… But that guy is also something. What does he think he’s doing by calling everyone and then bailing on the day?”

Although there were still about ten minutes before the start of the test, the players already believed that Seong Jihan wouldn’t show up.

“Ah… Thank God.”

The player who had spat moments ago, Gu Jaeho, sighed with relief internally.

Being the lowest level among the national representative warriors, he had been rumored to be Seong Jihan’s first opponent.

Until moments ago, after watching Seong Jihan’s highlights, Jaeho was half-convinced he’d lose.

‘It was a good idea by Dongwoo to broadcast this, but the level of interest was so high I thought we were doomed…’

A Gold-ranked challenging the Diamond-ranked national team?

This was an unprecedented event worldwide.

Several international media teams were already waiting inside the testing arena, and online, hundreds of millions were eagerly awaiting the live broadcast.

Not just Koreans, but a number driven by significant foreign viewership.

Jaeho was nervous at the thought of becoming the first sacrificial lamb to lose to a Gold-ranked in front of the entire world.

But the absence of Seong Jihan brought a barely concealed smile to his face.

“Ha! If Seong Jihan had shown up, I would’ve taken him down in one hit!”

Jaeho looked genuinely happy as he said this.

Just then.

“Hey, what’s that?”

“What’s flying over there?”

Players standing opposite Jaeho pointed at the sky.

“Huh?”

Jaeho turned and blankly stared up.

Far away, something was approaching at incredible speed, leaving a white trail.

At this velocity, a direct hit could be catastrophic for anyone nearby.

“Damn, what is that?”

“Hey! Dodge it!”

But no one thought to move.

Would you, with a speeding truck coming right at you?

The players stood still, transfixed by the approaching object.

Then, the ‘thing’ stopped abruptly in mid-air.

Realizing its identity, the players dropped their cigarettes in shock.

“Uh… Seong Jihan?”

The figure that had rocketed from the sky was none other than Seong Jihan.

Witnessing this, the minds of the players went blank for a different reason.

‘Did he fly from Sword Palace?’

‘Wasn’t he a Warrior? Can a Gold-ranker even fly like that?’

‘Am I supposed to fight someone like that?’

While the national representative Warriors stood frozen,

“Hello.”

Having landed smoothly, he greeted with a light bow.

“I don’t seem to be late. Let’s begin.”

With that, he beckoned his first opponent, Jaeho, and headed into the Management building.

Chills ran down Jaeho’s spine.

“What… Is he even human?”

Suddenly, Jaeho wasn’t keen on entering the testing arena.

The outcome seemed all too clear.

“Jaeho, what are you doing? Aren’t you going?”

But as Seong Jihan’s first opponent, Jaeho couldn’t back out.

“I’m going, brother.”

With a heavy heart, he entered the testing arena as if being led to slaughter.

And then.

The global spotlight was on the national representative test.

Many experts predicted that Seong Jihan would not lose easily.

– Considering the amazing performance he has shown so far, he could probably beat a few of the current Korean national team warriors.

– But probably not against players above level 225.

– Of course. Players at level 225 are on a different level. Unless Seong Jihan has improved even more.

– I agree. Honestly, I think if he reaches the Platinum Rank, he might be able to beat one or two players at level 225. But he’s only Gold now, right?

– Let’s be clear. There’s a definite limit for a Gold.

No matter how skilled one is, there’s a limit in the ‘fundamental’ level.

He will eventually lose to those above level 225.

This was the consensus among experts, and most people agreed.

Then the test match began.

– Alright, the game is starting!

– This map is a survival map called “Colosseum”, modified by the management bureau.

– Such a unique player. He doesn’t even use the BattleNet connector.

– Yes. Some fans say it doesn’t matter since he won’t get hit anyway…

– Haha! How can a player never get hit, especially a front-line warrior! Isn’t that mindset rather irresponsible?

Seong Jihan’s first opponent was Gu Jaeho, who had vowed to teach him a lesson.

“Please take care of me.”

“Oh, yes. Me too…”

They both bowed their heads to each other in greeting.

After the greeting, Seong Jihan rushed towards Gu Jaeho.

And threw a punch.

Boom!

Gu Jaeho, who hadn’t fully prepared his defenses, had many vulnerabilities, but Seong Jihan deliberately aimed for the shield.

“Kuhack!”

Gu Jaeho, holding an SS-grade shield, couldn’t withstand the blow and was sent flying, shield and all.

With just one blow, the match was over.

– What…?

– It’s, it’s an instant knockout. The game is over…!

Against Lee Yoon-Gi, who had surpassed the level 225 barrier and was part of the national team’s ace line, Seong Jihan still did not use a weapon.

“Not even using a weapon…”

Lee Yoongi seemed hurt in his pride and gritted his teeth.

“Make me use one.”

Seong Jihan shrugged.

Before the unification of the Three Dantians, he would have had to use a weapon against an opponent like Lee Yoongi.

And even with a weapon, he wouldn’t have achieved a one-hit knockout like now.

However.

‘Martial Soul. As expected, it’s in a league of its own.’

Martial Soul, when he truly exerted its power, it was indeed on a different level.

The power, which had been divided between Martial Power and Force, was now unified, allowing him to use it freely.

‘The Martial Soul Domain is incomparable to the old Absolute Domain.’

Although the Martial Soul Domain only extended for 20cm, its efficacy was incomparable to the Absolute Domain of the Force.

“And if I expand this, I can use it like the old Absolute Domain up to 40m.”

Indeed, there was only one being that could obtain the Star’s Ability.

It was an ability completely different from the unique stats.

“To maintain this ability, I’ll eventually have to fight against the Martial God… but that’s for later.”

He had decided not to confront him until he was fully matured, so that would be a long time from now.

Right now, he just had to focus on what was in front of him.

‘As befits the owner of the Martial Soul, it’s overwhelming.’

Seong Jihan slowly extended his fist, glaring at Lee Yoongi, who seemed to want to kill him.

It was a slow movement.

It wasn’t really an attack, but more like a provocative gesture.

“You brat… Are you not taking this seriously?”

Seeing that, Lee Yoongi tried to rush at Seong Jihan.

But even before his foot could take off the ground.

Boom!

“Ugh…”

Lee Yoongi’s body was sent flying backward, his internal organs completely crushed.

Even with a high-grade shield.

Even with a powerful passive skill like a Protective Barrier, it was of no use.

He was deemed an instant kill and disappeared.

Over level 225, truly representing the national ace line.

Was killed instantly by a punch that didn’t even touch him.

But still…

Being the team leader, he couldn’t be the only one to back out.

With a heavy heart, Kim Dongwoo sat down in the connector.

And when Seong Jihan faced him in-game, he thought,

‘He’s the national team’s warrior leader, I should show some manners.’

Although he could have finished it in one hit, he decided to give one more blow.

Bang! Bang!

“Ah! But Kim Dongwoo! He withstood one hit!”

“…It seems like he just decided to hit him twice.”

With the commentator’s gloomy voice as the last thing heard, that test day became legendary.

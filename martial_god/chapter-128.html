Chapter 128

There was a reason why Seong Jihan put his own name into consideration.

‘A Constellation hunting its own kind… The attitude of the Wandering Martial God is so odd.’

Back then, when he hit Martial Power 30 and acquired the skill Nameless Divine Arts.

The Wandering War God, cautious of him when his unification of Three Dantians was activated, withdrew the Gift and also canceled the transmission of martial arts.

Even at that time, Jihan just thought that the Martial God was highly wary of him.

‘The response was completely different this time.’

Even though Seong Jihan, whom he was highly cautious, came to Tuseong, the Martial God stopped Dongbangsak and instead showed mercy, encouraging him to gain strength.

Wasn’t this reaction so different from the initial one?

The Martial God, who mercilessly hunts down other Constellations by sending ultimatums, was exceptionally generous to him, and he couldn’t understand why.

Moreover,

‘I… obtained the Martial Soul too easily.’

The Martial Soul, a Star’s Abiltiy obtained through the unification of the Three Dantians.

Even if unifying the Three Dantians was a great achievement, the value of the Martial Soul exceeded it by far.

The Martial Soul possessed immeasurable potential, so much so that even after dismissing the national representative, it could not be said to have fully managed its power.

Had he really been that talented in martial arts to earn such ability?

‘I’m not that good…’

Of course, in his previous life, he did rise to the position of the strongest among humanity, but obtaining the Martial Soul was an incomparable achievement.

Yet, obtaining the Martial Soul this easily made him think that there must be some connection between the wandering War God and himself.

So he tried inserting the name Jihan, but…

[Incorrect.]

[Only the last chance remaining.]

[Hint: It is related to an ancient existence.]

‘Oh, it wasn’t.’

Seong Jihan sighed in relief, seeing the hint along with the notification of the wrong answer.

‘It’s fortunate that it wasn’t as I feared.’

Seeing that it was related to an ancient existence, it seemed that the Martial God was an entity separate from himself.

‘I’ll have to modify the three advanced techniques to upgrade the Nameless Divine Arts.’

Since the last chance was without much clue, he decided to leave it for now.

Seong Jihan decided to pursue the upgrade in a conventional manner for the time being.

‘I have somewhat caught the clue for modifying the Divine Thunder Decree.’

When it mixed with the flame of the phoenix, the power of thunder, had become much stronger.

The direction for modifying the martial arts of the Divine Thunder Decree seemed to be to integrate it with the Phoenix Flame.

‘There are no clues for the others.’

However, he had no idea how to modify the other two advanced techniques.

Seong Jihan decided to start changing the Divine Thunder Decree one at a time to suit himself.

“Uncle~ Come eat!”

“Is it ready already?”

“Yeah. Ariel is good at cooking. With her help, it was finished quickly.”

“Okay. I’m coming.”

She said she’ll tasted it but she cooked too?

Seong Jihan organized his thoughts and left the room with a lighter heart.

Four days had passed since Seong Jihan took the test.

In a conference room of the BattleNet Management Department, members of the National Representative Selection Committee gathered.

The National Representative Selection Committee was mostly composed of executives from the top 10 guilds in Korea, so they had a certain rapport.

“Did you see the reporters when you came in today?”

“Yes. There were way more than usual. It seemed to be twice as many as when the list of national representatives was announced in the second half of the year.”

“Goodness… just because of one player, what a fuss.”

“That player is none other than Seong Jihan.”

“Now, now. Committee members. Please take your seats.”

As the chairman stepped forward, everyone took their designated seats.

“The reason I have called you all here today is to revise the National Representative selection criteria of the Management Department.”

“Heh, we have to revise the selection criteria just because of one player…”

“The public sentiment is not ordinary. It’s been 3 days and they’re making a fuss asking why the selection is not being made yet.”

The selection of BattleNet National Representatives.

Since the rank in the BattleNet League wasn’t just a rank in entertainment, but something on which the future of a country hinged.

The representative selection went through as systematic and fair a process as possible.

So, even if Seong Jihan showed an insane performance, it was natural that it took some time.

But the public, who saw Seong Jihan’s one-man show, didn’t think so.

“My elementary school daughter also scolded me, asking what is daddy doing.”

“Hehe. My father, who is in a nursing home, called me. He asked if there was any reason not to pick Seong Jihan. Even though I tell him he’ll be picked soon, he forgets because of his dementia and contacts me every day.”

“Haha… That must be quite troublesome.”

“I wonder if there’s anyone here who hasn’t received such a question.”

After witnessing Seong Jihan’s performance, which blew away all the national-level Warriors in one room, the public opinion, which initially opposed Jihan being a representative because he was a Gold player, completely flipped, sending unanimous support.

Especially, the prevailing opinion was that Jihan should be put into the game against Russia, which will take place in a week.

“Okay. Then, let’s revise the criteria for selecting the Korean national representatives from Daimond Rank to Gold and above. Is there anyone against it?”

In this situation, opposing would be tantamount to becoming a national traitor.

No one raised their hand.

“Alright. So, understanding that everyone agrees… I will revise the national representative selection criteria.”

“And the next agenda is about changing the national representative selection list.”

“Are you replacing player Gu Jaeho with player Seong Jihan?”

“Yes. That’s right. We plan to replace Gu Jaeho, the lowest-ranked Warrior representative, with Seong Jihan. Is there anyone against this?”

“The result is the fairest.”

“No objections.”

Gu Jaeho, who was the first to be defeated by Jihan with one punch, inevitably got a taste of falling out of the national representative selection.

“It’s the first time a meeting has ended so quickly.”

“Shouldn’t we end the losing streak?”

“Did you watch Jihan’s broadcast yesterday? He’s now fighting Hojo with his bare hands. Considering his growth rate, it’s worth expecting.”

“If I don’t watch them, I can’t join in the conversation with my kids. Hehe.”

After the national representative test, Seong Jihan’s channel, which gained subscribers steeply again, surpassed 5 million.

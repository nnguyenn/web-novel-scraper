Chapter 131

“It’s inconvenient without Takeda.”

Ito Shizuru, in Akari’s body, furrowed her brows as she unpacked her luggage.

Takeda Kazuo was currently under detention, led by the Korean government due to the previous incident and was in the midst of a legal battle, leaving her without any assistance.

Clink!

As Akari’s hand paused while organizing the luggage, Ito Shizuru’s expression darkened further.

“Again with this.”

When creating an avatar using the body of an ordinary person, this didn’t happen.

However, since she made an avatar using the body of a Diamond Ranker this time, the control occasionally interrupted abruptly.

Especially, this phenomenon intensified as the distance from Tokyo, where the main body was, increased.

“Akari’s ego must have disappeared, but… just in case, I need to be careful.”

She regretted for a moment that she should’ve just sent Sword King to secretly kidnap her.

Shizuru quickly changed her mind.

“No. If I’m unlucky and the Enchantment breaks, that won’t do.”

When she had placed the Enchantment on Sword King, the most headache-inducing thing was nothing but his fatherly love.

Approaching him was easy.

Because he really liked women.

However, maintaining the Enchantment was another issue.

“Every time he went home, the Enchantment was half lifted.”

The enchantment kept trying to break especially after seeing his daughter’s face.

Shizuru, who did not give up, was able to obtain Sword King through lengthy efforts.

She determined that Yoon Seah absolutely had to stay in Korea.

If she went to Japan together and the Enchantment broke, anything could happen on the spot.

“I must keep the two from meeting as much as possible this time I bring her to Japan.”

She should stay in a safe house in a distant area, not Tokyo.

She set up her laptop inside the officetel, recalling Yoon Seah.

“She often goes shopping at the underground mart.”

Unlike Seong Jihan, who barely left the penthouse, Yoon Seah went out relatively often.

The reason for her outings was mainly to purchase ingredients.

The time was usually between 10 and 11 in the morning.

“I have to stay in the mart during that time.”

While the mart was not small, it would not be easy to run into Yoon Seah.

In any case, unlike Seong Jihan, she could easily enchant Yoon Seah just by making contact.

“As for the way back…”

Her thoughts were gradually organizing.

Now, the remaining plan was to check the route to return to Japan with her.

After a while typing on the keyboard, she…

Clink!

Biting her lip at the sight of a finger stopping, she muttered,

“…I need to finish quickly.”

Her body not obeying was a sign that she shouldn’t stay too long with this avatar.

The stat he received at level 72, Martial Soul.

After leveling up today and investing the stat points, the number did not increase to 105, it stalled.

“This is similar to when I raised Martial Power in my last life.”

In his past life, when he invested stat points into Martial Power, it didn’t just increase; he needed to accumulate a certain amount of training.

This time, Martial Power increased steadily as he invested points, but after being revised to Martial Soul, it rose well to 104 but got stuck before 105.

The Star’s Ability, Martial Soul.

– Player Seong Jihan will enter the BattleNet Center tomorrow. National team coach Noh Youngjun expressed his anticipation, calling him an astonishing player and promising to assign him a suitable role through training during the camp period…

Slurp!

Shizuru was watching the TV news while eating cup noodles.

Korean news channels were extensively covering Seong Jihan’s entry into the BattleNet Center.

‘He’s probably as popular as the former Sword King by now.’

Even though BattleNet’s popularity was absolute, all sorts of media were making a fuss just because one player entered the BattleNet Center.

Compared to the last time she came to Korea, Seong Jihan’s status had risen significantly.

“What if I also join in this situation…….”

She smiled as if she was pleased.

She didn’t have any particular feelings toward Korea as a country.

Rather, she was once a fan of a Korean idol and thus learned Korean to this proficient level.

However.

“Why are all the kids I want here?”

What can you do when all the exceptional players keep emerging from Korea?

Especially Sword King and Seong Jihan, talents who could greatly strengthen Japan’s Warrior class lineup, considered a weakness for so long.

If both of them work as a dynamic duo on the Japanese team, wouldn’t they easily snatch the victory in the World League?

‘Yoon Seah could also be useful if nurtured.’

She imagined a scene where Japanese is spoken publicly in BattleNet channel 0.

It would be quite rewarding once that day comes.

‘By the way… Seong Jihan will be gone from tomorrow.’

Shizuru thoughtfully tossed the empty cup noodles into the sink.

Surely he wouldn’t bring Yoon Seah, a full-grown adult, to the training camp.

‘If Yoon Seah is left alone… the training period is a perfect opportunity.’

Deciding not to miss this chance, Shizuru resolved to check the underground mart layout once more.

And then, as she went down to the mart, she.

‘Huh?’

Was able to spot Yoon Seah, who had been out of sight for several days.

“Sis~ What alcohol should I buy? Huh. You’re buying so much?”

She was on a call, cheerfully filling her cart with various food items and indiscriminately adding different kinds of alcohol.

“Ah… Is alcohol really necessary for a lecture on analysis methods? Can you analyze while drinking? As expected, it’s half the fun…”

She seemed happy.

Shizuru smiled as if she was genuinely pleased to see Yoon Seah.

‘I’ll make you even happier from now on, my daughter~.’

Yoon Seah was already her daughter in her mind.

Shizuru hurriedly approached her.

To use the supporting gift, ‘Edit’, a touch was necessary.

“The beer is…….”

As Shizuru approached, pretending to buy the beer behind Yoon Seah,

“Ah.”

She feigned stumbling and lightly touched Yoon Seah’s arm.

To anyone watching, it would merely seem like an accident.

‘Too easy.’

However, with that touch, the supporting gift ‘Edit’ was activated, and when Yoon Seah’s status abnormality editing window opened.

She quickly added Enchantment and Obedience.

‘Now where…….’

Should she give a command to this girl?

Shizuru thought it was all over, but.

Zap!

From the crown-shaped mark engraved on Yoon Seah’s hand, electricity surged.

[The Seal of Thunder resists mental contamination.]

[‘Enchantment’ does not fully take hold.]

[‘Obedience’ does not fully take hold.]

‘Huh? What’s this?’

Shizuru furrowed her brows at the sudden message.

The Seal of Thunder?

What is this that’s resisting?

“……Huh? You…….”

As Yoon Seah stared at her with a pause.

‘This won’t do.’

Shizuru tried to touch her hand again.

If Enchantment and Obedience do not fully take hold.

Just touch again and edit until it does.

However, her attempt was.

Swoosh!

Blocked by a black hand rising from the floor.

“You… What are you?”

Soon.

Ariel, who had been secretly following behind Yoon Seah, sprang up from the floor.

Chapter 133

Shizuru went too far targeting Yoon Seah.

Seong Jihan gritted his teeth.

‘If it wasn’t for Ariel, it would have been a catastrophe.’

If she hadn’t stalled in the middle, Yoon Seah might have also followed Shizuru.

‘I can’t just be a sitting duck.’

Offense is the best defense.

Seong Jihan decided to prepare a counterattack.

What he needed for that was, of course, information.

“Let’s take her immediately and interrogate her. She must spill every secret.”

Seeing Seong Jihan’s stern demeanor, Ariel, who had become a sword, changed her appearance back to a shadow elf.

“Interrogation? That’s my specialty. Master, may I participate?”

“You… are quite cooperative, huh?”

“I suffered quite a humiliation from this woman. That red dagger gave me quite a hard time.”

“The dagger transmitted through the laptop screen?”

“Yes. At least SS-grade… no, I’m sure it’s… an SSS-grade item. That woman, she’s smeared with money all over.”

Was the mere red-glowing dagger such an extraordinary weapon?

It seemed it wasn’t a lie when it was called a national treasure.

“Alright. Let’s do this together.”

The two began discussing their sinister interrogation plan.

But then…

[Wait, wait! I’ll tell you everything!!]

Akari, who was to be the subject of the interrogation, showed no intention of resisting.

“What?”

[Because of Shizuru, I thought I was going to live and die as a marionette after my body was taken. I will cooperate fully!]

Trembling!

Akari shook, clutching her arms.

[And that woman… after she took over my body, to verify she had full control, she used it to kill my parents… and then laughed at me, saying I couldn’t resist.]

“…So she says, Master.”

Tsk!

The statement involuntarily made him click his tongue.

‘She was more vicious than I imagined.’

Thus, Seong Jihan conversed with Akari through Ariel as a translator.

“Place of residence?”

[Mine? I…]

“Not yours, Shizuru’s.”

[Ah! Yes. She has a house in Tokyo and also in Kyoto. Mostly in Tokyo’s…]

Starting with her place of residence, Akari immediately responded to every question.

She did not stop there and even spilled all of Shizuru’s weaknesses that she knew of.

[Shizuru is obsessed with Akashic Pages. She even created a comprehensive trading company that only purchases Akashic Pages.]

“She created a company just for Akashic Pages?”

[Yes. It’s called SZ Company. It’s a global enterprise spread across the world… and also…]

Akari spilled everything she knew about Shizuru.

This even included murder incidents committed by Akari herself under Shizuru’s command.

Seong Jihan listened to her story and pondered.

‘The Akashic Pages themselves were sustaining Shizuru.’

The Akashic Pages that enhance the performance of Supporting Gifts.

Shizuru was using her abilities beyond their limits by obtaining them at every opportunity.

“The Akashic Pages are the problem. I’d like to completely cut off her supply.”

“Once the tutorial ends, Akashic Pages will rarely appear. If we wait a few months, they will disappear.”

“Really? But I can’t stand to watch her be so triumphant until then…”

He looked at Akari.

“Can you repeat what you’ve just said during a BattleTube broadcast?”

[…Yes. Of course. If it will harm that woman, I’ll do anything.]

“Good. Let’s get a spot ready soon. And Seah, you…”

Seong Jihan looked at Yoon Seah, who was standing beside him with her head bowed.

“What’s the matter?”

“Ah, Uncle. I’m sorry for today’s incident…”

“What do you have to be sorry about? I also didn’t expect something like this to happen.”

It wasn’t something the victim should apologize for.

He should rather be thankful that she was safe.

“However, you’ll have to stick with me until the matter with Shizuru is resolved. Who knows what crazy thing she might do next.”

“I’ll stick to you like a magnet from now on!”

“That’s a good idea. But you… Were you okay using the Veil of the Void?”

“Um… I did see a kind of turquoise fog blooming in clusters from afar.”

“Is that so?”

He had experienced nothing while he was enveloped in it.

Had he been slightly late, even if Shizuru didn’t get to her, she could have been buried in the void.

“And… I didn’t notice out of shock until a moment ago, but a class evolution job called Void Archer has been added.”

“Void Archer?”

“Yeah. It’s sparkling a lot compared to the other classes?”

A sparkling class?

Hearing this, Ariel, who was beside them, asked.

“What color is it sparkling?”

“Gold.”

“A Unique… class.”

“Oh. Unique, is it? What’s that?”

“Yes. It seems like you obtained it by witnessing the void. Void Archer… Although it’s generally advised not to get involved with the void.”

“Why?”

“If you get involved there, they say you can disappear without a trace anytime.”

“Ah.”

Yoon Seah, who was initially happy to hear about the Unique class, couldn’t hide her disappointment at Ariel’s words.

“Then I should give up.”

“It’d be a shame to give up the title of ‘Unique’. The information about the void is just a rumor after all. If you want power, it might be good to choose it.”

“Really? Hmm… I need to think about it.”

An unexpected class obtained through the Veil of the Void.

Yoon Seah decided to consider a bit more whether to change her class to this one.

Meanwhile, Seong Jihan spoke to Akari with a gesture.

“Then, shall we go up and broadcast?”

At Shizuru’s mansion in Tokyo, people were already gathering to gawk after seeing the address disclosed in the broadcast.

“…Yes. Yes. Prime Minister. No, you trust me, right? It’s just Akari’s one-sided claims.”

“Why would I commit murder? Chairman, did you see me as such a person?”

While the previous calls were mainly out of concern, now they were primarily to verify the truth.

Checking the list of callers, she frowned.

‘It seems only those less affected by the Enchantment are calling. I need to meet and strengthen the Enchantment…’

For that, she would need the Akashic Page.

Despite witnessing her and Akari’s exposé, the unwavering support from the Sword King was reassuring.

‘Right… As long as I have the Sword King under my control.’

In the Japanese national team, the Sword King’s position is paramount.

If he continues to support Shizuru unshaken, her current position might waver but won’t be overturned.

“I did everything for the country…”

“I’m here for you.”

“You’ll always be, right?”

Though tears flowed from Shizuru’s eyes, they seemed deeper than usual.

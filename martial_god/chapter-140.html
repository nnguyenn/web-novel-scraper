“I’m sorry about the last time.”

Given Ha Yoori’s overwhelming popularity among men, Ha Yeonjoo expected Seong Jihan to agree to go with her easily.

“Okay, so… you won’t go?”

Ha Yeonjoo hesitated for a moment and then spoke up, “I’m really sorry, but I can’t just let this go. Yoori feels really guilty and depressed, thinking that she ruined the match because of her. Can you please come just once?”

Seong Jihan responded with a firm rejection, and Ha Yeonjoo seemed flustered. However, someone (Seah) was watching from behind.

Meanwhile, Akari kept quiet and stayed Alert.

She had the S-Class gift, ‘Concentrated Sniping’.

The Korean archer team’s main feature was its concentrated shooting, which effectively incapacitated opponents individually. Ha Yeonjoo’s Gift was the key to this strategy.

Seong Jihan remembered how he had analyzed Ha Yeonjoo’s Gift when she was a brilliant Archer of the Space League. She was the best Archer in the world. However, her Gift was not Gold-tier back then.

“So, when she hits the target, her teammates’ accuracy and damage receive a considerable bonus.”

Yeonjoo’s Gift was the core of the Korean archer team’s strategies in team matches.

“After she retired from Space League, the Korean national team which was still kinda strong (Mid), plummeted further.”

The Space League matches featured the best players from around the world.

“The team became weaker after she retired.”

Ha Yeonjoo was also chosen to be an Archer. She died during the match against the Elves.

In the game, death can be resurrected with GP.


But… She made a big mistake during the first season of Space League, which was organized by the World BattleNet Association.

Because many of the top players died in their matches against the Elves, their absence had a significant impact later on. The disaster in the first season was the primary reason the Earth’s league ranking remained at the bottom.

“Ha Yeonjoo, this time I won’t let talented people die.”

Since he had returned, he couldn’t let the Stinky Elves manipulate them twice.

While thinking about how to deal with the Elves in Space League, the car suddenly stopped in front of a hospital.

“We’ve arrived.”

“Manager, what is Yoori doing right now? Is she sleeping? Oh, she was watching the match? Yes, please let her know that we’re coming up together with a guest.”

Ha Yeonjoo made a call to Ha Yoori’s manager.

“Shall we go then?”

Seong Jihan and the others got out of the car. Immediately, more than ten bodyguards emerged from the front and rear cars and surrounded them.

“You have quite a few people.”

“Without bodyguards, daily life is impossible for me. I don’t have a presence like you.”

As Ha Yeonjoo had said, all eyes were focused on her as she entered the hospital, even though it was a VIP ward with few people.

“Wow, it’s Ha Yeonjoo… She looks more like a goddess in person.”

“Seong Jihan is here too?!”

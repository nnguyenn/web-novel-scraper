With nine kills secured, first place was virtually guaranteed. Seong Jihan reached the center of the forest, which strangely had not a single tree. In the middle of this open space, there was a massive, elliptical brown sphere.

‘It’s the seed of the World Tree, absorbing the life force of the surrounding trees.’

Seong Jihan realized that this was the reason why there were no trees in this area.

The nearby trees were rapidly disintegrating, and their life force was wholly dedicated to the seed of the World Tree.

‘Could the previous Red Zone be inside this thing?’

No matter how large the seed of the World Tree might be, it didn’t seem possible for it to encompass the entire Red Zone and the space beyond.

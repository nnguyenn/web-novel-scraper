The administrator had the authority to generate maps as well, it seemed. Seong Jihan furrowed his brow.

While it was good that a penalty was imposed on the ‘Green Administrator,’ the sudden appearance of this entity was causing him a headache.

The World Tree Alliance alone felt like an insurmountable wall from his previous life. Were they somehow connected to the administrator, a figure who seemed to be related to the system?

‘I need to get stronger.’

While he had proven his strength to some extent on Earth, in the broader scope of the universe, he was still far from sufficient.

Thinking about the wandering martial god, at his current level, he couldn’t even beat his subordinate, DongBangSak. In the Space League, he might’ve taken down one of the World Tree Alliance’s planets, but they still had four remaining.

[Warning: The reward may contain traps, so please be cautious.]

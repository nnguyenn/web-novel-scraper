“Seah.”

“Yeah?”

“Eat this.”

“Oh, I’m really fine. Uncle should eat it. It increases stats!”

“I’m fine too.”

Yoon Seah, who had been about to vigorously applaud Seong Jihan’s words, glanced at Akari lying in the corner of the hospital room and cautiously spoke up.

“Then… let’s cut it in half and share it with Akari.”

“What? Why her?” Seong Jihan questioned her.

“If I wasn’t able to resist ‘Charm’ completely, then Akari is in the same boat, right?”

“…Are you willing to give up 3 health points?” Seong Jihan asked Seah.

“Yeah. I’ll find another leaf for myself later. Hehe.”

No matter how much Akari had turned to their side, it was still a valuable item to share.

“Surely, the remnants of Shizuru might still linger in her.”

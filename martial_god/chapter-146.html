Chapter 146

The day before, the true nature of the “method” that Lim Gayeong had mentioned in the Daegi Guild was to trigger a dating scandal.

“Let’s make a scandal between Miss and Seong Jihan.”

“Hey! What are you talking about?”

“Seong Jihan, why do you think they sent Miss to the Daegi Guild from her family?”

“Well…”

“They wanted to seduce you with Miss and recruit you to the Lee family. But now, your status has risen too much, and…”

Lim Gayeong’s expression hardened.

“Miss, who became the Guild Master, is doing too well.”

“Isn’t it a good thing when family members do well?”

“The mistress doesn’t like it.”

Seong Jihan had an idea about the Lee family’s mistress’s story.

‘Lee Hayeon is an illegitimate kid.’

Now, Chairman Lee, who is receiving treatment for severe dementia and not active in public, was known to have a reputation for liking women (too much) when he was healthy. He had a child outside of his marriage, Lee Hayeon, who was brought into the main family because she was special.

The mistress of the family was said to have welcomed Lee Hayeon, who was an illegitimate child, but…

‘The actual situation must have been different.’

Considering that she only matched strange guys with Hayeon, it didn’t look like she loved Lee Hayeon as portrayed on the outside.

“Gayeong, is it okay for you to say that? If you go against Mom…”

“I became independent thanks to the young lady. It’s fine.”

“Huh? Because of me?”

“You made money, didn’t you?”

“That… yes?”

“Yes.”

“Haah. How much did you earn, for heaven’s sake?” Hayeon asked with a little annoyance.

“Thanks to Miss, I could invest with confidence in different things!”

Freed from her dependence on the Divergent Guild, Lim Gayeong now had the opportunity to speak.

“If dating rumors with Seong Jihan emerge, even if it’s the Lee family, they won’t be able to force you to get married to another guy. Then Miss can continue as the Guild Master. And…”

As Lim Gayeong added a few more words in an attempt to persuade Seong Jihan, he responded as if there was no need to listen further.

“That’s fine. Let’s spread the dating rumors.”

“And… Uh, yes?”

With the presence of Lee Hayeon, who possessed S-rank gift, it was as simple as that.

“Let’s spread it today. What’s the most effective way?”

“Well, then…”

Upon hearing the lighting director Lim Gayeong’s words, Hayeon glanced at her from behind.

“I’m fine too! Just do it now!”

Saying so, Hayeon approached Seong Jihan and extended her lips.

“Oh, come on, not like a duck…”

Even when she extended her lips, it was almost comically exaggerated.

“Put your lips together, relax.”

Swiftly, Seong Jihan wrapped his arms around Hayeon’s shoulders and swept her hair back.

While holding the camera, he leaned in with their faces still close.

“What… why is he so skilled at this?”

While Hayeon was taken aback, their lips were getting incredibly close.

Click! Click!

“Okay, that’s enough!”

Yoon Seah quickly finished the shoot, and Seong Jihan returned to his spot.

“Take a look at this. The photos turned out well, didn’t they?”

“Yeah, they’re fine.”

Hayeon still had a blushing face as she looked at the photo.

It was a picture that unmistakably showed Seong Jihan and Hayeon, their lips just about to touch.

In fact, the coach was well aware that they had a shortage of power. But he believed that if he succumbed to despair, it would be the end. Instead, he focused on hopeful elements.

“Luckily, the first two games are on the same South Gate map as the previous Russia match. In the first game, we’ll be on the attack.”

“But in long-range matches, won’t we fall behind?”

“Why play a long-range match when we have Seong Jihan? We’ll use his Instant Death skill aggressively if we have to, but we must secure the first game.”

Seong Jihan’s marksmanship was unparalleled in maps with specific points to defend, like South Gate. Noh Youngjun, the coach, believed in that for the first game, or rather, he had no other option.

“And in the next game…”

Coach Noh Youngjun briefed the players on the strategies from the second to the fifth game. The expressions of the players changed subtly as they listened to the coach’s explanations.

“This… Is this a strategy focused on Seong Jihan?”

“This is a strategy similar to when we had the Sword King.”

For the China match, Seong Jihan was the linchpin in every game. Based on his outstanding performance in the Russia match, the tactics of the Korean national team gradually shifted to revolve around Seong Jihan.

“If he crumbles, it’s over.”

Excluding Seong Jihan, the Korean team’s strategies for the China match were virtually non-existent.

“If it’s not Seong Jihan, there’s no chance of winning.”

“This might be the better way…”

“Let’s focus on support like we did during the Sword King era.”

Thus, the Korean national team prepared their tactics with Seong Jihan at the center.

And time passed quickly. The day of the China match had arrived.

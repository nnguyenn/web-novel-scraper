[The map for the 5th game is the ‘Invasion: Capture the Flag.’]

[75 players will be participating.]

“Wow!”

“It’s a team match!”

Cheers erupted from the Chinese national team.

“Well… it’s a Capture the Flag map.”

“I guess it’s tough to have two individual matches in a row…”

Conversely, the atmosphere among the Korean national team was subdued. They expected the 5th game to be challenging, especially after suffering consecutive defeats in the team matches, except for the first one.

“The map size… with 75 players, it’s considered a small map, right?”

“Yes, that’s correct.”

“This doesn’t look good.”

Coach Noh Youngjun let out a sigh.

Capture the Flag, as the name suggests, is a game where players compete to capture more randomly generated flags at key points on the map. The game’s outcome is typically decided 30 minutes later based on the number of flags stored in each team’s main base. However, this map had several different victory conditions, such as eliminating all players from the opposing team or destroying the enemy’s main base.

The reason for Noh Youngjun’s dark expression was precisely these victory conditions.

“…In small maps, China has a 100% win rate.”

“So, of all things, it had to be a small map! If it were a 100-player map, we would have had a chance!”

Although it was called a small map, the size of the Capture the Flag map was substantial. However, the problem was that Je Galheon, China’s Grand Mage, had a spell range that matched it, even from his main base, sometimes to the point of using super spells.

“Yes… in small maps, even if Je Galheon draws a bad card, the meteor strike spell will reach the target due to the Joo-Ryung’s buff.”

“How long did the games take for China?”

“On average, they took about 20 to 30 minutes. If Je Galheon drew a good card, it could end in 15 minutes.”

“Hehe… this is just unbelievable!”

Contrary to the original game mode of Capture the Flag, when China played the map, they unilaterally bombarded the enemy’s main base, and the game ended. No matter how many flags the opposing team secured, they couldn’t win because the place where flags should be stored was blown up.

“Even if they try to disrupt our ability to use magic…”

“It’s because of that damn Golden Dragon. Only Seong Jihan can deal a proper blow.”

“It’s not easy because of 108 Arhat Formation”

Noh Youngjun let out a sigh. China, already a strong team, became even more formidable with 108 Arhat Formation. Of course, the formation itself had many weaknesses, so the side that received the blow could gain an overwhelming numerical advantage.

“Even if it’s 74 to 57, can we win… it’s quite daunting.”

With only one player missing from the team, Noh Youngjun couldn’t see a winning scenario. This was because the synergy between China’s mages and support players was overwhelming. The mood among the coaching staff seemed to have infected the national team players, as they all had dark expressions.

Except for one person. Excluding Seong Jihan.

“Coach, I think we can defeat 108 Arhat Formation this time.”

In the first, second, and third games, 108 Arhat Formation had been a formidable opponent. In the second and third games, they were even more powerful due to the suppression by the enhanced Jin. But Seong Jihan now had confidence. He had the conviction that he could defeat it.

“Really? You think so?”

“Yes, so please bear with it for just 20 minutes.”

“20 minutes…”

Surely, they could endure for about 20 minutes. Noh Youngjun nodded with a glimmer of hope in his heart.

After the formation was disrupted by the Eclipse in the first game, Shinsong Il-ryong continued to use martial arts formations.

With just the Pheonix Spear in hand, Seong Ji-han waited. The warriors holding clubs all charged toward him.

‘His power is only about 20% of its usual strength.’

108 Arhat Formation’s suppression, even with only half its strength, was quite formidable. Even half of the strong martial artists were suppressed. In this state, even the use of other martial arts that are not part of the Martial God system was difficult.

In addition, the fallen martial artists were quickly replaced by new ones, and the living ones swung their staff forcefully.

Seong Jihan’s spear moved gracefully, blocking all attacks and overwhelming the surroundings. However, the martial artists’ tactics seemed endless.

The commentators were exhausted by the relentless martial artists. If someone other than Seong Jihan were inside, they would have been overwhelmed by the staff.

Yet, Seong Jihan, even with his strength suppressed, was parrying all the martial artists’ attacks and counter attacking.

Since gaining the ability of the star and the void after obtaining the Martial Soul, Seong Jihan couldn’t fully control his power. He felt like he was using his newfound strength rather than pushing the limits of his ability. But now, with his power restricted, he could truly appreciate how limitless the potential of the star’s ability was.

“This guy… he’s incredibly persistent!”

The Monks wore an exasperated expression. It seemed like Seong Jihan was getting stronger as time passed.

He couldn’t unleash a powerful technique like in the first round, but his mastery of using the spear was exceptional, evoking both jealousy and awe in Shinsong Il-ryung. Seong Jihan wasn’t just a player who had been chosen to increase his stats. His martial skills were already complete, as if he were a finished martial artist.

“Would you just die already…! Palm of the Golden Buddha!”

Whoosh!

The level of skill contained within was far from being considered sophisticated, but with the assistance of 108 Arhat Formation, the power was quite restrained.

‘I can’t block it with my basic skills.’

That’s why, during the second and third rounds, Seong Jihan avoided the attacks and looked for openings. At that time, he thought there would be plenty of time.

But after losing the second and third rounds, he realized.

‘There’s no room for error on the national team.’

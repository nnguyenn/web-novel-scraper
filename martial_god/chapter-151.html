Chapter 151

“Wow, Uncle! Earlier in the car, I couldn’t speak, so I was getting really antsy… But they said they’re giving you a 10 percent share in the guild!”

Arriving at the penthouse, Yoon Seah excitedly showed the messages exchanged with Sofia to Seong Jihan.

“They’re giving 10 percent?”

Seong Jihan was equally surprised by the generous deal. He had suspected that Robert Gates, besides appointing him as the guild master, might come up with an appealing proposal, but he never expected a 10 percent share.

“Yeah. Isn’t this… heckin’ amazing? If it’s American First, it’s the world’s top guild. Considering the value of this share… Wow, this is incredible.”

Yoon Seah looked up the current value of American First on the internet and was thrilled.

How many zeros are there at the end?

“Still, I won’t go to the United States.”

However, Seong Jihan was momentarily surprised by the fact that they were offering a share but still had no intention of going to the United States.

“Uncle, even though it’s 10 percent?”

“Yeah. I have to feed the World tree’s Leaf to the Sword King. If I go to the United States, the chances of matching with Japan will be almost gone.”

The regional leagues were different for the United States and Japan. For the two countries to face each other, they had to meet in the World Champions League.

In that case, it made more sense for Seong Jihan, who was part of Korea in the same regional league.

“Oh… Am I crazy? Uncle, thinking about dad like that while I’m here getting lost in the share…”

“10 percent is quite a lot.”

“Thank you… Uncle. To give that up for Dad…”

Yoon Seah seemed deeply moved, but Seong Jihan just smiled bitterly.

“Well, it was originally because of the nationality bonus.”

Initially, he had intended to stay here due to the nationality bonus that could only be obtained in Korea.

However, after obtaining the martial soul, he no longer needed to rely heavily on the nationality bonus. He was confident that he could reach the top of the player world rankings without the nationality bonus, as long as he properly developed his martial skills and raised the martial soul stat.

But there was a catch.

‘After all, I’ll just be a foreigner there.’

Through his experience of exile, Seong Jihan had personally felt how difficult it was to fully assimilate into American society as a foreigner.

‘I already have a solid foundation in Korea; there’s really no need to go abroad. The only issue might be at the national team level, but… if Seah continues to grow and reaches Sword King’s level, the situation will improve. But, That’s only when we solve Ito Shizuru’s scandals.’

Regarding the national team level, there won’t be any more concerns. So, his decision was not to go to the United States.

“Seah, would you really feel okay just passing up on this offer?”

“Why? Do you have a good reason?”

“Before, when you looked at this house, Did it stress you out… Does it still bother you?”

In response to Seong Jihan’s question, Yoon Seah looked around the house and slowly shook her head. While in the past, the traces of her dad’s abandonment had made her feel suffocated and melancholic just by staying in the house, it was no longer the case.

“No, it doesn’t. I mean, now I know why my dad did that…”

“In that case, you can regain the building.”

“Regain it? Don’t you mean the building you donated back then?”

“Yes, exactly. The situation is different from back then.”

Back when the Sword King abandoned his family to go to Japan, Yoon Seah faced baseless criticism and condemnation. But now, with Seong Jihan emerging as the only hope for the Korean national team, the situation had completely turned around in just a few months.

Seong Jihan chuckled.

It was time to reclaim the Sword Palace.

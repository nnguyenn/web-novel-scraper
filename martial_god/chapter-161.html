Chapter 161

‘Already conceding?’

Seeing Brahma show such a weak side, Seong Jihan was inwardly surprised.

While most players would feel regret not getting more out of a Constellation’s sponsorship, Seong Jihan had an attitude of taking it if given, and not minding if not. So he had intentionally acted strongly, but…

‘He must really covet Crimson Thunder.’

The Crimson Thunder’s power created by fusion of Phoenix Flame and Divine Thunder clearly surpassed normal Celestial Judgment.

But as the God of Thunder, Brahma should be able to create Crimson Thunder too…

“Why does the God of Thunder covet Crimson Thunder so much? Can’t you make it yourself?”

When Jihan straightforwardly asked, the system messages stopped appearing for a while, but then they continued.

[Brahma states that the Crimson Thunder and the Thunder God’s powers cannot be mixed.]

[Only you, possessing the abilities of a star, can fuse them]

[He asks again for you to complete Crimson Thunder, saying he will fulfill what he can within reason, so please fuse them.]

“What I want…”

Seong Jihan contemplated deeply about what to ask from Brahma.

‘Nothing’s really coming to mind.’

In the past, during the time of martial power and force, he might have asked for stat points or something like that.

But now, he had surplus points left over since he had to modify skills to raise his Martial Soul anyway.

He had enough GP to operate Daegi Guild for now as well.

‘The only usable thing might be items. But with him coveting Crimson Thunder so much, it feels unsatisfying to just take an item for it…’

While thinking about what to ask for, Seong Jihan threw a question at him.

“Let’s talk about what I want later. Why do you need Crimson Thunder so much?”

[Brahma answers that it’s a question he can’t answer through the system.]

Then, a new message appeared in the system window.

Ding!

[The special dungeon map ‘Divine Throne – Brahma’ opens for Player Seong Jihan.]

[The special dungeon can only be entered by the permitted player Seong Jihan and his party members.]

[The special dungeon can be entered once per week.]

[The special dungeon is not included in the 1 game per day limit.]

‘Oh?’ A special dungeon suddenly opened.

Seeing a map exempt from BattleNet’s normal restrictions appear, Seong Jihan realized Constellation Brahma was no ordinary existence.

He hadn’t seen any Constellations capable of this in his previous life.

[Brahma says if you reach the Divine Throne, he will answer directly. He tells you to think about what you want in the meantime.]

“Alright.”

And with that, Brahma, who had given the rare stat Hellfire and the special dungeon, disappeared.

Seong Jihan organized the rewards he had obtained from the Promotion Match.

He had earned 1.8 million achievement points and his level had risen by 20 for surviving the Promotion Match.

‘At this rate, I might reach Diamond by December. I should pace myself a bit.’

He needed to be a Platinum player by December to qualify for the Space League opening match. Seong Jihan thought about the opening match from his previous life that had astonished the world, the result that Earth had accepted, and the need to turn it around for the World Tree Alliance, who had been their opponents.

‘The achievement points are hard to use.’

The Achievement Shop allowed upgrades only after donating five times. The “All for One” category, the most useful one, had already been upgraded to the maximum level. The other categories like Status Screen Expansion, had no immediate utility.

‘Regardless, I’ll have to upgrade them eventually. Let’s raise them to LV4 for now.’

So, he used 1.05 million achievement points to upgrade all the items in the shop to LV4. With over 700,000 points left, he contemplated how to use them.

‘Should I try opening a random box?’

There was a monthly changing “Random Box of the Month.” The items for October were related to items. Seong Jihan had not been interested in them until now, but since he had no immediate use for his points, he decided to open a box.

And…

“Huh…”

[Donation Reset Voucher]

– Grade: F

– Allows you to donate to the achievement store again.

– You are a donation angel!

Seong Jihan chuckled, seemingly disappointed. It was truly an item fitting for F-grade, a trash item. However, it had immediate utility.

‘Upgrading the Achievement Shop will be a month faster now.’

By using the Donation Reset Voucher, Seong Jihan donated 10,000 points again.

[You have donated 10,000 points to the system!]

[Vitality envelops your body. Fatigue will accumulate more slowly for one month.]

‘Now I only need to do this three more times, once a month, and it’ll take three months but…’

He didn’t want to drag this out until next year.

‘Let me pull the Donation Reset Voucher one more time.’

Seong Jihan reached into the random box again.

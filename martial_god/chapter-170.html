Chapter 170

Seong Jihan’s newly displayed martial art, Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods.

That martial art, possessing the earth attribute among the Five Elements, seemed to be a failure at first glance.

Whooooosh…

The smoke rising from the three swords didn’t rise any further and settled onto the ground, soon disappearing.

However,

[Unbelievable.]

The succubus queen, who was in the sky, couldn’t help but gaze at a certain spot with amazement. The location she was observing was the ground where the three swords were embedded. There, a small pattern was barely visible near the swords, appearing as a mere doodle of red markings when seen from a distance. Even when zoomed in, the enigmatic characters and geometric patterns made it impossible to grasp what it was.

Nevertheless,

[…A seal against demonic contracts. Impossible.]

The succubus queen, after just a brief glance, already had some understanding of Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods. A demonic contract is an absolute, universal law that even most divine beings cannot break. However, a seal made by mere humans prevented soul induction.

[…I cannot believe it.]

The Succubus Queen scrutinized the Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods then changed her tone gently towards Seong Jihan.

[Alright. Breaking it seems impossible. Then… would you consider making a deal? If you release the soul of the contractor, I will offer you a substantial reward.]

Unable to break it, negotiating seems to be her alternative. Seong Jihan pondered if there was anything to gain from the succubus queen in her current smoky state and responded.

“If you promise never to come to this planet again, I’ll think about it.”

[That’s impossible. I’ve already been assigned here.]

“Who assigned you?”

[I cannot disclose that.]

Seong Jihan furrowed his brow. In any case, it seems like she’s coming back.

In that case, he couldn’t afford to hand over the soul.

“Alright then, leave.”

[Don’t be like that… How about 10 succubi? I will specially bestow girls prepared to serve gods. They will follow you as their master until your death.]

“I don’t need that.”

[To be so decisive… Perhaps you prefer the same gender? While they are succubus underlings, I can also provide incubi. I have a deep connection with them as well. Hehe.]

Incubi, are those male demons? Seong Jihan furrowed his brow and brushed his hand.

“I don’t need anything else.”

[Then what do you like? In the Palace of Lust, there are entities that cater to all your tastes. Just name it.]

“I just don’t wanna see you.”

[Oh, do you desire me? You are not yet qualified to be with me.]

Is she even listening properly?

Seong Jihan decided not to respond and stared at the ground.

‘The scale is so small, it’s closer to failure.’

Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods.

Unlike its name suggesting it seals 10,000 ghosts,

What Seong Jihan has just unleashed is barely enough to contain one soul, that of Shizuru.

Perhaps because his internal energy level wasn’t high enough yet, and he wasn’t using it from the skill window like in his past life.

His proficiency in Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods was severely lacking, resulting in this small scale.

‘But even this much can…’

It could still demonstrate some of Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods’ power.

As Seong Jihan twisted the three swords.

Swish, swish…

The pattern of Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods simultaneously spun around then

[However, if you become stronger, I might consider meeting you as an avatar… Hmm?]

The succubus queen, who had been babbling for a while, rapidly began to disintegrate.

[Me too? It doesn’t simply end at the sealing formation…!]

With those words as her last, the succubus queen was completely sucked into Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods. Once she disappeared, Seong Jihan withdrew the three swords from the ground, and once again struck the patterns.

Bang!

As he did, the patterns of Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods that were drawn under the swords burst out.

Ahhhh!

From there, a sinister wail echoed, chilling to the bone.

‘Unintentionally, I’ve completely annihilated Shizuru’s soul as well.’

Though she was an enemy, he hadn’t intended to destroy even her soul by using Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods.

Who could blame her when she made a contract with the Succubus Queen.

Seong Jihan casually returned Ganjang and Makya to the Sword King then spoke breezily.

“It’s over. Let’s head back now. To Korea.”

“…Yes. We can finally go back.”

The Sword King’s voice was tinged with remorse. Now he had to figure out how to face his daughter, Yoon Seah, in Korea. The moments when he repeatedly claimed to have no daughter while representing Japan at the national level suddenly flashed in his mind.

No matter how infatuated he was with Shizuru, he shouldn’t have said such things.

“Sigh…”

As the Sword King sighed deeply in his melancholy, Akari cautiously asked Seong Jihan.

“Um… May I ask how you plan to go back…?”

“We’ll go the same way we came.”

“On the cloud?”

“Yeah. Summon Lightning Cloud.”

As Seong Jihan promptly summoned a lightning cloud upon saying that, Akari’s expression turned sullen.

If it wasn’t for the fact that she had a diamond-ranked physique, she probably would’ve vomited several times crossing the ocean on cloud.

“We’re riding that again across the ocean all the way to Korea?”

“How else do you suggest we go?”

“The National Intelligence Service gave me an emergency contact method… They said they can send transportation at any time.”

“You wanna keep waiting on this remote island until those people arrive?”

“No…”

Akari sighed and kicked the beach where Shizuru’s corpse had been.

If you were gonna die like this, why run all the way here to cause trouble for others.

Useless to the very end.

“Let’s go.”

“Yes!”

At Seong Jihan’s gesture, Akari stopped kicking the sand and got on the cloud.

It was the end of a long and intense pursuit.

The third descendant, who had already been asleep for 400 years.

For Seong Jihan to wake him, he had to seize destiny like this.

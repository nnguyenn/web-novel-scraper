Chapter 171

In front of the Sword Palace.

Reporters were bustling, discussing the return.

“He should be returning around now…”

Not knowing about the existence of internal energy, they only knew Seong Jihan’s spatial control as “force”.

Although the reporters were annoyed every time their bodies were paralyzed by force, since Seong Jihan became a national representative, his popularity skyrocketed. Now, getting paralyzed by him was considered an honor.

“It’s the association’s car!”

The reporters completely swarmed around the car.

“Let me pass.”

As Seong Jihan came out of the car, he extended his hand.

As if Moses parted the Red Sea, the reporters’ bodies floated up on either side, creating a path.

This was a method Seong Jihan commonly used when media coverage became too intense. He didn’t stop there; he even used force to subdue the reporters. However, the reporters’ reactions were odd this time.

‘Why do they seem to be liking it?’

Their usual response was to frown or look scared before their bodies stiffened. But now, they were smiling and expressing joy.

Moreover,

“Sunbae, please do it for me next time!”

Click! Click!

Junior reporters took pictures of the paralyzed ones.

Many questions were thrown at him, but the Sword King Yoon Sejin did not answer any other than saying,

“Murder charge….”

Slowly walking down the path opened by Seong Jihan, he opened his mouth towards the reporters.

“I did not kill Ito Shizuru.”

“What…?!”

“Ito Shizuru isn’t dead?”

“Yes. I just returned because I lost track of her. I failed.”

“But the Prime Minister of Japan said that the enchantment was lifted, and Ito Shizuru was dead….”

Speaking in a serious tone, Yoon Sejin recalled what had transpired just before.

* * *

Even though players may kill enemies in games, committing murder in reality is an entirely different matter. Yoon Sejin and Akari.

And Manager Park Yoonsik as well.

Y-yes! Ah, right. Ito Shizuru is tough, tough! It’s so regrettable. We should have completely uprooted her….”

Understanding Seong Jihan’s signal, Akari readily accepted it.

Seeing this, Yoon Sejin asked in disbelief.

“Jihan… Will that work?”

“If they’re already making baseless claims, we just have to respond in kind.”

Forced situations call for forced measures, and Seong Jihan had plenty of experience dealing with dirty plays.

“Besides, we have cards to play.”

“Soon, the Space League will begin.”

“A boycott of the Space League… You won’t participate?”

Park Yoonsik’s eyes widened.

The Space League, the dream stage coveted by all players.

To boycott that from the player’s side…

“A player who was stripped of his national team status getting selected for the Space League? Does that make sense? According to their argument, it’s aiding and abetting murder. They need to reflect on their actions.”

“If… if they actually accept that and you change your mind…”

“I hope it comes to that. Playing matches without the world’s 1st and 2nd-ranked warriors will show how significant those vacancies are.”

Seong Jihan recalled the opening match of the Space League from his previous life. The overwhelming performance by the Sword King during the opening series. If he hadn’t been there, the battle against the World Tree Association would have ended in a mundane 3-game losing streak. If the side that won that game was Earth, the MVP would undoubtedly have gone to the Sword King.

“I want to show clearly which side is more regrettable.”

Although he had prepared a lot in this life to lead the opening match to victory, if the World BattleNet Association came out like that, Seong Jihan had no intention of being involved in the opening match.

He wasn’t the type to just endure and let things pass.

And according to the policy set during this time on the plane,

“Now, Japan, stop hiding Ito Shizuru and make her face the appropriate charges. If the BattleNet Association unreasonably listens to one side and issues an unfair punishment, I will boycott the Space League.”

“S-Space League…?”

“A player boycotting the Space League…!”

“A player without national team qualification, how will they represent Earth?”

“I also agree with that. According to the Japanese side’s argument, I’m also an accomplice to murder. It seems I don’t have the qualifications to represent Earth.”

Those were Seong Jihan’s final words before entering the penthouse exclusive elevator with his group.

“Even Seong Jihan…!”

The penthouse of Sword Palace.

Yoon Sejin descended from the elevator with a trembling heart. What should he say in front of his daughter?

‘Do I even have the right to be here?’

Feeling he had no face to see Yoon Seah, he thought it might be better that his eyes were gone for now.

With those heavy thoughts, he moved his heavy footsteps.

“Dad!”

His unseen eyes.

But his daughter’s voice in his ears was still cheerful like in his memories.

However, Yoon Sejin, whose senses had become extremely heightened, could feel it.

Yoon Seah taking deep breaths several times, composing herself and trying hard to sound cheerful.

“How was… your trip?”

Trip.

She expressed the recent events with that single word while smiling.

“Seah… I’m truly sorry…”

“Why are you sorry! Don’t go getting enchanted on purpose!”

Listening to that voice, Yoon Sejin lowered his head unconsciously.

As if nothing had happened. As if nothing was different from usual. Seeing his daughter, who treated him, the father who had gone out and returned as if nothing had happened, he couldn’t raise his head.

Thinking of the past when he was crazy about Shizuru and repeatedly said with his own mouth that he had no daughter, he couldn’t face her because he felt ashamed.

The leaves of the World Tree remove abnormalities and restore vitality. He consumed it on his way back, but his vision didn’t fully recover.

“It’s okay. I deserved it.”

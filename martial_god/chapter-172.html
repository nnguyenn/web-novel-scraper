Chapter 172

“Mom…”

Yoon Seah’s eyes widened in surprise.

“But… mom has already passed away…didn’t she?”

Seong Jihan nodded beside her as well.

The death of his sister.

Hadn’t the whole nation witnessed it through live broadcast?

“Yeah, while trying to block the dungeon break in the north, she eventually used the self-sacrifice command…”

North Korea was the first country in the world to be destroyed by a dungeon.


They refrained from fostering players who might threaten their regime.

In the already turbulent Northeast Asia League, they ended up at the bottom with an overwhelming score.

There were a few other authoritarian regimes that also avoided nurturing players.

However, those countries didn’t rank last in league points because their regional leagues were not as tight as North Korea’s.

Thus, since 2011, when dungeons began to appear, North Korea, which had become a dungeon-infested area, was soon engulfed by the abyss.

‘The Abyss emerged.’

Upper dungeons formed by several dungeon portals merging.


The ‘Abyss’ engulfed North Korea.

Unlike dungeon portals, from the crimson rifts that engulfed the land, extremely powerful monsters emerged.

Especially the ghost-type monsters from the North Abyss couldn’t be harmed by modern weapons.

Even with the entire army mobilized, North Korea couldn’t stop the enemy.

So, North Korea collapsed, and about five years later, near the 38th parallel, a group of survivors, thought to have died, began to move south.

Around the same time, the argument arose, mainly led by human rights organizations, that North Korean compatriots needed to be rescued.

‘Unlike the early days of BattleNet, players have grown a lot, and the government showed a positive response to this, but…’

The result was a gruesome failure.

The monsters summoned to North Korea continued to pour out of the dungeon portals even if killed.

Especially as you went further north, most of them were ghost monsters, so the military couldn’t exert much force.

When calls to retreat began to emerge,


A player accidentally stepped into the Abyss area, and a new message appeared in the system.

[The owner ‘??’ of the Abyss is enraged by the invasion of its territory.]

[Dungeon Break is activated. Monsters belonging to dungeon portals will move beyond their territory.]

[Defend against the enemy’s invasion!]

Along with that message, North Korean monsters came south on a large scale.

Initially, it started under the pretext of rescuing North Korean citizens, but the outcome was a terrible disaster.

The massive ghost legions moving south.

Gold-ranked and above players of South Korea were mobilized to block the 38th parallel.


The first invasion was somehow repelled with almost no casualties.

But as the second and third invasions began, casualties started to occur.

And with the fourth invasion, when a massive darkness came down from the north,


Korean society fell into panic.

No matter how many players were gathered, the scale was far too massive to block.

Just as it seemed the defense line would crumble,

-I’ll… use my Gift.

Seong Jiah used her Gift, ‘Sacrifice’, to its limit.

Upon doing so, her body lost its vitality and turned into hard stone.

As the light burst out in all directions, the darkness that surged like a tidal wave hesitated and then stopped moving.

[If she’s a saint… she’s suitable as a sacrifice.]

With those final words, the darkness devoured Seong Jiah then retreated.

The sacrifice scene of Seong jiah, witnessed by the entire nation through live broadcast.

People praised her as a true saint, promising not to forget her sacrifice, but for the remaining family members, this incident was nothing short of a thunderbolt.

After that day, Sword King Yoon Sejin, who had been dispatched to prevent the invasion in other regions, began seeking solace in alcohol, saying that he should have been with Jiah that day.

Seong Jihan, confined to his home, became more immersed in gambling.

Trying to consider the gloomy home atmosphere, Yoon Seah made an effort to act as brightly as before, but the forced smiles were obvious.

Seong Jiah’s death completely overturned the Yoon Sejin household like so.

However,

“How do you plan to find deceased noona with this ring?”

When Seong Jihan asked, looking at the Aegis ring, the Sword King slowly opened his mouth.

“I remember what she said before modifying the ring. She looked at the ring’s options and said it had an owner.”

“Isn’t it because my noona was the owner of the ring?”

“No, no. It’s an item that chooses a new owner when the owner dies, which is interesting. She said your wife was alive, and she dared me to ask if she could still live with me.”

“I see…”

Seong jihan’s eyes sparkled.

There was no reason for Shizuru, who had already fascinated the Sword King, to lie.

Does that mean my noona is alive?

Even though she turned into stone and was swallowed by darkness…

“Do you have a method in mind? To find noona?”

“I was thinking of searching the dungeons in the north.”

“…Is that all?”

“Well… if we trace Jiah’s relics and the ring, won’t we eventually find her?”

He had asked for the ring thinking there was some groundbreaking method, but

This was way too half-assed, wasn’t it?

“Just blindly? What if you trigger another Dungeon Break situation by stepping into an Abyss again like last time?”

“I’d have to… somehow evade it well.”

“…Seeing this thoughtless side, you really are dad.”

Yoon Seah looked at Yoon Sejin, who was about to leave without any plan, and instead of being worried, she seemed rather pleased.

He maintained a dignified appearance outside as a top Warrior player, but

At home, he was scolded daily by mom for having no thoughts at all, still the same as before.

“Please wait a moment. Ariel.”

However, sending him off like this might lead to a big catastrophe.

“Why?”

“How do you deal with the Abyss in your world?”

“If it’s the Abyss… are you talking about the merging of dungeon portals?”

“Yeah.”

“Geez, your world really knows nothing. No wonder you didn’t even know how to remove dungeon portals…”

Ariel spread the shadow aura into the air and drew a circle.

A large circle with a center.

Around it, black dots were scattered here and there. Among them, Ariel pointed to one of the black dots.

“The Abyss absorbs the magic power of nearby dungeon portals and strengthens itself. To get rid of this, it’s urgent to eliminate the nearby dungeon portals first.”

“Hmm, the solution was simple.”

“Right. In the first place, Abysses don’t form on other planets except during the end times. Since dungeon portals are swiftly removed during exploration.”

“So the Abyss only forms under the extreme situation of not even being able to remove dungeon portals.”

“Right. That’s why planets where Abyss appears are destined to see the end soon. Only Destruction.”

Having heard a brief strategy for dealing with Abyss from Ariel, Yoon Sejin opened his mouth with an interesting tone.

“I see, this being is… your summon? Then, if we eliminate all the dungeons around North Korea and move forward…”

“To get rid of dungeon portals, you need a player with the exploration gift.”

“We can somehow obtain them if we just pay enough GP, right? GP is…”

“…Oh right, I gave it all to Shizuru…”

“You have none left?”

“I only received a modest allowance for maintaining dignity when I urgently needed money. I think I left only a million GP…”

While ten billion may seem like a huge amount to ordinary people, from the perspective of the Sword King, who possessed wealth exceeding tens of trillions, it was a situation where almost everything he had was taken away.

“Sorry, brother-in-law, to ask this right after you helped me… Could you lend me some GP?”

Seeing the pitiful reality of the world’s top Warrior, Seong Jihan sighed.

“I can lend you as much money as you want, but to remove the dungeon portals in the north, we need to prepare properly. It’s not something we can do with just a player with exploration abilities.”

“Umm…”

Investigating the dungeon portal in North Korea. If Seong jiah really is alive, it is a task that must be pursued.

However, relying solely on the Sword King seemed somewhat uneasy. Seong jihan thought about contacting the association to form an investigation team but then realized, ‘Ah, right… I can use them.’

Seong jihan’s eyes flashed as if he had come up with a good idea.

Dungeons that only appear in the bottom 10% of countries.

Rather than setting up an investigation base camp in such dangerous places, having them investigate North Korea from the stable nation of Korea was far better for the alliance.

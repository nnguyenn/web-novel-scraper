“Miss, there’s a guest from the People’s Guild.”

Since the decision to select the 20 players who would stand on the chaotic battlefield was made within the major guilds in China, people from the People’s Guild had been continuously visiting.

“Guild Master, nice to see you again.”

A middle-aged man with a greasy face entered the guild master’s office with a slick smile.

Zhang Shuzhong.

When Seong Jihan obtained the Phoenix Flag, he was one of the top officials of the People’s Guild who competed in sponsorship with Robert Gates.

An annoying guy pretending to be a fan.

They leave whenever they want, then try to naturally slither back in once the situation changes due to the Chaos Battlefield.

So the People’s Guild is that financially affluent.

No apology whatsoever, just arrogantly demanding they make room as if money could solve everything.

Lee Hayeon found the People’s Guild’s attitude preposterous.

“Neighbors can have minor quarrels for a bit. But since we’ll keep seeing each other, ultimately being on good terms benefits both sides, no? Even specifying which rental player’s contracts to terminate.

Lee Hayeon sighed and looked at Lim Gayeong.

“I’m afraid that offer is unacceptable. Gayeong, please escort him out.”

“This way, please.”

“Hehe… What a waste. It’s a chance for mutual gain. How regrettable, how unfortunate. I will visit again tomorrow.”

Zhang Shuzhong left the Guild Master’s Office still smiling slyly.

Not long after, Lim Gayeong came back inside.

“Was there any trouble?”

“He asked if I have a boyfriend. He said he has three hotels in downtown Seoul and asked if I wanted to stay with him. Nonsense like that.”

“Ugh, that crazy guy. I don’t know why he speaks Korean so well.”

“His ex-wife was Korean.”

“No, he had one child with her.”

How many wives did he have then? Lee Hayeon said with frustration, “I don’t want to meet that guy again tomorrow… Shall we go on a business trip?”

“I strongly agree.”

“Let’s finalize the list of 20 members and then go.”

Seong Jihan was selecting 20 members to take to the chaotic battlefield. Originally, the list included Wang Lin’s name. Regardless of nationality, he selected players with the highest growth potential. Besides Wang Lin, there were a few more players selected by Seong Jihan who were not from the Daegi guild.

‘I should have just chosen according to the list given at that time…’

Lee Hayeon regretted suggesting prioritizing guild members to give them merit. However, regret started to creep in when Zhang Shuzhong visited the guild again.

‘No, even so, he came this far… I should stick to choosing guild members until the end!’

Encouraged by the interest from the People’s Guild, Lee Hayeon confirmed the list of guild members that day. Due to the limit on Daegi guild members, the list ended with 10 Platinum-level talents.

“Boss, the guild member list is confirmed!”

“Yes, I’ll come to see it.”

Seong Jihan nodded as he looked at the confirmed list provided by Lee Hayeon.

“Is this our guild member list?”

“Yes, please select the remaining 10 from the list you provided. Shouldn’t it be easy to choose by deducing from that?”

“That’s right.”

The list included 10 individuals with talents ranging from SSS to SS gifts. It seemed colorful, probably because promising individuals from all over the world were sent to the Daegi guild. Now, with 10 more members to select from the remaining talents, they had to consider wisely.

“Should I choose based on gifts?” Seong Jihan took out a pen and started circling names. Hearing this, Lee Hayeon cautiously spoke up.

“Boss… Are you planning to choose Wang Lin?”

“Yes, I thought of choosing based on gifts. It’s better to take talent with us.”

“Ah, I see…”

“Is there a problem?”

“Oh, no. Boss, can I take a few days off then?”

“Vacation?”

“Yes, not going far, just resting at my house in Sword Palace.”

Lee Hayeon, who hadn’t taken a break while coming to the guild every day, asking for a vacation was surprising. Seeing Seong Jihan’s curious eyes, Lim Gayeong supplemented the explanation.

“Lately, a persistent middle-aged man kept assigning tasks to her. She seems to need some rest.”

“Middle-aged man? Who is that?”

“The People’s Guild’s Zhang Shuzhong.”

“People came from the People’s Guild?”

“Yes, he’s asking to join the guild again, saying they want their people back.”

After arrogantly leaving when they demanded he divulge the origins of his martial arts, now they’re asking to rejoin the guild to participate in the Chaos Battlefield?

Seong Jihan chuckled.

“Did they even apologize to me or the guild side?”

“Apologize? What apology? They just confidently told me to kick out other guild members and make space for them, offering more money. It seems like they believe money can solve everything.”

Thinking everything can be solved with money without the slightest apology.

‘That won’t do.’

He was just going to pick by ability but if they’re gonna act like that, he has no choice.

Seong Jihan originally intended to select based on abilities, but if they insist, there’s no choice.

“Don’t accept even if they offer 10 times more money.”

“Yes!”

“Yes, from now on, I’ll do just that!”

After Seong Jihan regulated them like so, Lee Hayeon’s eyes lit up.

As expected, he didn’t care one bit about them being China’s top guild.

She had to follow that stance.

“Well, then I guess I don’t need a vacation!”

“You should take a vacation. People need to rest a bit.”

“No, I want to kick him out myself. Hehe….”

Having endured a lot of stress from Zhang Shuzhong, Lee Hayeon smiled. And the next day,

“G-Guild Master! What is the meaning of this? Chasing away the representative of the People’s Guild who came…!”

“The list has been announced. Don’t come again.”

“H-How dare a mere Korean guild disregard the People’s Guild’s offer like this?!”

“Yes, we’re disregarding it.”

Aside from Lee Gayeong, Lee Hayeon also brought several bodyguards.

She promptly ordered Zhang Shuzhong to be removed from the premises.

“Especially from someone like you, Zhang Shuzhong, I have no thoughts of negotiating. How many times do you have to ogle someone? You even sexually harassed Gayeong.”

“Cough…! When did I ever do that…!”

He coughed awkwardly, looking around at the mention of sexual harassment.

The burly bodyguards were all glaring at him.

They didn’t look inclined to negotiate at all.

“…You will regret this.”

Unable to do anything, he could only spit out that one line before leaving the Daegi guild.

“Phew, that middle-aged man finally left.”

“He said we’ll regret it. What do you think he’ll try?”

“Don’t know, let him try whatever.”

Returning to the Guild Master’s Office, Lee Hayeon smiled refreshingly as she swiftly scrolled her mouse wheel.

“As the Boss said, we’re the boss here, right?”

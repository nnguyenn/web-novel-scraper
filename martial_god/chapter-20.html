Chapter 20

“50 billion.”

With those words, the excitement in the auction hall cooled down as if cold water had been poured over it.

“That Battle Net Connector might go for around 20 billion if it’s brand new.”

“If he were the former Sword King, there would probably be a premium on the item. But now, it’s basically just secondhand.”

“Even though the data is somewhat tempting… Is it really worth 500 million? Huh… This is unbelievable.”

“The auction seems pointless.”

The moment Takeda Kazuo of Japan’s Neo Self-Defense Force mentioned 500 million, the auction might as well have already ended.

“How will you proceed?”

When the auctioneer asked Seong Jihan, he stood up and walked forward with determined steps.

“If there’s something you want to discuss, let’s do it here.”

“I’m sorry, but the conditions for 50 billion yen include exclusive rights…”

Takeda Kazuo lowered his head excessively and folded his hands, but his demeanor contradicted his words.

Despite his attitude, the underlying meaning was clear…‘If you want the 500 million, a private conversation is a must.’

The slight lift of the corner of his mouth seemed to say, ‘You won’t back down from this proposal, will you?’

Watching this, Seong Jihan smirked.

“Then I’ll have to decline.”

“Haha, yes, Of course, Let’s go and have a private meeting … Wait?”

Takeda Kazuo, who was rubbing his hands, stopped in surprise and straightened up.

He seemed confused, wondering if he had misheard, then asked hesitantly while bowing his head.

“Um… I’m sorry, could you please repeat that? I’m not very familiar with Korean, so I might have misheard…”

Bowing again, he asked for an answer.

“If a private conversation is among the conditions, we won’t take that money.”

“Mr. Seong Jihan, I’m sorry, but this money isn’t for you. It’s an amount that will be given to Yoon Seah, the daughter of the Sword King.”

‘Why are you stepping in when this 500 billion isn’t yours in the first place?’

The smile disappeared from Takeda’s face.

“Isn’t that right, Ms. Yoon Seah?”

“…”

“50 billion yen… For someone like me, a mere salaryman, it’s an amount that I couldn’t earn even if I saved every penny for a lifetime. I don’t know what your uncle is thinking, but please judge it rationally as the one who will actually receive the money. Can you really give up on this money just based on your uncle’s words?”

50 billion is an astronomical amount, as Takeda said.

Even the viewers watching the auction progress on Seong Jihan’s channel could somewhat empathize with his words.

– It’s too much money to refuse…

– Is today’s broadcast already about to end?

– That seems to be the case. After all, it’s his niece who will receive the money. Would she really just stand by and watch his uncle squirm?

-Are you crazy? How could you say that after he sent that bald Japanese guy?

“I trust Uncle. So please talk to him.”

“Huuh… Is that so?”

Takeda sighed and then straightened his previously hunched back.

He had attempted to shake things up through Yoon Seah, but it only solidified their already pronounced connection.

– Did the manipulation fail?

– Tsk tsk, she’s young. Once she experiences social life, she would haggle even if it were 100 million, not 50 billion.

– She’s the Sword King’s daughter.

“Since Seah is also saying this, if you have something to say, please say it here instead of having a private conversation”

“Well, well… Why are you so insistent on refusing a private conversation? I’m saying this because I’m being considerate of your position.”

“It’s fine.”

Talking privately with the Neo Self-Defense Force recruitment manager for 500 million? That wasn’t a good image. He had no idea what people might say. They might criticize the fact that the father sold out for a woman and the daughter for money.

Of course, the criticism could be endured to some extent considering the astronomical amount of 500 million.

500 million.

To most people, it’s an astronomical amount of money.

However, for the Sword King, who ranked 3rd in the world rankings and 1st in the Warrior rankings, it wasn’t actually a large sum.

In fact, he probably earned more money than that in a month.

‘But he’s cutting the parent-child bond with this?’

At least he said ‘sorry’ to his daughter before he left Korea, but that had changed to him proudly saying he would sever their bond.

“Leave it. I’ll take care of it.”

Takeda Kazuo, the Recruitment Manager of the Neo Self-Defense Force…

“Please contact us. We’ll give you the best treatment we can offer! I promise, as the Recruitment manager of the Neo Self-Defense Force!”

“…”

Since the real world had many more restrictions than the game, it was a strength beyond a Bronze league player.

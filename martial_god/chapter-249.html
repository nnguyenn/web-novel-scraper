Chapter 249

Inside the Void Training Room,

‘Time’s almost up already.’

Seong Jihan was cognizant of the passing time and, with a hint of regret, set down his sword.

‘In such a short span, I’ve gained much.’

Facing Dongbang Sak in two matches during the Taiwan series, Seong Jihan acquired an immeasurable wealth of experience.

Dongbang Sak had dominated Seong Jihan with certainty.

The martial arts displayed by him had provided a foundation for Seong Jihan’s growth.

‘I may not yet grasp the Taiji Sword technique used by Dongbang Sak…’

The white sword, the Taiji Sword, summoned by Dongbang Sak—Seong Jihan had yet to understand the principle from which its overwhelming darkness emerged, martial soul notwithstanding.

Even without comprehending this, experiencing Dongbang Sak’s martial arts had led to significant advancements for Seong Jihan.

His proficiency in martial arts increased dramatically through the martial soul, partly incorporating elements of the Heavenly Demon’s martial arts into his own repertoire.

‘The martial soul’s stat itself has increased too.’

After reaching 200, the martial soul had been creeping up bit by bit.

Even when Seong Jihan managed to acquire the Fourth annihilating technique, the Ice Heaven Sword Rain, martial soul lingered in the 230s.

But since the matches against Dongbang Sak, his martial soul has touched 250.

Moreover, as he reiterated that battle in the training hall, his martial soul rose incrementally, now reaching 255.

‘Not to mention, I’ve started to unravel the Fifth Annihilation Technique, Heavenly Tree Descending Spirit.’

The wood-affiliated martial arts of Heavenly Tree Descending Spirit, deduced to be related to the elves’ regenerative abilities. For Seong Jihan, the fight with Dongbang Sak wasn’t just about level gains—it came with considerable wisdom.

Only a minor regret persisted.

‘I didn’t get to fully witness Nine Halls Eight Trigrams.’

The formation Nine Halls Eight Trigrams, which Dongbang Sak followed after the second annihilating technique, Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods, was cut short.

Just as Nine Halls and Eight Trigrams transcended towards Seong Jihan, the Wandering Martial God hastily intervened and whisked him away.

What effect would have manifested had the Seal Code come into full effect?

‘It’s suspicious that the Martial God would intervene right when Dongbang Sak’s formation was about to manifest.’

Did the Martial God not want those special characters to appear again?

During the incident with Longinus’s Spear of Judgment, some Destruction Code showed up but the Martial God never intervened.

Seong Jihan pondered in the Void Training Room, analyzing the Nine Halls Eight Trigrams Formation, but with too little information at hand.

The abrupt end before Dongbang Sak could properly demonstrate it offered no further clues.

He decided to put aside his concerns about the Nine Halls Eight Trigrams Formation for now, shifting focus to other annihilating techniques.

‘I’m still unsure of the third’s operating principle… Could the fourth be some sort of detection code?’

Detection, sealing, destruction.

Seong Jihan sorted through keywords he could speculate so far, furrowing his brows.

Annihilating Techniques.

They were peculiar if referred to as martial arts.

Seong Jihan recalled Dongbang Sak’s words.

-In an era where humans and demons coexisted, a man was granted the mission to seal demons and gods, ushering in the age of man…

Dongbang Sak’s duty was to seal demons and gods.

The Annihilating Techniques seemed less like a unified body of martial arts and more a compilation of these duties and abilities.

-For Longinus, it was the killer of gods. For Pythia, it was a searcher of immortals…

Based on the three revealed, the pattern looked clear.

Finding ‘immortals’ that would register as bugs in the BattleNet, to seal and destroy them.

Were Annihilating Techniques inherent powers to locate and eliminate immortality, as their name implied?

Yet,

‘The last Annihilating Technique. Heavenly Tree Descending Spirit deviates from this pattern. Infinite regenerative ability exhibited by this power could be a potential target of the Annihilating Techniques themselves….’

Instinctively, Seong Jihan felt it.

Heavenly Tree Descending Spirit,

This ability wasn’t just about regenerative power akin to that of elves.

The enlightenment gained through being cleaved by Dongbang Sak was just a fraction of Heavenly Tree Descending Spirit’s power.

Its true nature remained cloaked in mystery.

‘If only I knew what Gilgamesh’s power was…’

Gilgamesh, suspected by Pythia of being the wandering Martial God.

There was not even a small hint regarding his power.

‘…Dwelling on this unsolvable issue serves no purpose now. Let’s depart.’

Seong Jihan concluded his thoughts and stepped out of the Void Training Room.

Ahead lay a two-week series of matches, notoriously dubbed by the Korean team as a ‘race through hell.’

The next day was slated for the second match, against China.

The match between Korea and Taiwan.

A game predicted to end one-sidedly, still created ripples among global BattleNet fans.

Especially,

And the constellation who had pressed Dongbang Sak employed the Taiji and Heavenly Demon’s martial arts.

Was this the end of the special tutoring from the Martial God’s disciples?

Korea’s team strength, even without Seong Jihan, had ascended to the ranks of the powerful, reinforced by father-daughter duo Yoon Sejin and Yoon Seah.

The strategy to secure a definite victory in the fourth game with Golem Duel banked on ensuring two victories in the first three matches.

Even without Seong Jihan, the likelihood of defeating a Korean team featuring Yoon Sejin and Yoon Seah was slim.

So, Japan had resorted to a strategy of relying on luck each game, picking the Golem Duel map as a possible equalizer.

‘But China must be confident they can win without me.’

Unlike Russia or Japan, China, boasting several SSS-tier players, felt confident they could defeat Korea even with the presence of Yoon Sejin and Yoon Seah, providing Seong Jihan was absent.

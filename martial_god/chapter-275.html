Chapter 275

Somewhere in the universe.

The red lion shuddered as it gazed at its pursuers.

Among the infamous disciples of the Martial God in the universe,

The most notorious, Dongbang Sak stroked his beard as he looked down on him.

“Thunder God. Your power has been significantly diminished. What’s the source of that red lightning?”

[…Do you think I’d tell you?]

The red lion growled defiantly in response to Dongbang Sak’s pressing questions.

“Dongbang Sak, just kill him. He’s merely a fragment.”

“Is that so?”

[Wait, wait…]

Swoosh!

“Yes, just now… Seong Jihan pulled out the chains and obliterated it alongside the Ancient Elf with a massive explosion.”

“What are you saying? He happened to be in my scanning range during the mission. The Thunder god who had that red lightning must have gone to him.”

[What? What did you just say…?]

“Brahma. Wouldn’t it be better to just die without knowing? Disappear.”

[No. Wait…!]

“So persistent.”

Flash!

With a motion of Dongbang Sak’s sword,

The body of Brahma was shredded, discharging electric currents into the void before vanishing.

“Hmm. A failed mission, huh?”

“There’s nothing we could do. Until the Thunder God bearing the Crimson Thunder emerged from Seong Jihan’s body, we couldn’t detect him.”

“What about the other one?”

“Seong Jihan? He used the authority of Gilgamesh to eliminate both the Thunder God and Ancient Elf.”

“Hmm. I am curious. How does he know about all our abilities…”

“That’s why. Do we have any information on Martial Soul”

As the two marveled at Seong Jihan, who had handled the fifth technique, Gilgamesh’s authority,

[The Thunder God has been annihilated. Now, return.]

A deep male voice resounded over them.

They looked at each other, eyes widening with shock.

This voice is…

”…Gilgamesh?“

“Could he have awakened from the use of the authority?”

[Yes.]

“What about the master?”

[He’s gone.]

Pythia’s eyes sparked with realization upon hearing Gilgamesh’s reply.

It was the same as before.

When Gilgamesh awoke, the Martial God vanished.

There was never a moment when both of them existed together.

‘Gilgamesh and the Martial God… are they really the same person? Or…’

Pythia pondered this possibility when,

[The one who woke me. Is it Seong Jihan? I have something to say regarding him. So, return first.]

Gilgamesh urged their return.

“Understood.”

“We’ll head there immediately.”

Flash!

Both their figures vanished.

Zzzzt…

The spot where Brahma vanished briefly crackled with electricity before falling silent.

With remaining achievement points saved for later,

‘Martial God and Gilgamesh… are they really different people?’

“However, the master’s command is only directed at Longinus… Don’t interfere with this matter.”

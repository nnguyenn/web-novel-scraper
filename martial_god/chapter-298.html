Chapter 298

The pure white gauntlet that came out of Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods.

At a glance, it was exuding an intense energy.

‘There’s no item description or anything.’

Perhaps because it wasn’t classified as an item in BattleNet, there was no particular explanation.

‘Giving it to others seems risky, should I try equipping it?’

The constellation fragment that had threatened Seong Jihan quite a bit, Since it came from that being, there was a risk in giving this object to other humans.

Seong Jihan brought his hand to the pure white gauntlet.

It was an object whose size didn’t match a human hand, but…

Swooosh…

As Seong Jihan inserted his hand, it changed size to fit his hand and was sucked into him.

And then,

[Martial God… If I had this, could I have beaten him?]

A scene unfolded in Seong Jihan’s mind.

The opponent he had just fought, with a head and 3 hands.

The process and experience of that being ruminating on the battle it had with the Martial God, researching how it could have blocked his strike.

It was indirectly experienced by Seong Jihan as well.

‘This… is really completely different from humans.’

The original form of the constellation that fought Seong Jihan was an alien race with only an upper body and 6 arms attached.

The way it handled its energy was on a different level from humans.

Just as humans cannot understand how to operate wings they don’t have, The power usage method of a constellation with 6 arms was also something Seong Jihan couldn’t relate to at all.

[The characteristic of the Heterogeneous Martial Resolve class is activated.]

The Heterogeneous Martial Resolve that he had changed to before but never had a chance to use was activated here.

[The authority of an extraterrestial race is researched and absorbed in a way suitable for your species.]

[It is the authority of a constellation. You understand it only fragmentarily.]

The incomprehensible power operation of six hands moving.

However, the Heterogeneous Martial Resolve made Seong Jihan understand this, even if only fragmentarily, in a way suitable for him.

[Void increases by 5.]

[Martial Soul increases by 5.]

Furthermore, while absorbing the power inside the gauntlet to fit his body, it raised the two stats by 5 each.

‘This is quite a nice bonus.’

Not only did his stats increase, but adapting the experience of using six arms to fit human combat was also a significant help. Although he hadn’t fully absorbed this experience yet, if he could make it his own, it would undoubtedly be quite helpful

Seong Jihan thought while looking at his hand where the gauntlet had disappeared.

‘When I used Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods on the Succubus Queen’s avatar before, there was no such effect… Is it because the opponent was constellation-grade? Then the constellation artifacts in Tuseong might have all been made this way too.’

But then why was the Martial God just displaying those many constellation artifacts like stars in the sky?

Seong Jihan had that question, but…

[You survived the longest in the final round of the Northern Wall.]

[Logging out from the Northern Wall to calculate the rewards for the entire guild.]

As he was logged out of the game due to the end of the guild mission, he stopped thinking about this for now.

Right now, it was time to organize the rewards from the end of the guild mission.

* * *

Special Defense Map, the Northern Wall.

Even though Seong Jihan could have chosen an individual mission here, he proceeded with the guild mission to aim for additional guild rewards.

At first, the gap between the human rankers and Seong Jihan was so huge that it was doubtful if the mission could proceed smoothly.

‘Octane’s information was useful.’

Thanks to the ‘Ability Sharing’ information provided by Lue Investment’s Octane, The Daegi Guild was able to reach the final round without much difficulty.

And,

[You’ve earned 10 guild points.]

[You’ve obtained the guild-exclusive title, ‘Defender of the Northern Wall’.]

Although Seong Jihan couldn’t continuously bring buffs from the Resting Place of the Dead, Perhaps because the entire team survived for quite a while thanks to Ability Sharing, humanity took 1st place on the map.

In addition to earning 10 points, the Daegi Guild also gained a guild title.

[Defender of the Northern Wall]

– A title given to the guild that places 1st on the Northern Wall map.


– Raises the level of all guild options by +5.

Guild points that were extremely difficult to obtain normally.

The guild mission supplemented this in one go and these rewards weren’t just applied to the Daegi Guild.

“Wow, Uncle. I got +5 residual stats from participating this time!”

“Oh, really?”

“Yeah. Other guild members got at least +3 as well. Plus, we gained a ton of experience points and leveled up… Everyone seems happy that they participated.”

Lee Hayeon, the guild master who had recruited rankers for the guild mission and received a certain amount of GP.

She had agreed in the contract to receive additional GP if the rewards from the guild mission turned out to be more valuable than expected.

“Is this enough to receive more money?”

“Of course! Do you know how hard it is to get residual stats!”

“Even though it’s only +3 to +5?”

“That’s huge… Boss.”

“Uncle… That remark is like a chaebol not knowing bus fare.”

Yoon Seah glared at Seong Jihan and asked, “Then what rewards did Uncle get from the mission?”

“First, it’s focused on guild rewards. As for individual rewards…”

[You survived until the end in the guild mission.]

“Level +10, residual points +10. It’s mediocre.”

“Well, I have been getting rewards from missions so far.”

Every time he did a mission, an epic quest would pop up and give decent rewards.

But this time, perhaps because that didn’t happen, he only received double the rewards compared to other players.

‘Guess I’ll raise my red stat with the residual points.’

Seong Jihan dumped the residual points he received this time into the red stat, which had the lowest value.

But when he invested 20, the red stat only increased by 1.

Considering the point investment before, the unranked Red stat had a tremendously low investment efficiency.

‘It eats up a lot of points.’

“The World Champions League.”

In the past, the Champions League of BattleNet, the only sport for humanity, was a worldwide festival.

But now, the Space League matches were taking its place in terms of topic.

After all, the Champions League was about establishing a hierarchy between countries.

Whereas the Space League had the fate of all humanity riding on the outcome of each match.

The group drawing details that would have been featured in all media in the past.

Ever since gaining the power of life force, Seong Jihan’s body had changed to the point where he didn’t need to eat anymore.

“Then you’ll continue starving in the training room? Aren’t you hungry now?”

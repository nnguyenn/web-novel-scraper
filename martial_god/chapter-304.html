Chapter 304

“The Tower of Babel?”

Although Seong Jihan didn’t follow any religion,

He was well-aware of the Tower of Babel.

A tower built to reach heaven but, in the end, crumbled due to God’s wrath. Furthermore, the myth depicted how it confused humanity’s unified language into a babel of diverse tongues.

And now, to think Gilgamesh’s abode was this “Tower of Babel.”

“This tower… it’s said to become increasingly dangerous the higher you go. Even though we’ve entered through BattleNet connectors as avatars, my constellation has advised once more that logging out might be wise.”

“Hmm.”

Crunch!

Chewing on the golden fruit, Seong Jihan pondered.

The unsettling Tower of Babel.

Considering safety, logging out would be the right decision.

Even if he didn’t become a candidate for the constellation this time, there wasn’t much left until he could ascend.

But,

“I should see how high I can climb.”

Seong Jihan became intrigued by Gilgamesh’s Tower of Babel that offered golden fruits.

“Let’s go a bit higher. If it gets dangerous, both of you log out immediately.”

“Hmm… I guess hanging around won’t help Uncle much, right? Alright, I’ll scoot when it’s time.”

“Me too. And if my constellation sends any more messages, I’ll let you know right away.”

“But Uncle, shouldn’t you avoid eating those fruits?”

“Ah, I can purify myself, so it’s okay for me.”

“Oh, Then can I eat too?”

“No, you can’t.”

“Tch, they do look tasty.”

Yoon Seah watched enviously as Seong Jihan continued to consume the fruits.

The golden fruits appeared more delicious than any other food in the world.

If it weren’t for her mother’s strict advice, she might have already rushed towards them.

‘With this level of vitality, it’s about 1/5th of the World Tree’s fruit.’

And the ‘secret energy’ hidden within, also roughly 1/5th and definitely inferior to the golden fruit derived from the World Tree.

Seong Jihan deliberately chose not to purify that energy, scooping up as much fruit as he could.

“Ugh, so powerful… and he’s still hogging all of it…”

“Wow… they’re bad-mouthing Uncle. Seriously, what’s with them? Even if it raises stamina.”

“Exactly. How dare they act like that to Jihan?”

“…But Sophia, why are you closing your eyes?”

“It looks too delicious. I decided not to look.”

“I should do the same.”

As the two closed their eyes in resistance,

Seong Jihan smiled at their actions and began to eat the fruits more earnestly.

Crunch! Crunch!

Having swept almost half of the fruits dropped by the steel giant, The rest of the team’s gaze gradually fastened on him.

And then,

“Ah, please…!”

“Mr. Seong Jihan, could you please stop…?!”

Players, normally revered as sacrosanct beings,

-Wow, they dare to speak against Seong Jihan ㅡㅡ Ingrates.

[Move forward.]

The steel giant Enkidu cleared a path,

Revealing the stairs behind it.

Those stairs within the golden realm of the Tower of Babel were made of wood.

Seeing this,

“Ah… it’s over.”

“What a pity.”

“My stamina… increased by 3.”

“I got +5.”

“Wish we could have eaten more…”

The excitement quickly subdued as people regained their composure.

But that was momentary.

“Let’s head to the next floor then!”

“Who knows what treasures await us on the next level!”

“I will eat them all this time…!”

Most players rushed to climb to the next floor,

Forgetting they were part of the final match of the championship and belonged to a national team.

One person, however, didn’t get swept up in the frenzy.

The Sword King, Yoon Sejin, crossed his arms and turned to Seong Jihan.

“So, are you going up, Jihan?”

“I am.”

“A wise choice.”

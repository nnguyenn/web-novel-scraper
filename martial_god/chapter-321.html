Chapter 321

“I don’t necessarily believe in physiognomy, but…”

Yoon Sejin, the so-called “traitor’s face.”

That evaluation seemed a bit unfair to him.

It was true that he had followed Shizuru, who had the ability to “edit,” and that he had been ruled by the Constellation “King of the Humans.” But in a way, it was more like an accident.

In the first place, hadn’t Seong Jihan suggested that Yoon Sejin seek sponsorship from the King of the Humans, hoping that it would be good to have a sponsor constellation?

‘Still, even if it wasn’t intentional, there have been accidents several times.’

Yoon Sejin might just be unlucky, but the evaluation of the Shadow Queen, a constellation, was something he couldn’t just ignore.

‘It’s definitely better if I’m the constellation and take the lead.’

Seong Jihan was thinking this when…

“Uncle!”

Yoon Seah, who had gone out to show the human players to the Shadow Queen, came back in.

“The queen wants to sponsor Dad?”

“Wasn’t he called a traitor just now?”

“I’m not in a position to care about such things.”

As she said this, the Shadow Queen looked at Seong Jihan intently.

“But you. Did you do something just now?”

“Why?”

“My abilities suddenly surged, and several of the chains that were left on my body broke.”

Seong Jihan had an idea of what she was talking about.

The 200% boost to all stats when the constellation trait was turned on.

It looks like the Shadow Queen benefited from that too.

“Oh, I was just powered up for a bit.”

“Can’t you do a little more? If I can break these chains, I think I can be active as a constellation again…”

“Active as a constellation? Wasn’t that over?”

The Shadow Queen, whose Shadow Elves on the main planet were annihilated.

For her, who had the trait of Dominion, it was no different from receiving irreversible damage.

She nodded at Seong Jihan’s words, but…

“That’s true, but… our Shadow Elves are the only ones that World Tree Elves can continue to create. If the Elf type isn’t complete, they’ll still be scrapped. We need to unite those souls and make the Shadow Elves a force again. And I’ll get revenge on World Tree for making me like this.”

She was burning with a will for revenge.

“What about the main planet?”

“I have a planet in reserve in case something happens. Of course, before settling there, I need to put a decent number of Shadow Elves under my command. If I surprise attack one of the World Tree’s planets and liberate the Shadow Elves, I should have enough to settle on that planet.”

“Can you do that alone?”

“I may be in this state now, but I’m still a level 7 constellation in my own right. If I can just recover my strength, I may not be able to conquer a World Tree Elf planet, but I can surprise it and unite the souls.”

Level 7, huh.

So she maxed out the Dominion trait to level 7.

Considering that level 10 is a Great Constellation, level 7 is still a high rank among constellations.

The Shadow Queen was stronger than he had thought.

“But there is one obstacle that I must overcome for my revival.”

The Shadow Queen fiddled with the broken chains on her body.

“This Yggdrasil root that is still embedded in my body… If I don’t remove it, my location can be tracked at any time.”

“Shall I remove it for you?”

“No, I will overcome this on my own. If I rely on you too much, I won’t know how to deal with it if I get entangled in the roots again later.”

The Yggdrasil root that Seong Jihan’s power could deal with.

However, the Shadow Queen did not want to receive his help with this as well.

“If it doesn’t work, let me know.”

“Got it. Speaking of which, about the sponsorship thing just now…”

“Oh, for my brother-in-law? I’ll do that.”

The Shadow Queen’ eyes widened at Seong Jihan’s words.

“Aren’t you not a constellation either? And you’re only level 300 or so.”

“But I can sponsor five people.”

“No, but how can you sponsor a constellation if you’re not a constellation?”

“I can.”

“You want to make my brother-in-law as a constellation? Alright.”

Yoon Sejin, who had returned home, accepted Seong Jihan’s sponsorship offer without much hesitation.

“Dad is answering right away. He doesn’t even hesitate between the Shadow Queen and Uncle.”

From the brother-in-law’s point of view, accepting his brother-in-law as a constellation could be seen as a burden.

Yoon Sejin accepted the sponsorship offer as if it were natural.

“So do I have to show respect to the constellation from now on?”

“No, it’s not necessary between family. It would be awkward.”

Seong Jihan opened the Constellation window and opened the ‘Player Sponsorship’ section.

Perhaps because the Dominion trait was turned off, only five slots were active.

And at the top of the Player Sponsorship section was a section called [Investment Tendency].

“Investment Tendency? Queen, do you know what this is?”

“You really… you can sponsor as a constellation? This section determines how much a constellation will invest in a player. It is divided into five stages, from very active to very passive. At first, the investment tendency will be the same for all sponsored players.”

“Will it change later?”

“If you have a Dominion level of 3 or higher, you can adjust the investment tendency for each player individually.”

So until then, he had to sponsor all players with the same tendency.

The Shadow Queen, as a senior constellation, gave Seong Jihan some tips.

“Basically, the resources required for player sponsorship are GP and Constellation Fame points. Of these, Fame points, which are important for constellations, are not consumed much from the ‘Active’ investment stage onwards. It is better not to exceed the ‘Normal’ investment tendency unless you have a good reason. After all, the reason for sponsoring players is to secure Fame points.”

“So you spend Fame points to gain Fame?”

“Yes. Well-raised sponsored players will continuously increase your Fame points. That’s why many constellations are looking for promising players.”

As she said this, the Shadow Queen also commented on humanity.

“It seems that humanity is not getting much attention from constellations. The evaluation in the Battle net race report, which was not very good, played a part in that.”

“Battle.net report.”

Seong Jihan seemed to know what that was.

It was the report by the Battle.net manager that evaluated the possibility of remaining in the league at 3% and the investment value as zero.

This information, which Seong Jihan had made public, had been quite a topic of conversation in the past.

“But from my point of view, the report has actually increased the investment value of humanity. Because of the report, no decent constellations are paying attention to it.”

“I guess the report has a big impact.”

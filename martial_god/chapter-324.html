Chapter 324

In the heart of Gangnam, Seoul.

The streets, which would usually be teeming with people, were eerily empty.

And for good reason.

Boom! Boom!

Explosions roared above, with bright flashes lighting up the sky incessantly.

“Have all the people been evacuated?”

“Many have left the streets, but there are still quite a few who haven’t made it out of the buildings. However…”

The police officer glanced nervously upward as he continued.

“Are you saying that Seong Jihan and a Constellation are really fighting up there?”

“Hmm… The Sword King said so. Isn’t that why he’s still busy evacuating people?”

“To think this wasn’t just a BattleNet affair but happening for real…”

“What’s so surprising about that? It’s a world where dungeon portals appear.”

Since Seong Jihan had left his home, a huge explosion had occurred in the sky.

Yoon Sejin and Yoon Seah had already sought cooperation from the government, explaining the situation and aiming to aid in evacuations.

Together with those efforts, the father-daughter duo were actively helping people escape.

“Oh, oh…! Flying by sword! I can’t believe I’m actually riding one!”

“Hold onto the sword tight, and don’t try to take selfies. You might fall.”

“Yes, yes!”

Police officers watched enviously as ordinary citizens descended from tall buildings on swords.

Sword King Yoon Sejin’s flying technique.

Something they had only seen in BattleNet matches was now unfolding before their eyes.

“I’d like to try riding one myself.”

“Stop talking nonsense and finish evacuating the citizens. We never know when something might fall from the sky.”

“Understood! But… uh…”

The subordinate officer, following the command, pointed shakily at the sky.

“Look, up there! A massive object is falling!”

“What? What?!”

“Wow… Getting to ride a sword in my lifetime.”

“Being unable to evacuate turned out to be a blessing in disguise.”

“Oh, I got it on video!”

“This isn’t the time for leisure. Please, evacuate immediately!”

While police officers quickly managed to evacuate those who had descended from the swords, Yoon Sejin found himself thinking strangely.

‘Why does my body feel like this all of a sudden? I’ve become stronger.’

Swoosh!

He flew towards the red boulder faster than usual.

‘Is it because I accepted my brother-in-law as my sponsor Constellation? But, I didn’t feel any different until just now…’

Sword King, having his sponsor as the Constellation Seong Jihan.

However, as his powers as a Constellation were limited, the practical benefits for Sword King were negligible.

Hence, Yoon Sejin didn’t so much expect sponsorship benefits from Seong Jihan as he welcomed it as a gesture to avoid falling under another’s influence again.

‘I’ve become significantly stronger, beyond just support buffs. If I add buffs on top of this…!’

Yoon Sejin’s eyes sparkled.

With this newfound strength, he felt he could fight on equal footing with the World Tree Elves that had overwhelmed him.

As he recalled his fights with the Elves, the meteor came into his view.

“First, I’ll deal with that…!”

Intense sword energy surged from Yoon Sejin’s twin blades.

It forcefully stretched forth, striking the red meteor.

However,

Clang!

‘It’s tough…!’

Just as Yoon Sejin prepared for another strike.

“Brother-in-law?”

Yoon Sejin’s sword energy.

It was noticeably different from the last time he saw it.

“After you left, I suddenly felt a surge of energy and realized I’ve become more powerful. It feels like I’m not even utilizing my full strength.”

“Aha. It seems like I amplified the power as a Constellation. That might be the cause.”

“Yes. But the effect won’t last long since I won’t be an official Constellation until later. So, until then, these effects will be more short-term.”

“Well, if it’s my brother-in-law, you’ll become a Constellation in no time. It’s an honor to be your first sponsored player.”

Clang!

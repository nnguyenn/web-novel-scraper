“Release my sister… What exactly do you want?”

[May I enter and have a conversation?]

Upon Seong Jihan’s nod,

Swoosh.

The Arena’s Master soon revealed himself before him.

The figure before him was dressed in a neat suit and wearing a black fedora. Like the last time, his face still bore the semblance of the cosmos.

For Seong Jihan, now more powerful than before, he was an opponent whose limits he dared not gauge.

‘Longinus pales in comparison.’

Even when compared to Longinus, the Constellation he had just defeated, the Arena’s Master boasted a strength of a different class.

Could this level of power be considered that of a Great Constellation?

As Seong Jihan assessed his opponent, the Arena’s Master spoke up.

“I observed your recent battle with the Constellation. You skillfully utilized the Void Processing Facility of the World Tree Alliance.”

“You saw the fight?”

“Yes. Because you used the Processing Facility, I was able to observe.”

Swoosh…

With that, the white half-mask emerged.

The Arena’s Master tapped on the mask covering half of his cosmic face.

“The Void Processing Facility is an invention by the World Tree Alliance, by extension, Yggdrasil, designed to evade death. It has proven quite effective, giving us quite the trouble in monitoring the World Tree Alliance from the void.”

“The Processing Facility is that significant?”

“Yes. While we have our countermeasures, the World Tree Alliance keeps advancing the Processing Facility. Especially, the recently developed Processing Facilities, which haven’t leaked outside, posed quite a challenge for us… until you acquired one this time.”

Upon hearing that, Seong Jihan retrieved the half-mask from his inventory.

“So, this is what the trade is about.”

“Exactly. There’s more.”

Swoosh!

The Arena’s Master’s gaze shifted towards the spear shaft.

“If you hand over the ‘Code Medium’ as well, I will release the Witch of the Void. In her original human form, untainted by the void.”

“Code Medium?”

“Yes.”

“The system labels it as the Spear of the Blood Clan. But you call it differently.”

“The Spear of the Blood Clan, hmm? Gilgamesh named it so…”

Flash!

The eyes, resembling stars within the cosmos, briefly turned a shade of violet.

And then, a terrifying void swelled from within.

Merely a change of eye color, yet the Arena’s Master emanated an overwhelming power.

[The Arena’s Master… When I heard rumors of him possessing strength comparable to a Great Constellation, I thought them far-fetched. Turns out, the rumors were true.]

The Shadow Queen assessed the Arena’s Master’s power as that of a Great Constellation level.

‘BattleNet sure is full of formidable foes.’

Seong Jihan thought and opened his mouth.

“Enough with the power display. What exactly happened in the past that made you hand over this medium to Gilgamesh?”

“I can’t delve into details… but since you are the current owner of the medium, you aren’t exactly an outsider. I will share what I can.”

Swoosh!

The face of the Arena’s Master transformed, displaying a screen.

There appeared planet Earth.

“Planet Earth is brimming with the potential for life to sprout. Such places often witness the World Tree Alliance planting World Trees. Earth was one such case.”

“I’ve heard something similar from the Ancient Elf. The experiment chief sent to Earth by the World Tree Elves tried to fuse himself with Earth’s World Tree, causing an accident.”

The Ancient Elf, who couldn’t enter as it was an experiment-in-progress, and the branch manager Seong Jihan met after his death shared details about that time.

Infuriated by the incident with the World Tree, the Alliance’s investigative team dispatched to Earth vanished after only leaving a message about a special error occurrence.

“Ah… So on that side, do they consider it an accident?”

Hearing Seong Jihan’s tale, the Arena’s Master,

“We deemed it a fully intentional act by the Alliance.”

“The World Tree Alliance did it on purpose?”

“Yes. However, when the experiment resulted in an uncontrollable entity, they sought our assistance.”

The screen shifted.

Displayed within was a giant, with tree and body bizarrely merged.

“This entity. We referred to them as the Sylvan Gods.”

“Sylvan Gods…”

“Yes. They were beings deserving restriction by the void. Yet, they cunningly evaded our regulations, knowing our internal policies all too well. Direct intervention from our end was impossible. So, we decided to indirectly intervene through a proxy.”

“A proxy… Don’t tell me, it was Gilgamesh?”

“Yes.”

Swoosh.

The image of the Sylvan Gods vanished, replaced by Gilgamesh’s visage.

“He was bestowed with blessings from both the void and the World Tree Alliance and waged a long war against the Sylvan Gods. The Code Medium you possess, is a gift I gave him… it was the only means to completely annihilate the regenerative Sylvan Gods. And once the task was completed, it was supposed to be returned to me.”

“But he didn’t return it.”

“Yes. Gilgamesh broke not only the promise with me but with the World Tree Alliance as well. And then, the Martial God appeared…”

Whoosh. Whoosh.

Seong Jihan twirled the Spear of the Blood Clan in his hand.

The Code Medium that allowed for the casting of codes from a distance.

‘It’s too good to give back.’

Writing codes stitch by stitch up close versus launching them from afar were worlds apart in terms of utility.

If his sister wasn’t involved in the trade, he would never part with it.

But

‘If it’s about my sister, I have no choice.’

Family comes before anything, even valuable items.

“Alright. Let’s make the trade.”

“A wise decision.”

“But first, you return my sister.”

“Me first… is that your stance?”

“That’s right. If you take the items and disappear, I can’t do anything, can I?”

“I am ‘the Arena’s Master’. I swear on the Arena that I won’t do such a thing.”

“Forget the oath. Just return my sister first.”

“……”

The Arena’s Master fell silent at Seong Jihan’s words.

The Shadow Queen whispered to Seong Jihan.

‘This is quite useful.’

The utility of the Destruction Code could be vastly expanded this way.

Seong Jihan glanced back at the unstable portal.

While the best outcome would be his sister’s return, having tested the spear stirred a desire to keep it.

‘But my sister is more important.’

Seong Jihan stored the Spear of the Blood Clan in his inventory.

The more he used it, the more it seemed his greed for it would grow.

“I tore him apart 999 times, but he kept reviving. At one point, he even threatened to kill the witch.”

“I don’t know. Even when I offered a promotion, he was unyielding… However, he made a suggestion.”

“If you defeat him, he will hand over the Witch of the Void.”

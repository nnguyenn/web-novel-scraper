“Sponsorship, huh? As a Constellation, you wish to sponsor me?”

“It’s not exactly a Constellation sponsorship. Instead…”

Swoosh.

The Arena’s Master conjured something from the void.

Sparkle!

Unfurling his hand, a card shimmering with rainbow hues was revealed – an item that looked extraordinary at a glance.

[That, that’s… a VIP Membership?]

The Shadow Queen perched on Seong Jihan’s arm exclaimed in shock upon seeing it.

‘A VIP Membership?’

[Yes… It’s an item that’s a level above the Space League Premium Membership I’ve only seen around in BattleNet communities, never in person.]

An item that surpasses the premium membership.

Seong Jihan recalled the effects of his own premium membership.

Basically, during the membership period, a 100% increase in experience and GP acquisition, along with the ‘Hideout’ feature concealing his information and a 30% chance of being excluded from the opposing team’s ban list.

‘But I’ve hardly ever felt the 30% ban exclusion, so it’s practically only those two features that were of any use.’

The Hideout feature had been handy for concealing his identity.

However, ever since becoming Earth’s number one, its utility wasn’t as significant as before, with the membership becoming less useful.

Seong Jihan skeptically addressed the Shadow Queen.

‘It’s just a membership card, is it really worth getting this excited about?’

[Ha. “Just a membership card” you say! This is an item even the high-level Constellations struggle to obtain. Everyone desires it but nobody knows how to acquire it. And now, the Arena’s Master is offering it…]

‘Really? The premium membership has been less useful lately, anyway.’

[The VIP Membership is incomparable to the premium one.]

As the Shadow Queen asserted, the Arena’s Master tightly gripped the card.

Then,

Swoosh…

From the card shining with rainbow light, void energy emanated, swirling with violet smoke around it.

“I’ll offer you this VIP Membership Card with ‘Void Authentication.’”

“Void Authentication?”

“Take a look.”

As Seong Jihan received the card, the item description appeared.

[Space League VIP Membership Card (Void Authenticated)]

– Grade: EX

– Certified by ‘The Owner of the Arena’ as a VIP membership card.

– Holding this card increases the acquisition of experience and GP by 500%.

– Enhanced ‘Spotlight’ and ‘Hideout’ features are applied to the player, allowing for a choice between them.

– Holding this VIP membership includes a 50% chance of being excluded from the opposing team’s ban list.

[Spotlight]

– Announces the player’s premium membership across the BattleNet. It attracts the attention of many Constellations, allowing for sponsorship from across the cosmos.

– Being a VIP member intensifies the effects.

– Ads about the player will randomly appear on the Alien Species 0 channel, as the subscriber count of alien species on BattleTube is increased, it will boost cosmic fame and Constellation fame.

– With BattleTube active, achieving a certain comprehensive viewer count activates the ‘Star’ buff. The Star Buff intensifies as the comprehensive viewer count rises.

‘…The ability is strengthened based on the comprehensive viewer count?’

While Seong Jihan had skipped over the Spotlight feature when he had the premium membership, the Spotlight feature of the VIP membership was clearly not on the same level.

Despite the downside of being globally recognized.

Fame from notoriety, and a Star buff dependent on viewer count, were unique benefits only available to VIP members.

Though the details were not specified, it was clear why the Shadow Queen was so astonished by the VIP Membership.

‘Then, the next feature is…’

Given the promise shown by Spotlight, Seong Jihan wondered about Hideout.

Skimming the description for Hideout’s details revealed.

[Hideout]

– Conceals the player’s information under premium membership. It reduces attention from Constellations and makes it difficult for opposing teams to acquire the member’s information, prolonging the time to gain fame within BattleNet.

– The VIP member status enhances its effects.

– Players are removed from racial rankings and have a 100% chance to avoid bans. During the VIP membership, any mention of the player within the BattleNet community will be automatically deleted. Only admins can observe you.

‘Spotlight seems more appealing compared to this.’

Despite its upgrade, Hideout, aimed at concealing oneself, seemed less impressive, even with VIP enhancement.

While the guaranteed ban avoidance was profoundly effective, it didn’t specifically bolster Seong Jihan’s strength.

‘Choosing Hideout would ensure ranking first among humans…’

Since Seong Jihan had already dominated the World Tree Elves’ competitors.

Being free from bans would enable him to win every remaining game.

But Seong Jihan’s adversaries were not limited to the Space League species.

‘With Longinus dead, who knows what Constellation from Tuseong will come next. To free my sister from The Abyss Master, I must increase my strength without fail.’

If he had to choose between the two, it’d be Spotlight this time.

Seong Jihan then noticed the purple text added at the bottom of the item description window.

[Void Authentication]

– Received the Void Authentication from ‘The Owner of the Arena.’

– The capacity to accommodate the void significantly increases, allowing for one refusal against being bound by the void’s will.

– Access to the Space Arena is granted for free.

– The authentication effect lasts for 6 months, requiring re-certification by a Great Constellation to renew the VIP membership effects.

The Arena’s Master truly was a Great Constellation.

Realizing this from the last line, Seong Jihan inquired.

“So the 6-month authentication applies to the entire VIP membership effects?”

“That’s correct. Without re-certification, the VIP Membership will expire.”

“I see…”

“This time, I’m offering the VIP card merely as sponsorship. For re-certification, I’ll be asking for a nominal fee.”

The Arena’s Master pointed at half of his face, tapping it while hinting at what he wanted.

“You’re asking for the Void Processing Facility?”

“Yes. The VIP Membership Extension and the Void Processing Facility… it’s a fair trade. Next renewal, I will claim the Code Medium.”

Planning to obtain what he couldn’t recover this time through the VIP Membership.

Seong Jihan grinned, storing the VIP Membership in his inventory.

“I’ll consider that when the time comes.”

“I look forward to a wise decision. Then.”

Pop!

The Arena’s Master snapped his fingers, closing the portal connected to the Abyss.

And his body gradually became translucent.

“It’s been a lengthy inconvenience. I should take my leave.”

“Such inconveniences are always welcome.”

“Ah, one last piece of advice before I go. Prepare thoroughly before challenging the Abyss Master. The one who has bound the Witch of the Void possesses the strength of a high-level Constellation.”

“high-level, huh…”

“Make good use of the VIP Membership.”

Seong Jihan’s eyes widened as he read the message.

If he could gather as much data about the Abyss’s owner here and then analyze it in the training ground of the void, it would be beneficial.

Deciding to fight, Seong Jihan observed his opponent closely.

‘Those red eyes embedded in the giant’s body… I feel like I’ve seen them somewhere.’

The massive eyeballs wriggling on the giant’s body.

‘Indeed, no easy adversary.’

A foe perhaps too strong to face immediately upon becoming a Grandmaster.

Whoosh…

As time passed, the mist of the void grew denser.

‘But the extinction occurring after 24 hours wasn’t a phenomenon caused by the void…’

The phenomenon of the world distorting was distinctly different from the encroachment of the void.

Even before being thrown, the Phoenix Flag emitted a fierce white flame.

‘…This still isn’t enough.’

Even as Seong Jihan witnessed the power contained in Dragon Thunder, he instinctively felt it.

The opponent was even stronger than Longinus.

Swoosh.

Seong Jihan took out another weapon from his inventory.

-A stick?

-Did Seong Jihan have such a weapon?

-That doesn’t seem useful against that giant eyeball creature…

It was the Divine Spear, inherited from Longinus.

Seong Jihan’s supreme offensive measure, the ‘Code of Destruction’.

The Divine Spear allowed for striking at a distance through the spear, acting as a conduit for the Destruction code without requiring close combat.

For 10 seconds, the aura of destruction flickered, and upon contact with a target, it served as a medium to obliterate the foe.

However, considering the Abyss’s owner had nullified even Mount Tai Suppression, solely relying on it might get intercepted midway.

‘Therefore, clear a path with the Phoenix Flag, then insert the Code of Destruction.’

Whoosh!

The Phoenix Flag, spewing white flames, moved first, burning all the void’s mist blocking the path.

Yet, as it approached the owner of the Abyss, the firepower of the Phoenix Flag weakened rapidly.

And then,

Swoosh…

By the time the Phoenix Flag nearly reached the owner of the Abyss, most of the white flames emitted from the spear had already vanished.

Even the Constellation Longinus couldn’t withstand its power, yet for the owner of the Abyss, only a portion of his flesh was annulled.

He hoped continued attacks would eventually eradicate it but then,

The purple giant was regaining its former form.

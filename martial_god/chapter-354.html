Chapter 354

‘If Pythia hadn’t pinpointed it, I would have completely missed it.’

The life force felt under the North Sea was extremely faint.

Had Pythia not specified its location, it was subtle enough to go unnoticed.

However, once detected, the life force emanating from there began to intensify gradually.

“We need to look below.”

“Yes, I’ll apply a barrier.”

As Pythia moved her hand, a large protective barrier enveloped her and Seong Jihan.

Venturing beneath the sea.

‘The deeper we go, the stronger the life force feels.’

Seong Jihan surveyed his surroundings as he descended to the seabed.

And moving towards the area with the strongest life force, what he found was.

‘Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods.…’

A representation of the Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods, no larger than the palm of a hand.

Though it was the smallest rendition of the Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods Seong Jihan had encountered, its completeness was beyond anything he could dare replicate.

‘The degree of perfection is overwhelming just from looking at this formation.’

Merely observing the formation had Seong Jihan feeling overwhelmed.

What exactly was sealed within such a flawless sealing formation?

And even with such a powerful seal, how could life force still seep through it?

He quietly observed the symbol of the Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods before speaking to Pythia.

“You mentioned earlier about visiting the place where the Martial God and Dongbang Sak met, right?”

“That was mentioned, yes…”

“Is this really the place?”

“That’s….”

Swoosh.

Pythia scanned around.

The world beyond the barrier was in the middle of the seabed.

Fish were visible above them.

Could the Martial God and Dongbang Sak have met in such a place?

“Well, it seems unlikely their first meeting was here….”

“Still, seeing the Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods drawn on the seafloor, it suggests there’s definitely something significant here.”

“I mentioned earlier that the souls of the giants had regenerated, right?”

“Yes. You mentioned Dongbang Sak ventured around to seal them.”

“Yes. The souls of the giants scattered around the world… Perhaps this sealing formation is related to that?”

The Red tribe and the Great golems, who had fused with the World Tree and acquired the ability to regenerate even in soul form.

The intricate Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods drawn on the ocean floor possibly sealing them was a plausible theory.

However.

“The souls of giants… It seems there’s more than just that here.”

“Is that so?”

“This Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods is a perfect seal. If life force can escape through it, it implies there’s a potent essence of life inside. But, would the souls of giants, even partially merged with the World Tree, emit such precise life force?”

The power of life felt even above the sea, breaking through the Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods.

It seemed improbable for the souls of giants to exhibit such presence, even having merged with the World Tree.

“Yeah… Unless it was the World Tree itself.”

“…The World Tree?”

“The World Tree Alliance mentioned planting one on Earth. Where is this tree located?”

“That’s….”

Swoosh.

Pythia’s gaze inadvertently shifted to the Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods on the ground.

‘If not only the souls of giants but even the World Tree itself is within….’

Then it would explain the refined life force emanating despite the perfect sealing formation.

“We should take a closer look.”

“Be, be careful.”

Swoosh.

Seong Jihan knelt to inspect the formation more closely.

Then,

[The Nine Palaces and Eight Trigrams.]

As Dongbang Sak’s voice echoed, a new sealing formation began to form over the Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods.

At first glance similar to the Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods, yet entirely different, the Nine Palaces and Eight Trigrams was drawn.

[Open the formation.]

Upon opening the formation.

Whooooosh!

“Ah…!”

Seong Jihan and Pythia were sucked in without a chance to resist.

“Ah, be more careful, will you…!”

“I didn’t do anything. Just now.”

“True, we got sucked in before touching anything. But this place…”

Swoosh.

Pythia looked around.

A single massive tree extended high into the sky, with the ground around enveloped in greenery.

“Behind the tree… there’s a large lake. The interior of a sealing formation is unexpectedly peaceful.”

“Yes. But this tree…”

Thud. Thud.

Seong Jihan slowly walked towards the towering tree.

An exceptionally large tree.

Identifying the essence of this tree, brimming with life force, became apparent through.

[Your ‘Eternity’ stat increases by 1.]

A system message quickly shed light on the situation.

“Is this the World Tree planted on Earth?”

Just being close to the tree saw life force gravitating towards Seong Jihan.

His Eternity stat, which had been stationary at 2, increased by 1 simply by nearing the tree.

“Is this the World Tree? Hmm… If it is, I’m not feeling the World Tree’s unique vitality that strongly.”

Pythia tilted her head, tapping Sophia’s body lightly.

Holding a bamboo fishing rod over his shoulder, an old man with a white beard hanging long.

His face was familiar to the two of them.

“Dongbang Sak?”

“Dongbang Sak? Who’s that?”

“What do you mean? That’s your name.”

“What nonsense. I am Kang Sang.”

“Kang Sang?”

“Dongbang Sak’s original name. Grand Duke Kang Sang.”

Grand Duke Kang Sang.

Might as well increase it while he had the chance.

Had he sensed the Eternity within him as well?

‘Truly a monster.’

The Dongbang Sak before him,

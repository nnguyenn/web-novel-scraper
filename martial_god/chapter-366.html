“You… are you Gilgamesh?”

‘Can’t let someone like Gilgamesh operate in the open.’

‘Seeing them become puppets and still desiring sponsorship. Sigh.’

Help really does come from unexpected places.

“Let’s continue with the Constellation sponsorships.”

Could there be some disadvantage?

“Hold on.”

Chiik…

The Martial God was already incomparably powerful even now.

If he used the power contained in the Constellations’ Artifacts on top of that, there would be very few beings who could oppose him.

Even Gilgamesh, who had witnessed a fragment of that power, judged that he couldn’t possibly face him and proactively proposed cooperating with Seong Jihan.

That thing would ignite flames that would burn the entire Earth.

To make use of the power to turn back time, he ultimately had to collaborate with the Martial God.

In the end, to save humanity from the conflagration, he had offered the Martial God the carrot of infinite regression.

“…If humanity was saved because of that, I suppose even my birth is all thanks to you.”

“Haha. Saying your birth is thanks to me is too much. It’s thanks to your parents.”

“But because of that infinite regression, a ridiculous monster was born. The already powerful Martial God kept accumulating power as he returned to the past…”

“I see. But looking at you, it seems humanity… has survived for a long time. Isn’t that enough then?”

Since he had extended the lifespan of humanity on the brink of extinction anyway.

Is he saying he’s done his part?

“If that were the case, my future self wouldn’t have chosen you.”

“You mean there’s a countermeasure against the Martial God?”

“For now,”

Swish.

Ashoka pointed at Dongbang Sak, who was floating cross-legged.

“Release the seal on this world and restore Dongbang Sak’s memories.”

“His memories?”

“Yes. One of the reasons the Martial God tried to discard the human species was because of Dongbang Sak.”

“…Why?”

“The human species, with no set limits. Countless gifted individuals were born among them, but there was no martial artist with transcendent martial arts like him.”

“He captured all the Blood Tribe across the planet, sealed them here, and controlled the World Tree’s unrest. Thanks to Dongbang Sak, the Martial God’s plans through the Blood Tribe were thwarted for a long time. He is now just waiting for Dongbang Sak to die after reaching the end of his lifespan.”

Capturing the souls of the Blood Tribe wandering around the world, sealing them in the World Tree,

And guarding this place for 600 years to prevent them from running amok.

It was an absurd feat for a human to accomplish alone.

‘No matter how you look at it, Dongbang Sak is the one who fits the title of Martial God.’

Thinking that, Seong Jihan asked Ashoka,

“Dongbang Sak is still strong even in the future. But what does this have to do with the Martial God discarding humanity?”

“He has a compulsive dislike for uncontrollable situations. But the human species, with no set limits, gave birth to Dongbang Sak. To prevent the variable of a second Dongbang Sak appearing, he wants to reset the species.”

Because the variable called Dongbang Sak was born from humanity, he wanted to burn the Earth and go experiment somewhere else?

‘That Martial God bastard… has a narrow mind.’

At that moment,

Dongbang Sak, who had been sitting cross-legged, opened his eyes.

“Hearing such words from you makes me ashamed. Wasn’t the snake most afraid of you?”

“How could I possibly be his fear?”

“I merely used my strength. You, who repeatedly blocked the snake’s plans, were the one he was most wary of…”

As he said that, he looked at Seong Jihan.

“Did you say my future self drew out the sword of light from the Taiji?”

“That’s right.”

“Then that sword is not one tempered to destroy souls.”

“…Then what is it?”

“It’s compressing power to pass on to someone.”

Saying that, Dongbang Sak stroked his beard with a frown.

“You, make sure to show respect to my future self. Since it seems you’re the only one I can pass the taiji sword to.”

“I’ve learned so much that I’m being quite respectful in my own way. That’s why I’m even using honorifics, right? I immediately use casual speech with other disciples of the Martial God.”

“Oh my… Bragging, are you? You, don’t get too excited about stealing martial arts in front of me even when you return to the future. It makes my beard stand on end.”

“Hmm… I’ve already done that.”

“…And you’re still alive and well.”

Dongbang Sak, who had been glaring at Seong Jihan with disapproval, spoke to Ashoka.

“I’ll accept your proposal to become the snake’s servant. Well, looking at this fellow, it seems I’ve already accepted it.”

“You’ve made a wise decision.”

“Then…”

Swish.

“This is…”

Dalgi, that’s a story from the Kang Sang era.

Save the World and Help the People.

Saying that, Dongbang Sak handed Dalgi’s horn to Ashoka.

Receiving it, Ashoka let out a deep sigh.

“No…! What will you do if you use this dangerous power?”

Ashoka exclaimed in surprise and hurriedly tried to dissuade Seong Jihan.

“The void is a power that will collapse you from the inside. Just harboring it will devour you. This is a power that must never be handled!”

“No, Ashoka. We don’t necessarily have to see it that way. It looks like the sword will be completed soon in the Taiji…”

“Dongbang Sak! He is the one chosen to oppose the Martial God who has entered infinite regression. Can we let him perish like this?”

Chapter 414

The Martial God, who was certain that Seong Jihan would succumb to the Taiji this time, sharpened his gaze as he watched the battle conclude.

He had been prepared to activate the Golden Wheel at the cost of personal loss if Seong Jihan emerged victorious.

[The arm is firmly attached.]

“He has made a reckless choice. For a human to implant an administrator’s arm… He will soon be devoured.”

Ashoka spoke outwardly with calm, but his heart was uneasy.

Why choose such a path? Managing the Taiji could have been done without resorting to such extreme measures. Was it because of the key?

‘It was for his sister… no doubt about it.’

Bending to familial affection at the expense of the greater good… However, if he is soon devoured by the arm, it amounts to the same betrayal.

‘…This world is done for.’

Ashoka tentatively concluded that Seong Jihan, in his attempt to save his sister, would be devoured by the administrator’s arm, leading to humanity’s annihilation and a return to the past.

The end result would be the same even if the process had differed this time.

‘No, the end might differ, since Martial God would hastily activate the Golden Wheel.’

Martial God had resolved to turn back time even if it meant relinquishing power should Seong Jihan win.

At least, the accumulation of power would slow down, making it a slight blessing in disguise.

‘Do I need to find another candidate?’

Seong Jihan had been the best individual capable of creating variables in this endlessly looping world.

However, from the next cycle, he would be instantly dealt with by Martial God, necessitating the need for a new candidate.

Ashoka pondered who could serve that role but came up with no immediate names.

The fact that they had progressed this far in the current cycle was itself a series of fortuitous events.

However,

[If I acquire that arm… my long-cherished desire will be fulfilled.]

The party intending to revert time seemed to have different thoughts.

“…Will you not activate the Golden Wheel?”

Having lost hope in Seong Jihan after the arm’s implantation, Ashoka asked, but,

[Wait for now.]

Given Martial God’s historically cautious nature, turning the Golden Wheel seemed like the prudent choice.

Considering how Seong Jihan had acted unpredictably thus far, and now having gained both the arm and the power of the void, further variables were likely.

However,

[If I acquire that, I won’t need to regress anymore.]

The administrator’s hand – its value shook Martial God’s resolve.

[Time that has already become countless.]

Originally, strength and authority had steadily grown, but recently, they had stagnated. The more power they garnered, the more apparent this stagnation became.

Martial God, though known for his patience, found himself stirred by the notion of achieving his goal and ending the repetitive cycle.

[If I can secure that, I can accomplish my goal and end this endless life.]

Ending the cycle resonated deeply with Martial God, shaking his usual stance.

Had the arm remained in Seong Jihan’s inventory, thoughts of forfeiting the time to secure it would not have arisen.

Flash!

Martial God kept his gaze fixated on Seong Jihan’s arm.

[If he is overtaken by the red energy, I will have my chance.]

Absorbing that arm would end everything.

The endless, tiresome cycle would conclude, and he could redesign the world as a permanent administrator.

[Until I obtain that arm, I will hold off.]

In an uncharacteristically rash decision, Martial God resolved not to turn the Golden Wheel just yet.

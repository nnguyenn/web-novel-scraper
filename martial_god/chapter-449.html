Chapter 449

The Sun King’s laboratory.

“You actually succeeded this time?”

“Yes, the Dragon Lord’s head has been successfully transplanted into the 17777th!”

When Martial God was created as the Sun King’s 17777th son, he could understand language from the moment he gained self-awareness.

‘This place…’

Twitch.

He opened his eyes.

And the first thing he saw through the glass of the test tube was a white-haired man with a haggard face.

“Vital signs are stable. There is no sign of rejection from the Dragon Lord’s head. This body has perfectly accepted the transplanted head!”

The white-haired man was making a fuss and pointing at him.

And then—

Screeech…

A massive giant with grotesque features and glowing red eyes on its face pushed the white-haired man aside and peered down.

The giant’s lips curled into a wide smile.

“Well endured, my son.”

His voice was gentle and kind.

To the newly born child, it sounded just like a father’s love.

However, the words that followed were in stark contrast to his tone.

“Your body will carry the name of the Sun King and shine even more brilliantly. I will gladly enter your body today, so be grateful for your birth.”

“Ah! For the Master to choose his son so soon… This is unprecedented! 17777th, know that this is an honor!”

The white-haired man echoed the giant’s words with zeal.

Then he abruptly stopped and flashed a sinister smile.

“Well, not that it matters since he doesn’t understand a word of this anyway.”

“Would he stay still if he did? The original host of this body put up quite a resistance to survive.”

“It is unfortunate considering the time and effort we spent on the 14722nd son…”

“No need to lament, for once I claim that body, there will be no more need for transitions.”

Their conversation didn’t make sense to the newly born child.

‘Does this mean I’m going to die?’

Perhaps it was due to having the Dragon Lord’s head transplanted onto him, but the 17777th son could vaguely understand their words.

‘…’

But as he had just been born, understanding their words didn’t mean he had the ability to do anything about it.

He could only stay still in the test tube and watch them.

“Then, let’s start with the branding.”

However,

As soon as the Sun King finished speaking,

Sizzle…!

A scorching pain erupted from the center of his body, and letters began to engrave themselves onto his chest.

The first thing that was engraved was the number 17777.

“Hmm, this body is still too small for me to enter. Administer the growth stimulant.”

“Yes, Master.”

No sooner had he spoken,

Bubbling…

The liquid inside the test tube began to boil.

Then,

Crack. Crack!

Every part of the 17777th son’s body started to swell and burst.

Beyond the glass of the test tube,

A giant head with a snake’s head was trying to grow its eyes but couldn’t.

“The body is not developing eyes… are the red eyes clashing with the Dragon Lord’s head?”

“Yes, it seems so.”

“We need to allow more time for growth. I’ll give it a week to manifest the eyes.”

“Understood!”

The Sun King turned his head, satisfied as he looked at the 17777th son.

With the father who intended to immediately take over his body leaving,

“Hey, Dragon head. Shall we grow a bit larger?”

The white-haired man manipulated something with a smile on his face.

Then,

Bubbling…

The liquid inside the test tube began to boil again.

* * *

Four days later.

“Oh dear, the eyes almost formed, then it molted again. This dragon head is really troublesome.”

The white-haired man frowned as he looked at the 17777th son.

The master, the Sun King, had ordered the growth stimulant to be administered until the eyes formed.

The body was now large, and the Dragon Lord’s head had grown significantly, making the test tube seem cramped, but still, the crucial red eyes had not appeared.

“If this continues, I will face terrible punishment again… Ah, what to do?”

While the man was contemplating what to do with the dragon head,

‘The manifestation of the eyes can’t be delayed any further.’

Inside the test tube, the 17777th son realized that he had reached his limit.

The entire body had already been branded thoroughly with the Sun King’s marking.

Once the red eyes were manifested, the body would be immediately taken over by him.

‘Would it have been better if I didn’t understand?’

He didn’t know how the Dragon Lord’s head, which the Sun King coveted so much, worked on him.

Within his four days of existence, the 17777th son had developed the capacity to understand language as well as assess his current situation.

Had he been without consciousness, the Sun King could have claimed the body without protest.

‘…I will live. I must live somehow.’

He couldn’t die right after being born.

Driven by a sheer will to live, the 17777th son focused on every aspect of the laboratory’s operations.

And then,

He discovered a possibility.

“Tsk, removing another shed skin again.”

Whoosh…

A large shed skin was sucked into a vortex at the top of the test tube.

The skin that had been shed from the giant body with a snake head was ejected onto the floor of the lab.

“Let’s see…”

The white-haired man squatted down to examine it and,

“Nothing of value, as expected. To the incinerator with it.”

Snap!

With a snap of his fingers, the shed skin vanished in a flash.

‘The shed skin takes an exit route…’

While the Sun King’s body, of immense importance, could never be allowed to leave, the shed skin was a different story.

Initially, the white-haired man had examined each shed skin for research value.

However, upon finding nothing of value, he had begun sending them all to the incinerator after a brief inspection.

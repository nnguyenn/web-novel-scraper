Chapter 459

Tap. Tap.

“I have pondered for a long time.”

Siddhartha approached Seong Jihan as he spoke.

“On what must be done to free humanity from the enemy.”

“Did you think about this while the Martial God repeated his infinite regressions?”

“Yes.”

The regressions of the Martial God.

During the era of Ashoka, Siddhartha had undertaken it.

While regressing, he accumulated power and,

simultaneously thought of ways to eliminate humanity’s enemies.

‘Truly a superhuman.’

As Seong Jihan was marveling at him.

Swish.

Siddhartha brought his hand to the floating red eye.

“And, yet again, I must apologize. I tested you with this.”

“What… this was a test too?”

“Yes. I am already a dead man. This red administrator emerged from within you.”

So that red eye that came out of Siddhartha knew the circumstances regarding Seong Jihan because it was actually inside his body.

As Siddhartha touched the eye,

[You who realized for the sixth time. You have kept your word. What you did was not turning away, but true defiance…]

The eye lost its color and disappeared.

At the same time, the red energy was reabsorbed into Seong Jihan’s body.

He could now roughly understand the situation.

‘The red administrator showed me an illusion. He wanted to see if I would succumb to it.’

The ecstasy when becoming the red administrator was indeed significant.

Especially when he crushed Tuseong underhandedly.

Ah, no wonder anyone would desire to become a permanent administrator.

Of course, his thrill quickly diminished when he returned to Earth and transformed Yoon Seah and Seong Jiah into beings of the red lineage.

‘Without witnessing that, I might have briefly considered it.’

Recalling the grotesque Yoon Seah who turned into a monster with eyes, Seong Jihan furrowed his brow.

“How many tests do I have to go through?”

“I apologize. It was important to me.”

“What if I had failed this test? Your true form has already self-destructed with the Martial God. The infinite regressions would have come to an end. You’d have no body left.”

Swoosh.

At Seong Jihan’s words, Siddhartha quietly looked at him.

“But you do have a body, don’t you?”

“…Are you saying it’s mine?”

“Yes. If you succumbed to the red’s temptation, I intended to use your body in your stead and then return it.”

With those words, Siddhartha smiled.

“But seeing how firm your will is, it seems I wouldn’t need to do that.”

He had even prepared to hijack his body in case he fell for the administrator’s temptation.

When he told Seong Jihan to burn him with the sacred flame, he had everything planned.

Seong Jihan’s expression showed he was overwhelmed.

‘No wonder the Martial God was so wary of this person.’

The reason he turned into a snake head and tried so desperately to eliminate him became clear.

“Now that I’ve passed all the tests, tell me what solution you’ve come up with.”

“It’s quite simple. I created an ability to counter the red.”

“An ability… is it a stat?”

At Seong Jihan’s question, Siddhartha nodded.

“As you can see, the realized ones can escape from the red. But realization… it’s not something that can be bestowed upon all of humanity. Humans are beings who find it hard enough just to sustain life. Few pursue self-realization.”

“Well, of course.”

If every dog and cow could realize, Red would have been eradicated long ago.

“So, I isolated part of that realization. Solely to create a means of fighting the red.”

“…Is that even possible?”

“I had plenty of time.”

In this regard, infinite regressions served a useful purpose.

As Seong Jihan nodded, recalling what the Green Administrator once said, he inquired.

“The Green Administrator once told me that becoming an administrator would enable me to erase the red inherent in humanity. Wouldn’t it be better to just delete it?”

“If that method works, that would be excellent. But the Green Administrator is not a reliable entity, is he?”

“True.”

You couldn’t fully trust Yggdrasil’s words.

Having multiple methods to eliminate the red definitely wasn’t a bad thing.

As Seong Jihan consented,

“Then I shall pass the ability to you.”

Hummm…

A golden light began to seep out of Siddhartha’s body.

And soon,

A message appeared before Seong Jihan’s eyes.

[You have obtained the FFF grade ability, ‘Azure’.]

[When the Azure stat increases by 1, the red’s factor disappears.]

[When the red’s factor disappears from humanity, the evolution limit of the human race is reset based on the “Mid-Grade Species”.]

Azure.

It seemed it was meant to contrast with the red.

The administrator’s hand was supposed to control him and switch to a new heart.

“The seal is lifted, but the Red doesn’t fully return to 700.”

“That is because.”

[Seong Jihan, what have you done? You’ve freed yourself from my control.]

“Is the new body Yoon Seah?”

[Correct. She seems suitable for a new settlement.]

The administrator’s hand openly declared its intention to switch to Yoon Seah.

Seong Jihan grimaced.

This creature had to be dealt with here.

“Now that the administrator’s hand opposes you, all the abilities you gained through it have transferred over.”

“So, of the 700 points, 600 belong to that thing?”

“That’s correct.”

At those words, Seong Jihan recalled when he had transplanted the administrator’s hand.

Back then, just having the hand increased his stats by 300.

‘Considering that, the 600 being there makes sense…’

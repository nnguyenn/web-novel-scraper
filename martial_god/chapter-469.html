2 hours later.

‘The red factor reappearing isn’t just limited to Seah.’

Seong Jihan confirmed that all the red factors he had erased with his power had regenerated.

‘I clearly uprooted the red from their bodies, but it eerily reappeared…’

The power of Stat Azure was definitely a counter to Red.

But even after the fire had evolved once,

As if embers were flying in from somewhere, the red factor kept appearing inside humans.

‘Especially Seah, unlike others, the red is getting stronger and stronger.’

Was it because the Red Administrator was considering Yoon Seah as the next target?

The red she possessed earlier was less than 1, but the red that reappeared after being erased by Blue,

Was now reaching almost 2 in terms of stats.

And once it reached this point,

“Uh… Uncle. This time, I feel like something has disappeared.”

Yoon Seah, who had been unable to feel the red until now,

Expressed her puzzlement, saying it felt like something was gone.

‘There’s a limit to directly erasing it like this.’

Seong Jihan’s eyes sunk deep.

By manifesting Blue directly like this, it was impossible to remove the red factor from humanity.

‘I need to open the Administrator System.’

Although he felt various changes after becoming the Blue Administrator,

The biggest among them was the Administrator System that only Administrators could access.

Seong Jihan, who had not been able to enter the system even once because he was experimenting with Red,

Thought that this system was the key to fundamentally solving the red factor.

“Administrator System.”

As Seong Jihan said that,

[The Administrator System is opening.]

[The Administrator accessing the system is the temporary Administrator ‘Blue Administrator’.]

[Only a few unrestricted areas are accessible.]

The Administrator System emphasized that he was a temporary Administrator and opened a part of the system.

And what was revealed was,

‘…Why is it so complicated?’

Endlessly listed management items.

The species and planets Seong Jihan had seen so far,

The distorted parts of the system, etc.

The targets he could touch as an Administrator were endless.

However,

‘They’re all marked with an X.’

Perhaps because he was a temporary Administrator,

Planets, species,

Even the correction of system distortions were all marked as inaccessible.

“Then what can I access?”

As Seong Jihan made a remark after seeing everything marked with an X,

[Only accessible management items are displayed.]

The Administrator System erased all the numerous items,

And displayed only three items.

‘Humanity, me, and Stat Azure.’

No matter how limited the authority of a temporary Administrator was, this was a bit too much.

As Seong Jihan quietly stared at the three items,

[Administrators exist to develop the BattleNet system.]

[Administrators gain more authority by resolving distortion phenomena in the BattleNet system.]

[The current temporary Administrator is an error that goes against the BattleNet system itself because he became an Administrator by defeating an impossible feat.]

[Find your problems and resolve the distortion phenomena.]

The Administrator System explained why only three items appeared.

‘I’m an error in the BattleNet system?’

Seong Jihan saw this and at first wondered what it meant, but,

‘A Constellation Candidate defeating a Great Constellation could be seen as a bug from the system’s perspective.’

He soon understood why he was pointed out as a problem.

Becoming an Administrator from a Constellation Candidate,

Was no different from reaching the summit by taking a helicopter on a hiking trail where you had to climb stairs.

‘As a commemoration of becoming a temporary Administrator, it’s telling me to fix the bug that is me before touching anything else.’

The Administrator System was telling him to fix the bug that was himself first to expand his authority.

‘Well, let’s see what kind of problem I am.’

Seong Jihan smirked and decided to take a look at what kind of bug he was.

“Open the management item ‘Seong Jihan’.”

Whiiiing…

As soon as Seong Jihan’s words fell, the screen changed.

On the blue background of the system screen,

Seong Jihan’s status window was displayed.

‘What is this, it’s just my status window?’

The status window listing the stats of Martial Soul, Void, Eternity, Red, and Blue.

The values of each ability were not much different from before he became an Administrator.

What bug was he supposed to find here?

As Seong Jihan looked at his abilities with puzzled eyes,

‘Oh… Something is changing.’

Behind each stat, a new evaluation value called ‘Distortion’ began to appear.

The stats where the distortion value appeared first were Void and Blue.

‘Both are 0.’

Looking at the stats marked as Void (Distortion – 0), Blue (Distortion – 0), Seong Jihan nodded.

Blue was currently an FFF-grade stat whose abilities had not been identified.

Void had outstanding output, but it was not an ability that distorted the system in the sense that it led the user to death.

And,

‘Eternity is 30, and Red is 50.’

Eternity and Red.

These stats, which were no different from an Administrator’s abilities,

Had a low distortion score, perhaps because Eternity was incomplete.

Then the only one left was Martial Soul.

‘Martial Soul isn’t an ability that greatly distorts the system…’

Why is the evaluation taking so long?

Around the time Seong Jihan was puzzled,

‘It’s out.’

With Martial Soul (Distortion – 52),

Martial Soul was unexpectedly measured to have the highest distortion value.

No, this shakes the system more than the abilities of Administrators?

While Seong Jihan was looking at this with bewilderment,

‘Hmm…’

The system distortion value of Martial Soul,

Was suddenly being upgraded in real-time from 52 to 54.

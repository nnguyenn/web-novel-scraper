Standing in a secluded spot, Gilgamesh looked beyond the sea, a smile forming at his lips.

Gilgamesh’s smile deepened as he mentioned the small restriction.

[…Fine. Let’s go.]

[From here, you must go to the bottom of the sea.]

Once he absorbed life energy from the World Tree, which was sealed there, he could halt this aging.

Standing on the seabed, he noticed the seal.

‘This is the seal formation.’

Most of the complex patterns had disappeared due to Seong Jihan’s interference.

The shape itself was simple enough to seem easy to dismantle.

‘But the power contained within it is immensely strong.’

Clicking his tongue, Gilgamesh scrutinized the seal formation underwater.

He had come all this way, but this was a problem.

‘The World Tree is surely inside the formation…’

Despite the strong seal, life energy was faintly leaking from the red dot.

[It seems like it will take decades, no, even centuries to regain your former power with that…]

“I am someone who has lived since the beginning of time. I can wait that long.”

There stood Seong Jihan, calmly crossing his arms.

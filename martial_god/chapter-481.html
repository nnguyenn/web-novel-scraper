Then he should at least examine that.

‘Seeing the light sword, it seems clear that Dongbang Sak cut my head.’

Nobody else could create such a sword.

Confirming the suspect who severed his head, Seong Jihan looked at the actual Nine Palaces and Eight Trigrams formation.

‘Looking at this again, I can be sure. The Nine Palaces and Eight Trigrams formation shown by the divine eye was weakened.’

And Seong Jihan inferred that he was the one who weakened the formation.

If Dongbang Sak had tampered with the formation, it wouldn’t be merely weakened but completely dismantled.

The final seal of the Nine Palaces and Eight Trigrams formation.

Before, it seemed impossible to approach.

But now, as a Blue Administrator, it didn’t seem that difficult.

Unlike the ambiguous scene shown by the divine eye, he felt capable of dissolving it entirely.

Was there a reason for his future self to leave it intact?

‘…I have to go in and check.’

Contemplating it from outside wouldn’t yield any answers.

Seong Jihan decided to enter the Nine Palaces and Eight Trigrams formation to verify.

As he placed his foot on the central red dot,

Ssss…

His body was sucked into the Nine Palaces and Eight Trigrams formation.

‘Could it be a strong opponent made me unable to dismantle the formation?’

Expecting such a scenario, he entered the Nine Palaces and Eight Trigrams formation.

‘…Why is it so peaceful?’

Contrary to his expectations, the inside of the final seal was calm.

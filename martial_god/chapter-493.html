As he spoke, his hand moved toward the injured area behind the beard—his neck.

“It’s also the first time I almost had my neck cut.”

“Is that so? That’s unfortunate.”

Seong Jihan swallowed his disappointment.

Though his Taiji Sword had severed Dongbang Sak’s beard and injured his neck, it did not deliver a killing blow.

‘I took the shot perfectly when his formation collapsed…’

Enduring his limbs being severed dozens of times over 5 minutes had been solely to seize this opportunity.

He waited for Dongbang Sak to fully deploy his formation and attempt to suppress him, only to counterattack using his Administrative Authority.

‘The plan worked flawlessly, but he evaded the critical strike.’

Watching Dongbang Sak’s beard quickly regenerate to its original length, Seong Jihan furrowed his brow.

‘He’s incredibly quick to react.’

“But how exactly did you manage it? My power suddenly diminished.”

“And this sensation… it feels reminiscent of the restraint I experienced under the Martial God.”

“Let’s fight with this.”

Chapter 497

The Martial God’s star, Tuseong.

Quiet to the point where only the Martial God remained,

It began to shake wildly after Dongbang Sak transformed the Martial Soul into Devil’s Intrusion.

Boom! Boom!

Some of the constellation relics floating in the sky burst explosively.

Hisssss…

Some began to lose their light and started falling to the ground.

[The power… it’s uncontrollable…]

Martial Soul.

A power selected as the “Main Ability” for the species ‘humankind’ due to its exceptional traits.

Yet, it wasn’t enough to fully contain Dongbang Sak’s talent.

A long time ago, during the early stages of infinite regression,

He tried to absorb Dongbang Sak’s martial talents through Martial Soul, only to unmistakably confirm the gap in their abilities.

At one point, he tried to match it,

But as time passed, he realized the sheer difference in dimensions between their abilities, contenting himself with just plundering Dongbang Sak’s martial skills through Martial Soul.

[…It shouldn’t have been this way…]

Whoosh!

The Martial God, from the sky, looked at the constellation relics moving as they pleased and felt powerless.

About 30% of the constellation relics he had raised.

But as time went on, the number of relics escaping his control continued to increase.

Relics moving and bursting on their own.

Why these things would react so violently to Devil’s Intrusion wasn’t something he understood, as he only utilized the Martial Soul but never sought to comprehend it.

The Martial God could only helplessly watch the constellation relics fall and explode.

When over half of the relics were beyond his control,

[…Now, I can control it.]

His Martial Soul,

‘So 1000 is the threshold for Devil’s Intrusion to manifest…’

The Martial God judged the current situation and quickly moved the constellation relics.

Attempting to absorb their power,

As soon as his Devil’s Intrusion broke past 1000 again,

Boom! Boom!

The constellation relics began to explode autonomously once more.

[Argh…! Dongbang Sak…!]

Absorbing power from the constellation relics only for it to turn into a boomerang.

What kind of absurdity was this?

At this rate, how could he utilize the constellation relics he had amassed thus far?

‘I’ve spent so long accumulating this power…!’

Seeing the practically useless constellation relics, the Martial God fell into despair.

Dongbang Sak.

From the moment he drew the Taiji Sword, he had a bad feeling,

But he believed that as long as he was in Tuseong, he wouldn’t be harmed.

Since Dongbang Sak was heavily restricted after Ashoka’s betrayal,

Even if he regained his senses, he thought he could just kill him and be done with it.

However,

Transforming Martial Soul into Devil’s Intrusion to bind me like this…

‘In contrast, the Blue Administrator has undoubtedly grown stronger…’

In the recent battle, despite countless defeats,

His power grew even more.

A situation diametrically opposed to his, constrained by the constellation relics.

Moreover, the strike that severed Dongbang Sak’s arm,

It would have been challenging to deal with even with the constellation relics.

Yes.

Even if used, it wasn’t easy.

If the amplification of power through the relics was blocked…

‘I won’t be able to seize the Red World Tree, much less—I’ll die at his hands…’

Without the strength from the constellation relics, could he truly defeat Seong Jihan with his current power alone?

Even amidst his fury, the Martial God coolly assessed this and reached a quick conclusion.

[I cannot win.]

An ability that even restricted Dongbang Sak,

He could also use Ashoka’s Golden Wheel.

Seeing Seong Jihan scrape every possible resource to fight, the Martial God assumed,

The loss was recognized from the start.

So now, what needed to be done was…

[…Escape.]

Flight and hiding.

The solution conceived was inconsistent with the typical image of a “Martial God.”

However, from the perspective of the snake, prioritizing survival,

This was the most rational approach.

If only, dropping Tuseong and fleeing alone felt wasteful knowing what had been accumulated over time.

‘Moving Tuseong alongside…’

Without amplifying strength through the constellation relics, there was no quick way to move this star.

As the Martial God contemplated his next move,

Vrrrr…

It resembled Dongbang Sak’s Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods.

[Dongbang Sak… what on earth are you trying now…!]

As he watched, raising his power in tension,

Crack!

The pattern of the Myriad Ghost Sealing Gods shattered,

Thunk…

Within it, a red eye fell.

[That I should be unfettered like this…]

Roll…

The red eye rolled on the ground.

[It seems you’re facing some trouble.]

Shine!

It radiated an intense light.

The red light spread in all directions, revealing a BattleTube screen.

The video played at high speed, and then,

[Indeed…]

Beep!

It turned off in less than a minute.

[See, I told you this would happen. Suffering by your own sword.]

[Red Administrator…]

[Are you more inclined to cooperate now?]

The Martial God silently stared at the red eye.

The Red Administrator.

The most dangerous adversary to cooperate with.

However,

‘…Bound by Devil’s Intrusion, I have no other means.’

No, there was one way.

Abandoning everything.

Destroying all the constellation relics and escaping with only the current power,

While avoiding the Blue Administrator’s sight, fleeing far away.

But living like that,

It was not truly living.

[Let’s hear what you have to say.]

[Huhu… you’ll like it.]

In the end, the Martial God chose to deal with a dangerous adversary.

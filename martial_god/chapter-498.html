But seeing the red’s distortion fluctuating, it seemed he didn’t have much time left.

Now it should be okay to receive treatment.

The Taiji Sword being stuck there was a problem, but.

He examined the sealing formation closely and found a gap to enter.

It would be better to try healing without using the Golden Wheel if possible.

‘But if it doesn’t seem to work inside, I’ll have to get out immediately.’

The Golden Wheel returns to the state of 24 hours ago.

If he dawdled inside and too much time passed, it would be neither here nor there.

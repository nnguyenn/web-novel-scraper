Chapter 502

“U-Uncle, what are you doing!?”

As soon as Seong Jihan stabbed his face, Yoon Seah immediately appeared in the training room.

“Why are you hurting your face that just recovered…!”

She was about to absorb the void again, but,

Seong Jihan lightly waved her off.

“I need the void for training. Let’s heal later.”

“Oh, I see…”

“Yeah, I’ll ask for your help when I’m done training.”

At Seong Jihan’s words, she looked at his face with anxious eyes.

“You understand, right?”

“…Yes.”

She nodded with effort.

“So don’t worry about me and focus on your own work. Why do you rush in as soon as I stab my face?”

“This is more important than anything else.”

“I can control this much. Don’t you have Space League games or national team matches to be busy with?”

The weight that the world’s #1 ranked player had to bear wasn’t so light.

She shouldn’t have the leisure to watch Seong Jihan’s every move like this.

To his question, Yoon Seah answered nonchalantly.

“Oh, there is a Space League match going on right now.”

“Going on, you say?”

Seong Jihan looked at Yoon Seah with a surprised face.

Wait.

How could she come here during a Space League match?

“Yeah, but I just didn’t go. The opponents are Orcs anyway.”

“Hey!”

“Anyway, your void is more important than one of those matches, right?”

Yoon Seah said this while carefully observing Seong Jihan’s broken face.

“What’s wrong with humanity coming in second? You raised us to the top tier anyway. We’re just finding our place. Isn’t it a problem if we lose without me?”

“…Still, you could absorb the void after going to the match. You’re not even participating?”

“What if the void suddenly overflows and your cracks widen? We can win the next match.”

Seong Jihan looked at Yoon Seah with a puzzled expression.

His niece, who used to be a model student,

Was now neglecting her duties as the world’s #1 ranked player to absorb the void from his face.

Why was she suddenly acting like this?

‘Could it be because of the hat?’

Could the void side be influencing her somehow to make her the definite owner of the Arena?

Seong Jihan asked her with a serious expression.

“…Haven’t you felt anything strange since becoming a Void Apostle?”

“Huh? What do you mean?”

“You didn’t used to be like this.”

“What do you mean I wasn’t like this? I’ve always worried about you a lot, haven’t I?”

Yoon Seah tilted her head at Seong Jihan’s words.

“But… you’re right. Something has changed. Before, I could only worry about what to do, but now I can really help, right?”

“Yeah, helping is good, but do it in moderation. People need to do their own work too.”

Not going to the representative team’s match and watching in real-time for void leakage,

This was going too far.

As Seong Jihan smiled bitterly, Yoon Seah hung her head.

“…I thought you were invincible, Uncle. Until I saw you being buried in the void and disappearing.”

“Well, I ended up coming back to life anyway.”

“But when I saw you disappearing… It was shocking.”

Her eyes trembled slightly.

“All this I’m enjoying… you made it all possible, right? I just received it. Mom went missing. Dad went to Japan… I thought I’d never see them both again. But you brought them all back.”

“That was just what I had to do.”

“This is what I have to do too. I need to repay the kindness I’ve received so far. Especially.”

Yoon Seah’s eyes turned to Seong Jihan’s face again.

“I’ll keep focusing on this until your face returns to normal.”

“I need to stay in this state until I finish training anyway.”

“Then I’ll keep caring for you until then.”

“…So you’re going to keep monitoring in real-time?”

“Real-time monitoring! It’s care. Care.”

Seong Jihan thought as he listened to Yoon Seah.

‘This, she’s not going to change her mind easily.’

Was seeing him die at Dongbang Sak’s hands that shocking?

Yoon Seah’s attitude about dedicating herself to caring for Seong Jihan was more stubborn than he thought.

Even if he said she didn’t need to care for him, she seemed determined to keep watching in real-time.

In this situation, simply saying no wouldn’t work.

Seong Jihan tried to persuade her slowly.

“Alright, Seah. I appreciate your concern, but right now I need to train with the void, right? You should do your own work too.”

“But it shouldn’t overflow, right? Don’t I need to keep watching?”

“I’ll call you if I need you. I’ll ask you to come and absorb the void through your battletube, so you can come then, right?”

“Oh, the battletube works here too…”

“If it doesn’t, you can just go out and turn on your battletube.”

“I’ll call you through the battletube if it’s urgent, so do your own work.”

At Seong Jihan’s words, Yoon Seah chewed her lips.

“…Okay. Then I’ll come when you call me.”

She nodded with effort and retreated.

After she left, Seong Jihan thought.

‘Once I unite Blue and Martial Soul, I should smash that hat first.’

While he was genuinely grateful for Yoon Seah’s concern,

It wasn’t right for her to prioritize this over her own life.

At this rate, his niece would be severely criticized by the public.

He couldn’t bear to see that.

‘As for my shattered face, I can probably fix it somehow by squeezing Gilgamesh.’

Thinking this, Seong Jihan replayed the battle with Dongbang Sak.

His face was much more stern than before.

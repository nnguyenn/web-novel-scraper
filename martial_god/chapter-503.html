Dongbang Sak talked as if it was almost done, but that was by his standards.

Seong Jihan thought it would take about 5 years with his talent.

He thought it could easily take even longer than that.

‘This estimate came after absorbing the Devil’s Intrusion ability… Before that, I didn’t even know how long it would take.’

Until he absorbed abilities through Heterogeneous Martial Resolve, he didn’t even know how long it would take.

He got a rough sense as his martial talent increased while absorbing the remnants of Martial Soul.

With diligent training, 5 years.

With the void training room, it would take 1 year of actual time, but…

‘This is the old way.’

There was no need to take the long way when he could find a shortcut.

However,

‘There’s nothing to learn from other human players.’

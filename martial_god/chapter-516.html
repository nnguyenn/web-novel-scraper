“We might have set a viewing record, but now we’ve caught the Red Administrator’s eyes,”

“Wouldn’t it be better to shut down the channel rather than let the Red Administrator use it?”

-Aw, I exchanged a billion yesterday, such a loss…

-Lost money and health…

-Wait, I used GP to pay back tuition fees to my parents; convert it to dollars already!

As GP also vanished with BattleNet’s disconnection, panic ensued since so much was lost in such a brief moment.

Amid this, a red eye unexpectedly appeared beneath the Shadow Queen’s channel.

[Humanity, do you wish to regain what you’ve lost?]

Spoken in a gentle tone by the Red Administrator, the camera then zoomed in on Yoon Seah approaching.

[For that, she is needed.]

“Why are you zooming in on me?”

Next to her, a starfish suddenly appeared.

[Red Administrator, what are you doing right now?]

[Isn’t it obvious, Messenger of the Void.]

[I have already reported this incident to the Black Administrator. He will not ignore this.]

[Heh…]

The Red Administrator only laughed at the mention of the Black Administrator intervening soon.

[Earth and humanity no longer belong to BattleNet’s world. The Void cannot intervene immediately.]

[But if you intervened to cause this problem, it’s different. We have the authority to intervene during emergencies.]

[Is that so? Why haven’t you come then?]

The starfish, the Messenger, kept silent for a moment before continuing.

[Communication has been severed… Could it be that the White Administrator is also involved in this?]

[Who knows, I’m not sure.]

[…That explains it. There’s a reason behind what you’ve been doing.]

Swoosh…

As the starfish enlarged, blocking Yoon Seah’s path, the Red Administrator spoke.

[There’s no need to block her. I have no intention of touching her directly unless she takes off the hat herself.]

“Why would I take this off?”

Yoon Seah, touching her head.

Ziiiiing…

Suddenly, many screens appeared before her, showing people in turmoil after suddenly becoming the lowest tier, collapsing, vomiting blood.

-Emergency lines aren’t responding, now I see why…

-Ah, isn’t that Oliver from America? Why did he kill himself?

-He wasn’t committing suicide, he was flying with magic and fell.

-Wow, such a big accident…

Among various emergency outbursts, many were caused by players overestimating their abilities.

In these screens, the Red Administrator then said,

[Seeing people like this, do you intend to remain the owner of the arena?]

“You did this.”

[You can help them.]

“On what grounds?”

Yoon Seah’s firm response was met with laughter from the Red Administrator.

[Heh… I see. So this is your answer. Humanity, one person’s sacrifice would have restored prosperity to six billion.]

-Who would listen to his words…

-Didn’t the Red Administrator cause this mess?

-Maybe cooperating was better given the circumstances.

[Still, I will offer you an opportunity.]

And then,

Eyes popped out from the screen.

-Wow, spooky, the eyes actually popped out!

-Ugh, so gross.

-I almost touched it

[This building… was also home to the Blue Administrator.]

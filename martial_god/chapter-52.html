Chapter 52

Unable to contain her anger, Kim Heesu arrived at the Gift Department and made contact with the security guard.

“On the 22nd, there was an incident in front of the Gift Department. Do you have the CCTV footage? Give it to me now.”

“I can’t do that.”

“I’m requesting it legitimately from the victim’s perspective.”

“Even so, unless an official complaint is filed, we can’t release it…”

“Well, do you also think Korea Daily is that easy to deal with?”

Kim Heesu’s eyes were filled with determination, and the security guard flinched.

He had been on duty on the night of the 22nd, and he had seen Kim Heesu being mistreated by Seong Jihan with his own eyes.

The hatred and anger he had witnessed in Kim Heesu at that time were genuine.

‘I shouldn’t mess with her for no reason…’

He wasn’t an exceptional player like Seong Jihan, and he had no influential background.

He was just an ordinary citizen with no support to speak of.

He feared that if he pushed the rules too far, he might become the target of her anger, which was already simmering.

“Ah, I understand.”

Unable to resist Kim Heesu’s insistence, the security guard finally agreed to replay the CCTV footage from that day.

The scene was of her cousin, who had a broken leg and had all his eyebrows plucked.

Kim Heesu herself was shouting loudly, and the scene where her hair and eyebrows were also plucked was vividly displayed.

It brought back painful memories of that day.

“Ugh…!”

Kim Heesu gritted her teeth and tried to hold back the involuntary shivers running through her body as she continued to watch the footage.

She saw herself hurriedly escaping with her cousin, and not long after, the scene where Seong Jihan and Yoon Seah were having a conversation came up.

She narrowed her eyes.

‘Yoon Seah…?’

Yoon Seah, who usually maintained a bright and cheerful demeanor with a sunny smile, had a deeply saddened expression on her face in the CCTV footage.

If her Gift had worked well, she would never have made such an expression.

“What did these people talk about here?”

“You’ve already shown me the CCTV footage. Is there anything to hide?”

Kim Heesu spoke in a cold tone, and eventually, the security guard reluctantly recounted what he had heard that day.

“She said she is ruined and needed to focus on studying for the college entrance exam…”

“What? She’s ruined?”

“Yes.”

“Hahahaha! Unbelievable. So, Yoon Seah was just showing off…!”

Kim Heesu smiled widely at the news.

How should she reveal this information? Through a newspaper article in Korea Daily? Or should she confront Yoon Seah in person?

‘Both, I guess!’

Kim Heesu had various ideas on how to ruin Yoon Seah even more.

She returned to the classroom with light steps and looked around.

“Hey, where’s Yoon Seah?”

“She submitted her withdrawal papers and went home.”

“What?! Already?”

“It’s just the process of withdrawing, it’ll be quick.”

After receiving their Gift, many students chose to withdraw from the academy without completing the curriculum, so the withdrawal process at the academy was simple.

‘Darn, she ran away.’

Kim Heesu wanted to mock her in front of everyone, but it was a pity.

“But why were you looking for Yoon Seah?”

When her friend asked with a puzzled expression, Kim Heesu considered telling them about what she had just learned but decided to keep quiet.

‘I can’t reveal it here when she’s not here.’

If she talked to these gossipmongers, the news might break through elsewhere before Korea Daily could publish it.

‘Korea Daily should be the first one to publish this…’

How could she ruin Yoon Seah more provocatively?

Countless ideas came to her mind.

Kim Heesu smiled with satisfaction at the thought.

After having her hair and eyebrows plucked, it was the first time she smiled with joy.

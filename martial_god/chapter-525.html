Chapter 525

‘This is… what I bestowed upon humanity.’

During his time as an administrator,

To erase the Red factors present in all of humanity, Seong Jihan used administrator mode to universally grant Stat Blue.

This use of administrator authority was possible because Blue’s grade was FFF.

Since then, Blue had existed as 1 in every human’s status window.

‘Is that what the World Tree Alliance was after?’

He had wondered why they bothered making humanity a colony, but now it seems they coveted Stat Blue.

As Seong Jihan’s gaze deepened,

Zing…

A green message window appeared before his eyes.

[Player ‘Kim Jihun’ obtains Stat ‘Blue’.]

[Stat ‘Blue’ increases by 1.]

[Class changes to ‘Blue Knight’.]

‘Blue Knight… Is this the official class name for male Half-Elves?’

Male Half-Elves were known to have a unique class.

The ‘Blue Knight’ class seemed to be forcibly assigned rather than chosen.

As ‘Kim Jihun’s’ class changed, additional messages appeared.

[Gain 1 Stat ‘Eternity’.]

[Blue and Eternity combine, upgrading the grade of ‘Blue’.]

[Body undergoes changes.]

‘Blue and Eternity are fusing…’

As Seong Jihan widened his eyes at this message,

Thud. Thud thud…

His flesh twisted and then,

Pop pop pop!

It began to burst all at once.

“…!”

Blood vessels and flesh exploded instantly.

The space inside the tree was quickly covered in blood and flesh.

Kim Jihun’s body was soon left with only the head, and then,

Pop…!

Even that burst immediately.

Only the brain remained.

As his physical body completely disappeared,

Flash…!

A green light rose inside the tree space,

And his body slowly began to reconstruct.

Into the male Half-Elf form that people so desired.

And inside that body,

Blue, merged with Eternity, moved actively, regenerating the body.

‘Blue combined with Eternity… This is quite different.’

Although Seong Jihan had raised Stat Blue to SS rank,

Blue merged with Eternity felt completely different from the Blue he had developed so far.

This could be quite informative in its own way.

As Seong Jihan tried to thoroughly analyze the Blue merged with Eternity,

[Stat Blue increases by 9.]

[Stat ‘Blue’ grade rises to E.]

Messages about increasing stats kept appearing.

‘Ah, right. I should adjust it. Soon.’

At this rate, it seemed poised to absorb all the Blue.

This would far surpass the current record of 25 percent.

‘It seems my hair color has already changed quite a bit… I should restrain myself now.’

The Blue merged with Eternity was so intriguing that he kept accepting it,

Seong Jihan ended up absorbing more stats than originally planned.

He decided to restrain himself now and refused the entry of more Blue.

In fact, what he had gained so far was enough for researching this stat.

Then,

Hiss…

Several times, blue smoke rose inside the tree space, trying to push in more Stat Blue, but,

‘If I take any more, my hair will be 100 percent dyed.’

As Seong Jihan continued to completely block this,

Whoosh…

Finally, the smoke gradually subsided.

And then,

The space inside the tree slowly opened.

Seong Jihan stepped towards the bright gap.

Then,

“Wow…”

“A, a Half-Elf… Another one!!”

“After three months…!”

Everyone in the waiting room stood up at once, looking at ‘Kim Jihun’ with surprised eyes.

And,

They were soon even more surprised by Kim Jihun’s hair.

“That, that hair color…”

“Could it be a new record?!”

“It seems to be over 20 percent…”

Part of Kim Jihun’s hair had turned sky blue.

The proportion clearly looked over 1/4.

“…Player Kim Jihun. We will proceed with a detailed examination.”

The High Elf Priestess, who had shown little interest no matter how many female Half-Elves appeared,

Hurriedly approached Kim Jihun and examined him.

“Scan.”

Zing…

With the High Elf Priestess’s magic, a green beam of light quickly scanned Kim Jihun’s body.

“…Scan.”

After seeing the results, the High Elf Priestess tilted her head and used scan once more.

Then,

“Hmm.”

Comparing Kim Jihun’s hair with the scan results,

“Unlike what we see, the test result is 20 percent… I see.”

She announced the result with a puzzled expression.

That would have drawn too much attention.

‘Well, even this appearance will make daily life quite inconvenient.’

Swish.

Seong Jihan touched his ears.

Ears that protruded like an Elf’s.

Touching his face, he found that ‘Kim Jihun’s’ features, which he had made somewhat blunt,

Had changed to be quite similar to Seong Jihan’s original appearance.

‘I’ll need to look in a mirror later to see how I’ve changed.’

As Seong Jihan was thinking this,

“…”

The High Elf Priestess compared the scan results with Kim Jihun’s face, then,

“Player Kim Jihun. After the tutorial ends, come directly to the Colonial Government in Namsan. A detailed examination seems necessary.”

She told him to come directly to the Colonial Government.

“Understood.”

He was planning to go there anyway, so this worked out well.

As Seong Jihan nodded readily, the High Elf Priestess looked around and clapped once.

“Then, we will conclude the aptitude test for Zone 1.”

With 5 out of 30 passing.

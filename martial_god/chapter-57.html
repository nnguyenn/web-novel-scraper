She’d tied them up, and was curious about how he’d killed them?

“I’ll tell you if you answer my question.”

[“Agreed.”]

Seong Jihan then revealed, “I pre-severed the blood vessels of their hearts.”

[“Hearts…”]

“Yes. I just connected them with the Force of my Qi.”

In case he successfully provoked the Shadow Queen and she attacked, Jihan had prepared a fail-safe mechanism.

By severing the connected blood vessels of the four players he’d kept alive, he ensured their immediate death if Qi was unavailable, thus securing his top position.

[“Just that led to their deaths?”]

The tone of the voice grew even colder.

[“No matter how irregular, to think they’re the lowest of beings.”]

[“It’s been decided. The main body will not support you.”]

The Black Sun said in an arrogant manner but Seong Jihan just chuckled.

‘Has it been decided that the species known as ‘human’ is useless?’

A Constellation’s support would certainly have its advantages, but it would also come with a price.

And he wasn’t desperate.

With the end of the tutorial, many Constellations would rush to offer their support.

There was no need to rush and regret later.

His current aim was different.

Wandering Martial God.

He needed information from the same constellation regarding the strange behavior of the Wandering Martial God, who was acting completely different from his last life.

“That’s enough. Now it’s your turn to answer my question.”

The jungle of the Valley map, despite its name, resembled a typical forest. The paths within the jungle were well-trodden and even, akin to a park trail. However, the presence of massive monsters scattered throughout made it impossible for Silver players, whose maximum level was only 50, to pass through.

As Seong Jihan entered the jungle, he was immediately greeted by a massive two-headed wolf. The wolf, standing at a height of 3 meters, charged at Seong Jihan upon sight. This was the Twin-Headed Wolf, considered one of the weakest jungle monsters. Even so, its level was 65, meaning a party of three would typically collaborate to defeat it. However…

A grey smoke emerged from the wolf’s carcass, which was then absorbed into Seong Jihan.

[The energy of the Twin-Headed Wolf has been absorbed.]

[All stats are enhanced by 5%.]

The blessings from the jungle monsters varied depending on their level, but a minimum of a 5% stat enhancement was guaranteed. If one hunted jungle monsters quickly, they could stack buffs faster than by killing 50 minions.

[You’ve achieved the ‘Silver League Jungler (1)’ feat.]

[You’ve earned 1,000 achievement points.]

‘1,000… Not bad.’ The ‘Silver League’ prefix in the achievement indicated the high difficulty of a Silver player defeating the monster alone.

‘Let’s clear our jungle first.’

Nameless Divine Arts Footwork Technique: Flashing Sky Thunder Steps

“Kiii!”

Suddenly, a monster would leap out, only to be swiftly defeated by Seong Jihan’s Phoenix Stance. Then, Seong Jihan would move again at lightning speed, clearing the entire northwest jungle area within minutes.

With some time remaining before the monsters respawned, Seong Jihan thought, ‘Now, time for some kills.’ Before heading to another jungle area, securing a first kill seemed like a good plan. While the enemy players at the top lane were hiding near their tower, the mid and bottom lanes were still fiercely battling.

‘A swift ambush is the most effective support.’

[You’ve earned 100 achievement points.]

‘Could it be…?’

Seong Jihan immediately thought of Ariel, whom he had summoned earlier.

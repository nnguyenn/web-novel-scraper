Chapter 74

“Shall we now discuss the Akashic Page? Ariel.”

As Seong Jihan returned home, he mumbled.

From the elbow area, a black shadow spread, revealing Ariel.

[The Akashic Page… It’s equipment for those with supporting gifts.]

“Equipment, you say?”

[Yes. The Akashic Page amplifies the abilities of the supporting gifts.]

Ariel reached over to Seong Jihan’s desk.

From her touch, a black energy spread, and she grabbed the Space 23 model, a gift she received after filming the advertisement.

[Although it doesn’t function permanently… It’s a remarkable amplifier. It’s not just for folding and unfolding such devices.]

Saying that, Ariel shook her head as if she couldn’t comprehend.

[The description of the Akashic Page’s item should mention it amplifies supporting gifts. I don’t understand why it’s used for such things.]

“Hmm. There was no such description.”

The item description of the Akashic Page on Earth was simple.

[Akashic Page (SS-grade)]

[Contacts a higher-dimensional information entity.]

In the past, since no one knew how to use it, the Akashic Page became a meme, getting called ‘the most useless SS-grade item’ on the internet.

When Seong Jihan showed this to Ariel, she blinked in surprise.

[No appraisal? Isn’t there a player with an appraisal gift?]

“On Earth, SS-grades can’t be appraised.”

Among the players with the appraisal gift, no one could appraise SS-grades.

A minimum A-grade was required for the appraisal gift.

[Hmm… How many are there in the lowest-ranked species?]

“About 7 billion.”

[7 billion? And not a single one among them can appraise an SS-grade? Truly… they are the lowest-ranked species!]

Ariel nodded in agreement, further solidifying her belief in the equation human = lowest-ranked species.

“Even if they had known about it, it’s likely it would’ve been used this way. In this world, supporting gifts are rarely used. If one gets that gift, they simply live like a regular person.”

But to forsake technological innovation and invest in the Akashic Page for such people?

It was unthinkable for humans on Earth.

[I can’t understand. There must be Dungeon Portals in this world, too. Don’t you need players with the ‘Exploration’ Gift to eliminate them?]

“What…? With ‘Exploration’, Dungeon Portals can be closed?”

Seong Jihan widened his eyes at Ariel’s sudden revelation.

Dungeon Portals.

During the current tutorial phase, they only appeared in the bottom 10% of Leagues.

But after the tutorial, that percentage steadily increased.

Every nation, except the final ten, got covered with Dungeon Portals and perished.

‘The problem with Dungeon Portals was that their complete eradication was impossible.’

There were multiple attempts to close Dungeon Portals.

They tried bombing them, and players attempted to destroy them with magic.

However, Dungeon Portals wouldn’t disappear from such external attacks.

Humanity chose to enter the portals instead.

‘But even entering one, fully dismantling the dungeon was impossible.’

Even if everything inside a dungeon portal was annihilated using tactical nukes,

The portal would become stronger in a day and regenerate.

The types of monsters coming out of these altered portals kept changing.

If ghosts or similar entities emerged, that region had to be abandoned.

Although attacks from Supporters or Mages did work, there were too many of them to fend off with those means alone.

And now, to hear those Dungeons could be closed with the Exploration Gift…

[You didn’t know? To find a dungeon core, exploration abilities are essential.]

“I had no idea… Supporting Gifts seems quite useful.”

[Right. That’s why in our world, they are highly respected. To look down on supporting Gifts, what an… intriguing world.]

Ariel shook her head as if she couldn’t fathom it.

Then she asked, [By the way, what’s the highest grade of the supporting Gift for the lowest-ranked species?]

“The highest I’ve seen is A. Our guild master is of that grade.”

[A is the highest? Then there must be many lower grades like C, D.]

“That’s right.”

[Those grades are useless in supporting gifts… It’s only natural they’re treated like ordinary people. What about in combat?]

“There are several SSS grades.”

[I see…]

“Do you have any suspicions or ideas?”

[Your kind is not suited for battle. For one, they have too many vital points.]

[Even like this, a Shadow Elf doesn’t die.]

[Even if only the head remains, they can survive.]

“…Impressive.”

[That’s the survival ability of a mid-tier race. Humans, on the other hand, are so easy to kill. In terms of combat abilities, they would probably rank the lowest among the species participating in the BattleNet.]

Seong Jihan recalled the races he had faced as enemies in the Space League.

‘…Indeed, judging by species alone, humans were the weakest.’

Humans had a much inferior basic physical ability compared to other species.

Moreover, they had many vital points, and they could die from excessive bleeding from just a few injuries.

Meanwhile, other species could reattach severed limbs, and some could regenerate like starfish.

Especially the ‘Elfs’ had vitality that even cockroaches would envy, making it unfair to be in the same league balance-wise.

[So such fragile humans participated in BattleNet… To balance things out, the system must have granted them exceptional gifts. There are several who received SSS-grade gifts.]

“Don’t other species get that?”

[No. When our star first participated in the Space League, the highest gift grade for combat roles was A.]

“But they receive better supporting gifts?”

[Yes. The average is B-grade, with the highest reaching SSS-grade.]

Seong Jihan nodded in agreement.

‘She is more useful for providing information than for fighting.’

Ariel, the Shadow of the Sun, was beneficial as a summoned entity and as a shadow sword, but her knowledge of the other world was even more valuable at the moment.

She can locate a dungeon core with her exploration skills…

‘I need to disclose this information later.’

It would be best to immediately disclose information about dismantling Dungeon Portals for Earth’s sake.

However, Seong Jihan decided pragmatically that if it became known that Ariel provided this information, his life might be endangered.

He’s currently treated as the world’s top prospect, but becoming a target of powerful national intelligence organizations knowing Ariel has knowledge of the otherworldly League would be dangerous.

Regardless of how strong Seong Jihan is, he’s currently only in the silver league.

Facing a nation-level threat would be challenging.

‘I need to release the information when I can handle the repercussions.’

It’s still the tutorial period, so the Dungeon Portals aren’t immediately threatening the world.

‘But I need to secure the Akashic pages.’

Amplifying of supporting gifts.

It’s something Seong Jihan, who still has a lot of growing to do, definitely needed.

If he could enhance Lee Hayeon’s ‘Nurturing’ Gift, he would also benefit.

‘I need to find a way to obtain it.’

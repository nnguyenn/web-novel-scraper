Chapter 77

Inside the guild master’s office after Barren left.

Lee Hayeon looked towards the door and said, “It seems Barren is quite conscious of you, Owner.”

“It seems so. He should focus on improving his skills.”

In his previous life, Barren ranked higher than Seong Jihan, hence he didn’t act like this.

Now, he visibly exhibited signs of an inferiority complex.

“Well, he was once considered one of the world’s top prospects, right?”

“To that guy, an SSS-grade Gift is like pearls before swine.”

As Seong Jihan harshly criticized Barren’s abilities,

Lee Hayeon sneakily checked outside the guild master’s office door.

‘… He left, right?’

“When will the other players arrive?”

“They should be here by tomorrow or the day after. Oh, speaking of which, where should we use the nurturing fee we received from the player loan?”

When they take care of 5 players, they receive a total nurturing fee of 25 million GP a month, equivalent to 250 billion won.

‘With that much, I could even buy an A-grade relic.’

To absorb divine power and gain force, he needs to collect A-grade Otherworldly Relics.

The price of an A-grade relic starts from 2.5 billion won. He wasn’t been able to purchase them due to financial constraints, but now,

‘I can surely buy now.’

Now that he had the money, it was essential to purchase and absorb even a little more of the attributes.

“Hmm… According to my share, I’ll allocate 20 billion. Is that okay?”

“No, why consider shares? It’s your guild, use all of it if you want.”

“I can’t do that. We need funds for guild operations.”

“Operating expenses… We barely have any significant expenses now.”

“No. You said you wanted to grow the guild, so please use it as you see fit.”

After all, this money is almost entirely earned thanks to Lee Hayeon’s Gift.

Seong Jihan gave 20% of it to Lee Hayeon to use as she pleased.

“Oh my, you really… You didn’t have to do this…”

Unaware of this, Lee Hayeon looked at Seong Jihan with eyes filled with emotion.

Giving her 5 billion to spend.

That’s too generous!

“Anyway, now that we have the money, shall I try and multiply it?”

“By multiplying… You mean…”

“I have a sure bet, just like last time.”

There’s no need to spend all 20 billion on A-grade relics.

Especially when there’s a sure bet.

“Betting… really?!”

At those words, Lee Hayeon’s eyes sparkled.

She had been so busy with guild duties that her gambler’s passion was suppressed.

But with Seong Jihan mentioning a sure thing, her hands itched once again.

‘Ah… I decided not to gamble… But maybe I can just recover my principal this time?’

The biggest reason gambling addicts can’t escape the trap:

Thinking of ‘just recovering their principal’.

Lee Hayeon had already become a splendid gambler.

“Yes. This time, don’t use your head unnecessarily. Just follow my bet exactly. Understand?”

“Of course! From now on, I’ll only trust and follow you, Ownerr!”

At Seong Jihan’s advice, Lee Hayeon immediately nodded.

She had already lost money twice by acting impulsively.

This time, Lee Hayeon firmly resolved to trust and follow only Seong Jihan.

‘She seems committed now.’

She won’t make a mistake this time.

Seong Jihan began in earnest,

“You know that the East Asia League’s second half of the season is until December, right?”

“Of course, I do.”

“Who do you think will win?”

“Umm… China, perhaps? Japan has become stronger with the addition of the Sword King. But the original first place has its strength, right?”

“No. Japan will win with a clean sweep in the second half of the season.”

“A clean sweep?”

Upon Seong Jihan’s assertion, Lee Hayeon’s eyes widened.

It was understandable that Japan would rank first.

The power of the Sword King was that immense.

However, a clean sweep was a different story.

They would be facing China, which has consistently been the top in the East Asian League, four times.

Would Japan win all those matches?

‘China has two players with SSS-grade gifts…’

Lee Hayeon couldn’t believe it.

However, everything he had said up to now had been correct.

So, this time, she decided to trust and follow.

“Also,”

“Also?”

“Our country will have a total loss.”

“A total loss?”

“The absence of the Sword King was a significant loss. Who can our country beat now?”

With the absence of the Sword King, South Korea couldn’t secure even a single win in this year’s second half of the regional league matches.

But still… Not a single win until December?

Granted, China and Japan are strong, but not even against Eastern Russia or Taiwan?

‘Russia has concentrated its players in the western part, so the eastern forces should be weak…’

Russia, with its vast territory, participated in the regional league divided into two regions.

Western Russia, which included Europe and was more significant, had most of the skilled players, while the East had slightly less skilled players.

Therefore, when ranking in the East Asian League, China was the dominant top team.

Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, and Russia were all competing closely.

But not even beating Russia or Taiwan once, even with the absence of the Sword King?

“That’s… hard to believe.”

“Well, I don’t know if I become a national representative. I won’t be able to participate in the second half of the season due to my level. South Korea will lose everything.”

Seong Jihan affirmed.

“So, bet on Japan’s clean sweep victory and Korea’s total loss.”

“So you’re suggesting to bet separately for each match?”

“Yes. As time goes on, Japan’s victories and Korea’s defeats will become commonplace. The odds won’t be that high, but with compound interest, it grows.”

Lee Hayeon nodded.

Even if the odds are just 1.2 times, getting it right 10 times will multiply the money over 6 times.

‘I must bet according to the Owner’s words this time!’ This time, don’t ‘think’.

‘Just blindly follow! ‘

Lee Hayeon made this promise to herself several times.

Chapter 86

At a café located on the fourth floor of the building next to Sword Palace.

The building where the café is situated also houses the Korean branch office of the Neo Self-Defense Force.

In fact, this place had been previously designed by the Neo Self-Defense Force to entice former Sword King Yoon Sejin.

The café, which had been maintained since its contract period was still valid, has now reopened after a long time, to firmly capture Seong Jihan.

“Goddess… aren’t we rushing things too much?”

Inside a private room in the café, Takeda spoke, wiping his sweat.

In his hand was a piece of paper the size of A4, shining golden.

‘I can’t believe the Akashic Page has a feature to strengthen the supporting gift…’

It was surprising that the Akashic Page, which was worth billions and used for the technological advancement of large companies, had such a feature.

Unless one was as powerful as the Goddess, it made more sense to invest billions in technological advancement rather than enhancing support gifts.

Still, Takeda felt anxious, as if he’d discovered a hidden secret.

“It can’t be helped. Seong Jihan… it seems my Enchantment is almost wearing off.”

“Even Sword King couldn’t resist your Enchantment, Goddess! I can’t believe it.”

“Being an avatar means my powers are weaker. Even using the Akashic Page, it didn’t take effect properly.”

“But didn’t you say it was successful last time?”

“No, it failed. He didn’t contact me for two days. I had to send the first text.”

Joo Eunji gritted her teeth.

To Takeda, not hearing back for just two days might not be alarming, but for Joo Eunji, it was an absolutely unacceptable situation.

Such an incident had never occurred since the Enchantment took effect.

‘And his reply to my message seemed insincere.’

[Yes, is the time good for you? Shall we meet today?]

Too dry a tone for a message from a man in his twenties under Enchantment. If the Enchantment had worked properly, he wouldn’t have replied in such a manner.

‘I need to make my move this time.’

Even if it costs a fortune, she plans to use several Akashic Pages to ensure the Enchantment works.

Then she’ll quickly take him to Japan.

“Akari.”

As Joo Eunji subtly shifted her gaze behind her and called out a name, a woman dressed in full ninja attire sprang out of her shadow.

Takeda’s eyes widened.

‘Even Lady Akari came.’

Kato Akari.

Among the players of Japan’s Diamond League, she was a female player who transitioned to the special class of ‘Assassin’.

While she wasn’t selected for the national team due to her lower level within the Diamond League, her unique stealthiness as an assassin gave her many real-world assignments.

Ito Shizuru had noticed Kato Akari’s potential as a ninja long ago and used her power to make Akari her confidant.

Since then, Akari has been working as Ito Shizuru’s right hand, handling covert operations for her.

“If you’re here, the will of the main body must be significant.”

“Yes. The main body in Tokyo has entrusted me with escorting the Akashic Pages. Furthermore, she told me to kidnap Seong Jihan in case things go awry.”

“Kidnapping won’t be easy.”

“He’s at most a Silver Rank player. A ship is also ready at Incheon Harbor.”

Akari looked at the Akashic Page she pulled out.

“Personally, I think it’d be better to kidnap him with my abilities rather than using that item.”

“No, if Seong Jihan resists too fiercely during a clash, there’s a risk our plan could be exposed even before we get to Japan. It’s better to finish things discreetly.”

“Understood. I’ll be prepared in case things go south.”

Joo Eunji nodded.

If they failed even after using several Akashic Pages, they’d have no other choice.

‘Even if the Encantment doesn’t work fully, I can suppress his resistance.’

Once they get him to Japan, it’s over.

He won’t be able to resist the main body’s allure.

The sound of the café door opening was heard, and Joo Eunji signaled to Takeda and Akari.

Takeda quickly hid behind the private room’s wall, and Akari slipped back into Shizuru’s shadow.

“Oh my~ Owner. You’re here?”

And Joo Eunji, with a different tone from earlier, greeted Seong Jihan.

“You’ve reserved a nice place.”

Seong Jihan calmly walked in and sat down as soon as he pulled the chair.

Right away, Joo Eunji tapped the floor twice with her heel.

Tapping the floor twice was a signal she had pre-arranged with Takeda.

‘Believe in the Goddess…!’

Just as Takeda was about to emerge from where he was hiding,

Buzz!

The smartphone Joo Eunji had placed on the table vibrated slightly.

“Huh…?”

On the smartphone was a message,

[“Seong Jihan has started a live broadcast on BattleTube!”]

* * * * *

On his way to the cafe Joo Eunji had told him about, Seong Jihan was deep in thought.

“Continuing to pretend to be enchanted might be too hard, since it’s not my major.”

He could pretend to be enchanted in front of Joo Eunji, but it was impossible to keep it up long-term, even just by text.

Instead of failing to act properly, wouldn’t it be better to get rid of the avatar sooner?

If he gives away that he isn’t really enchanted, the hands of Ito Shizuru could reach out to those around him, including Seah.

“But getting rid of the avatar is also problematic. They’ll find another way to infiltrate…”

Lost in his thoughts, Seong Jihan entered a nearby building.

“Oh, it’s Seong Jihan…!”

“What’s happening here?”

People inside started murmuring upon seeing Seong Jihan.

“Wow… He looks even better in person!”

“Mr. Seong Jihan! Can I get an autograph?”

“Um… Can I take a photo with you?”

Some fans even approached him, asking for autographs and photos.

‘People recognize me a lot. Do I need to start covering my face now?’

Having always been at home, Seong Jihan had never truly felt his offline popularity.

But, smiling, he responded kindly,

“Yes, it’s fine.”

For the first time in this life, Seong Jihan, who had tirelessly done fan service during his America First days, was doing it again.

“Jihan! I love watching BattleTube! My parents and I always wait for your 12am live streams!”

“Thank you.”

“Oh, I’m so curious when you’ll hit 1 million subscribers. Can you show your status window just to me?”

“Haha, I can’t do that. Just wait a bit longer. It’ll happen soon.”

“Wow, you look even more amazing in person…”

“Hmm, did I not look as good during the broadcast?”

As Seong Jihan grinned and asked, the female fan blushed.

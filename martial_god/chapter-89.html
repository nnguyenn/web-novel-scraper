If cut by the enemy’s blade, the cut would be imbued with malicious energy, and the Dark Soul Vortex from the Shadow Sword Eclipse would quickly lose its strength.

[If the sword hadn’t properly merged with the arm, you could’ve lost your body. Humans, being the lowest- ranked species, aren’t they sensitive to physical loss?]

“Regeneration is the key. We have many good supporters in our country.”

Seong Jihan spoke nonchalantly.

In his previous life too, as a warrior, hadn’t he lost countless body parts?

Complaining about losing an arm now seemed trivial considering the brutal battlefields he had faced since his past life.

“But…”

Seong Jihan pulled the Sword from the ground with his right hand and looked up at the sky.

Akari neither fled nor attacked. She was just floating aimlessly.

“She hasn’t left yet?”

His eyes gleamed menacingly, like a predator stalking its prey.

“Let’s resume the Diamond hunt.”

Nameless Divine Arts, Footwork Technique – Flashing Sky Thunder Steps

‘How can there be such a person…’

Just a while ago, she almost died.

Her ninja outfit being SS- class equipment, and being able to use all of Diamond abilities, she barely withstood that attack.

Had she made even a slight mistake, her entire body would’ve burned in pain until she died.

‘The Golden Rule will be activated again… in 1 minute.’

She still had strength left.

She could face Seong Jihan.

Objectively, if a battle ensued, she would likely have the upper hand.

However.

‘…Can I suppress him in less than a minute?’

She was uncertain.

And upon confronting the deadly intent in Seong Jihan’s eyes.

[…]

She was terrified.

If she couldn’t suppress him within that minute, once the Tutorial’s Golden Rule reactivates, she wouldn’t be able to overcome Seong Jihan.

No, it wasn’t about gaining an upper hand… she would die.

Akari trembled.

She had died countless times in BattleNet, but that was just a game.

The concept of death in real life was a completely different matter.

She never thought she would die, so she believed she could overcome the fear of death.

Facing Seong Jihan’s genuine murderous intent, she realized her thinking had been delusional.

‘I…’

Whiz!

Seong Jihan’s sword, which had approached without her noticing, grazed her face.

With the power of the unlocked Diamond, she could easily dodge and counter.

However, Akari could only manage to dodge the sword.

She couldn’t think of countering.

‘I… can’t win.’

The feeling of defeat was already deep in her heart.

However, the reason Akari was still there was due to the order given by her master, Ito Shizuru, even at the cost of his clone.

Then.

[Akari. It’s time. Withdraw from the battlefield.]

In her ear, Ito Shizuru’s voice echoed.

It was like the voice of a savior giving her a reason, especially since she couldn’t run away.

[Understood. I’m sorry… Goddess.]

Akari turned around as if she had been waiting.

And for the remaining minute, she used all her strength to flee desperately.

Whooosh!

With a gust, Akari disappeared in an instant.

Her escape at full power was almost at the speed of light.

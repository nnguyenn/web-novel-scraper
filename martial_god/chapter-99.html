Chapter 99

5 minutes ago.

When Seong Jihan faced off against Count Naseed, he thought,

‘This… is easier than I expected?’

Perhaps it was due to the enhancement bonus from the canyon map and the buff from Sophia’s Trinity.

From the moment he confronted Count Naseed, Seong Jihan realized one thing.

Ah, this.

I can win this easily.

‘I didn’t need to gather party members after all.’

Using the Nameless Divine Arts, he could subdue the opponent right now.

However, the reason Seong Jihan prolonged the battle for 5 minutes was due to the Shadow Sword stat.

[Master.]

[The power of Count Naseed’s darkness is tremendous. How about cutting a bit more?]

Ariel, in the sword Eclipse form, suggested to Seong Jihan with a brighter voice than usual.

And even before she finished speaking,

[Shadow Sword stat increases by 1.]

His stats went up immediately.

‘Nice, huh?’

It’s worth stalling like this.

[If you cut the snake’s scale, you can absorb even more shadow power.]

Following Ariel’s suggestion, Seong Jihan began to cut off all of Count Naseed’s scales.

In the past 5 minutes, over 70% of the giant snake’s scales were cut.

[In your words… it’s amazing.]

The Shadow Sword stat increased by a whopping 4.

Since entering this game, his stat had risen by 5 in total.

With the Shadow Sword’s power becoming stronger, Seong Jihan could control the size of the sword even more freely.

[It seems we’ve absorbed most of it now.]

“Really? Time to finish him then.”

Just when Seong Jihan, who had acquired 5 Shadow Sword points, was about to happily strike down Count Naseed,

A huge wall of flames suddenly appeared in the valley to the east and north.

‘Over there. It’s the area Barren is responsible for.’

There was no need to keep things in check like this.

Why ignite such a blaze?

Seong Jihan had a hunch about what he was up to.

‘In my previous life, he hated it when someone stole his spotlight.’

But back then, he held the firm position of the world’s No. 1, the guild master of the America First Guild.

He was more laid-back than he is now.

The Barren of that time would have at least waited 10 minutes.

But the current him was incredibly impatient.

Whooosh!

Watching the Fire Wave heading towards Count Naseed, Seong Jihan clicked his tongue.

Tsk!

Does he really think that will finish him off?

Even concentrating mana to pierce a single point would be insufficient, yet he’s deploying such wide-area magic.

Although it seemed unlikely that a Fire Wave would deliver the final blow,

‘I can’t let this slide.’

Starting petty fights like that.

That…

‘I need to set him straight.’

Thud! Thud!

As Seong Jihan quickly distanced himself from Count Naseed, he flew towards the western cliffside where his party members were.

“What…what’s this? Why are you here?”

Thud!

As Seong Jihan landed on the cliffside and approached, Barren seemed to flinch, a stark contrast to his previous confidence.

Sophia spoke with a tone of disbelief, “If you were going to do that, why did you use my magic…”

Regaining his composure, Barren puffed out his chest and replied, “Ahem! Seong Jihan! A warrior should be marking the monster at the frontline. Why did you come here?”

“You’re not following my instructions, are you?”

“I only came to help because it seemed like the damage wasn’t getting through!”

“Oh, really? Without a word?”

“I had to act quickly! If I had been there, we would’ve dealt proper damage!”

Swooosh!

With a faint smile, Seong Jihan spread out his palm. All the party members behind Barren began to levitate, moving backward.

“Then, show me.”

“What?”

“I’ll just watch from behind. You do it.”

Saying so, Seong Jihan took position behind Barren, as if guarding the other party members.

“I’ll control the symbols. Ariel.”

[Should I move to the cliffside?]

“Yes. That fool, balcon, can’t use magic simultaneously in a crisis. Head over and block the symbols from the east and north.”

[TL/N: Balcon – means ‘showy but useless’. I forgot to add this note in Chapter 52.]

[Ah, understood.]

The shadow sword Eclipse sank into the ground and, turning into a long shadow, moved towards the cliffside.

“Balcon? Who’re you calling a balcon…?!”

Barren flared up at that comment, but Seong Jihan continued, ignoring him.

“Masied will keep controlling the symbols from the south.”

“Got it, Boss.”

[TL/N: Seong Jihan’s title as Owner has been changed to Boss for accuracy.]

While Masied, who was stationed at the southern cliff, continued his task, Seong Jihan said, “Seah, join Sophia and attack the markings from the west.”

“Okay. Just do as usual?”

“Ah… Sorry, Jihan. For not stopping him.”

“What for? He can handle it, can’t he? I’ll guard both of you.”

“…”

Barren was rendered speechless.

“Alright. Positions are set.”

Having rearranged the party, Seong Jihan gave Barren a sly smile.

“Do it your way.”
